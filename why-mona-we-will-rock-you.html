PEMBAHASAN

Change these sentences into active voice

=> Ubahlah kalimat berikut menjadi kalimat aktif

Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense

➡️ Passive (+) S + be (was/were) + V3 + By S

➡️ Active (+) S + V2 + O

Maka berikut:

1. The song " we Will rock you" was popularized by queen

=> queen popularized the song “we will rock you”

2. the police were called just after midnight by someone

=> Someone called the police just after midnight
