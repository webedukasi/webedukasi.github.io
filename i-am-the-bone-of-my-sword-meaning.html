Answer:

The meaning of idioms 'down on my knees' is 'to beg'. Such idiom is used to express the emphasis when saying a request. Here is the possible example.

'Please, sweetie, I am down on my knees to ask for your forgiveness for the mistake I have done.'

In literal meaning, such idioms can be considered as the kneeling position.

'Get down on your knees or I'll kick your legs with this long stick!!'
