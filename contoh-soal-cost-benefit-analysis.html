18. Define of the following terms 1. Capital 2. Cost benefit analysis 3. Factor of production 4. Human capital 5. Market


Jawaban:

1. wealth in the form of money or other assets owned by a person or organization or available or contributed for a particular purpose such as starting a company or investing.

2. A cost-benefit analysis is a process businesses use to analyze decisions. The business or analyst sums the benefits of a situation or action and then subtracts the costs associated with taking that action.

19. Aktivitas A merupakan aktivitas ekonomi yang memiliki fungsi total benefit (TB) dan total cost (TC), sebagai berikut: TB = 50A-0,0125A² TC = 40A +0,0125A² Aktivitas A diukur dalam unit sedangkan TB dan TC diukur dalam dollar. a. Tentukan fungsi marginal benefit (MB), marginal cost (MC), dan net benefit (NB).b. Pada tingkat optimum dari aktivitas A di atas, berapa nilai-nilai total benefit (TB), total cost (TC), marginal benefit (MB), marginal cost (MC), dan net benefit (NB).​


Jawaban:

TB = 50A - 0,0125A²

TC = 40A + 0,0125A²

a.

MB = ΔTB/ΔA = 50 - 0,025A

MC = ΔTC/ΔA = 40 + 0,025A

NB = TB - TC = 50A - 0,0125A² - [40A + 0,0125A²]

NB = 10A - 0,025A²

b. Tingkat optimum: NB' = ΔNB/ΔA = 0

ΔNB/ΔA = 0

10 - 0,05A = 0

10 = 0,05A

A = 200

TB = 50 (200) - 0,0125 (200)² = 9500

TC = 40 (200) + 0,0125 (200)² = 8500

MB = 50 - 0,025 (200) = 45

MC = 40 + 0,025 (200) = 45

NB = TB - TC = 9500 - 8500 = 1000
