Thank you very much. Thank you very much, it was very helpful.Thank you, I appreciate your help.Thank you.Thanks a lot.Thanks a bunch.Wow, you are so kind.I owe you big time.I'm grateful for you.We really appreciate what you did.It was very kind of you.That's so pretty.You sound amazing!Such a great idea!I appreciate your effort.Nice work guys!You did so well.What would I do without you?To say thank you is not enough.I can't thank you enough.I truly appreciate.All I can say is thank you.We’re so thankful.You’re the best!Hats off to you.You’re a lifesaver.You made my day.I appreciate your time.I feel blessed to have such great coworkers.You’ve gone above and beyond.

Pembahasan

Appreciation menurut Oxford Learner's Dictionaries adalah perasaan bersyukur atas sesuatu atau bisa disebut sebagai rasa terima kasih atas suatu hal. Terdapat banyak sekali cara kita untuk mengungkapkan rasa syukur/terima kasih, sebagai contoh adalah jawaban di atas merupakan kalimat untuk mengungkapkan rasa terima kasih atau mengapresiasi orang lain.
