31. Bahasa indo wish you happy


Answer: 

The sentence above is actually incorrect. It should be
 'I wish you're happy.'    OR
'I wish for your happiness.'   OR
'wish you a happy life.'

Those three expression shows one meaning of 'wish' as a hope for the future. One normally says such when they are departing, on certain events or occasion like birthday, marriage and so on. 
Meanwhile the Indonesian translation of it is 'semoga kau bahagia.'

Kelas: SMP
Mapel: English
Kategori: translationberharap kamu senang
