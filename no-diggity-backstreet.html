16. 4. Write 3 parts of generic structures in Recount text! Flea Market I went to flea market in Cambodia with my friend 10 years ago. After arrived in cheap hotel in Hanoi, we went to the market by bike. When we arrived there, we looked a lot of things to buy. I saw someone selling some old cassettes in the corner of the street. Then I tried to choose my favorite singer when I was young. It was about ten minutes to get the cassette entitled "Backstreet Boys". They were American boy band on 2000's. I bought two old albums of Backstreet Boys. They were first and second album named "Millenium" and "Black and Blue". I paid twenty thousand rupiah for both. I was really happy at that time. Finally, my friend and I could hear our favorite's songs for a long time.​


1. Orientation: The introduction of the text that sets the scene, identifies the participants, and establishes the time and place of the event. For example, "I went to flea market in Cambodia with my friend 10 years ago."

2. Events: The body of the text that describes a series of events that took place in chronological order. In this recount text, it includes the experience of going to the flea market, looking at various items for sale, choosing and purchasing the Backstreet Boys cassette, and the feelings of happiness and excitement that followed.

3. Reorientation: The conclusion of the text that provides closure to the events and often reflects on the significance of the experience. For example, "Finally, my friend and I could hear our favorite's songs for a long time."
