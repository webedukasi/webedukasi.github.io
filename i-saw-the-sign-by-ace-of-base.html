38. the name of sign is........pliss bantu!!​


That little "a" with a circle curling around it that is found in email addresses is most commonly referred to as the "at" symbol. Surprisingly though, there is no official, universal name for this sign. There are dozens of strange terms to describe the @ symbol.

The tilde is used as a diacritical mark over various letters to indicate a variety of sounds in different languages, but it also appears midline, like a dash (and is sometimes called a swung dash), to denote “approximately (“Last night's attendance: ~100”).

The at sign, @, is normally read aloud as "at"; it is also commonly called the at symbol or commercial at. It is used as an accounting and invoice abbreviation meaning "at a rate of" (e.g. 7 widgets @ £2 per widget = £14), but it is now seen more widely in email addresses and social media platform handles.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Identifikasi bentuk alasTentukan volumeTentukan luas permukaan

SOAL NOMOR 3A

Merupakan bangun ruang prisma segitiga maka alasnya adalah segitiga

1. Tentukan volume

Volume prisma = luas alas x tinggi prima

Volume prisma = 12 x 16 x 14

Volume prisma = 2688 cm³

2. Tentukan luas permukaan

Luas permukaan = 2 x luas segitiga + luas ketiga persegi

Luas permukaan = 2 x 16 x 12 + 14 x 20 + 16 x 14 + 12 x 14

Luas permukaan = 1056 cm²

SOAL NOMOR 3B

1. Tentukan volume

volumenya adalah jumlah 5 volume balok. Karena bangun ruang tersebut terbentuk dari kumpulan balok.

Volume = 5 x 3 x 4 x 5

Volume = 300 cm³

2. Tentukan luas permukaan

Luas permukaan = 10 x 4 x 5 + 4 x 3 x 5 + 2 x 3 x 4 + 2 x 3 x 4

Luas permukaan = 308 cm²

SOAL NOMOR 3C

Merupakan bangun ruang prisma segitiga maka alasnya adalah segitiga

1. Tentukan volume

Volume prisma = luas alas x tinggi prima

Volume prisma = 6 x 8 x 14

Volume prisma = 672 cm³

2. Tentukan luas permukaan

Luas permukaan = 2 x 6 x 8 + 14 x 8 + 14 x 6 + 14 x 10

Luas permukaan = 432 cm²

SOAL NOMOR 3D

1. Tentukan volume

Volume = p x l x b tiga balok
