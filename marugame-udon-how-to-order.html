10. Buatlah teks prosedur tentang :How to write order letter


Jawaban:

An order letter is usually written when you need to assign orders for goods. It is quite common and is written almost on a daily basis. The language of the letter, however, needs to be formal and the letter follows a standard format, since the information is very specific.

An order letter needs to pen down the terms and conditions of the purchase. These benefit both the involved parties. It generally contains details such as product specifications, their quantities, a price that the parties have agreed upon, the date of delivery, what to do in case there occurs a late delivery, etc.

Tips before Writing an Order Letter:

Before writing the order letter, here are some tips for you:

The letter should be very precise, there is no pep talk or casual talking required here.

The language should be easy while also being formal.

Do not make any mistakes in the specifications and quantities.

Avoid spelling and grammatical errors.

Stick to the topic

Be polite. You are ordering for goods, you're not ordering the person.

Avoid using too many adjectives.

Don't use jargons.

Don't hesitate to place the order, but don't forget to plan before writing.

Now I can tell you how to write an order letter, since you now know all that you needed to know before writing one.

Elements of the Order Letter:

An order letter needs the following things to be in check:

- Contact information about yourself, the person/company that will supply to you and the date.

A subject line that helps the recipient to understand your purpose at once.

- A salutation, like, if you know the name of the seller, write, “Dear ……………”. If you don't know the name of your seller, simply write “Dear Seller”

- Information about your company; but this is totally optional. However, this can ensure a long-term relationship. You can also say a few words about your future plans related to the sale.

- Order details, and by details I mean everything. The model number, color, size, number, etc. Be attentive since they'll send whatever you will write. If possible, use bullet points or tables. If you are buying different items of different types, describe them in separate paragraphs.

- Mention your payment mode or if you've paid already, mention that next.

- Indicate your preferred delivery method and date.

- Include any special instructions as a part of the terms and conditions agreed between the parties.

- Tell them that you'll enjoy future cooperation with them and encourage them to contact if needed.

- Close with “Sincerely”, your signature and name.

The quality of the delivered goods will depend on your instructions. Not just that, it is literally a legal document between yourself and the seller. So beware of what goes in your order letter and pay a good deal of attention while drafting one.

19. Berikan 10 contoh in order to,in order not to,so that,to​


Berikut di bawah ini adalah 10 contoh in order to,in order not to,so that, to​:

In order to succeed in life, one must set clear goals and work tirelessly to achieve them. This phrase "in order to" is used to express the purpose or goal of taking a specific action. In this case, the action is setting clear goals and working hard, and the purpose is to achieve success in life.In order not to disturb the neighbors, please keep the noise level down after 10 PM. "In order not to" is used to convey the purpose of avoiding a specific action. Here, the purpose is to prevent disturbing the neighbors, and the action to avoid is making loud noises after 10 PM.She studied diligently so that she could pass her final exams with flying colors. "So that" is used to indicate the intended outcome or result of an action. Here, the action is studying diligently, and the intended result is passing the final exams with excellent grades.He wore a raincoat so that he wouldn't get wet in the downpour. "So that" is again used to express the intended outcome of an action. In this case, the action is wearing a raincoat, and the desired result is staying dry in the heavy rain.I brought an umbrella to the picnic so that we would be prepared if it started raining. "So that" here indicates the reason for bringing an umbrella to the picnic, which is to be prepared for rain.She locked the door in order to keep her valuables safe while she was away. "In order to" is used to explain the reason for locking the door, which is to ensure the safety of her valuables during her absence.He whispered in class so as not to disturb the teacher during her lecture. "So as not to" is a variation of "in order not to" and is used to convey the purpose of speaking quietly—to avoid disrupting the teacher while she is giving a lecture.Sarah turned off her phone so that she could focus completely on her work. "So that" is employed here to indicate the reason for turning off the phone, which is to eliminate distractions and concentrate on work.They planted trees along the riverbank so that the soil would be stabilized and erosion prevented. "So that" is used to express the desired outcome of planting trees, which is to stabilize the soil and prevent erosion.He took a deep breath before speaking so that he could calm his nerves. "So that" is used to explain the purpose of taking a deep breath—to achieve a state of calmness before speaking.Pembahasan

Tiga frasa yang umum digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris untuk mengungkapkan tujuan atau niat di balik suatu tindakan adalah "in order to," "in order not to," dan "so that." Mari kita bahas penjelasannya dalam bahasa Indonesia:

"In Order To" digunakan untuk menyatakan tujuan atau maksud di balik suatu tindakan. Ketika Anda menggunakan frasa ini, Anda ingin menjelaskan mengapa Anda melakukan sesuatu. Contohnya, "Dia belajar dengan tekun in order to lulus ujian akhir dengan gemilang." Ini berarti tujuan belajarnya adalah agar bisa lulus ujian dengan nilai yang baik."In Order Not To" juga digunakan untuk mengekspresikan tujuan, tetapi dalam konteks menghindari suatu tindakan. Contohnya, "Tolong diamkan volume suara setelah pukul 10 malam in order not to mengganggu tetangga." Di sini, tujuannya adalah menghindari mengganggu tetangga dengan tidak membuat suara keras setelah pukul 10 malam."So That" digunakan untuk menunjukkan hasil yang diinginkan dari suatu tindakan. Ketika Anda menggunakan frasa ini, Anda menjelaskan apa yang Anda harapkan akan terjadi sebagai akibat dari tindakan tersebut. Contohnya, "Dia memakai mantel hujan so that tidak basah saat hujan turun." Tujuannya adalah agar tidak basah saat hujan datang.

30. Apa perbedaan dari "in order to" dengan "in order not to"?​


Jawaban:

In order to is a subordinating conjunction.

We use in order to with an infinitive form of a verb to express the purpose of something. It introduces a subordinate clause. It is more common in writing than in speaking:

[main clause]

Mrs Weaver had to work full-time

[subordinate clause]

in order to earn a living for herself and her family of five children.

We all need stress in order to achieve and do our best work

The negative of in order to is in order not to:

They never parked the big van in front of the house in order not to upset the neighbours
