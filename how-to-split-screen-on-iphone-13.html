14. Search for lost phone by IMEI code online - How to find a phone number, Find IPhone


To find a lost phone by IMEI code online, refer to the following steps:

1. Visit the lost or stolen report site to select the type of lost phone, such as iPhone, Android, Samsung, or other brands.

2. Select the “Report lost or stolen” option and fill in the required data, such as phone number, IMEI number, or serial number correctly.

3. Upload supporting documents, such as proof of phone purchase.

4. Fill in the required data, such as E-mail address and shipping address correctly.

5. Press the “Report Lost or Stolen” button.

6. Wait for some time, until the report of loss or theft is stated to be received on the website and the data will be sent to the authorities who will help find the lost cellphone.

However, do not forget that searching for a lost phone by IMEI code online does not guarantee that it will be easily found. However, this lost or stolen report process can help recover lost mobile data as quickly as possible by coordinating effectively and directly with the authorities.

1. A descriptive text is a type of text that describes a particular person, place, or thing in detail using sensory observations.

2. The purpose of a descriptive text is to provide vivid and detailed sensory information about a person, place, or thing to the reader or listener.

3. Yes, the general structures of a descriptive text are: an introduction that presents the object of description, a body that provides detailed sensory descriptions, and a conclusion that summarizes the description.

4. The general structures of a procedure text are: an introduction that states the goal of the procedure, a list of materials or ingredients needed, a series of step-by-step instructions, and a conclusion that may include warnings, tips, or suggestions.
