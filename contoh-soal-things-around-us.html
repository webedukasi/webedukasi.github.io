39. They are many things around us. We will find and describe them one by one! ​River


Jawaban:

A river is a naturally flowing watercourse, usually freshwater, flowing towards an ocean, sea, lake, or another river. Rivers are part of the hydrological cycle, and they are a major source of energy, providing water for irrigation and hydroelectric power production. Rivers are also important for transportation, providing a route for boats and barges. They are also important for recreation, such as fishing, boating, and swimming. Rivers also provide a variety of habitats for wildlife and are home to many different species of fish, mammals, birds, and reptiles.
