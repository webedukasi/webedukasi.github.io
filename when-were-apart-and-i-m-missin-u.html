Answer:

17 seedlings

Step-by-step explanation:

Given;

The seedlings are at an equal distance in a straight line.

Distance between 3rd seedling and 5th seedling = 9/10 m

So, distance between 3rd seedling and 4th seedling = 9/10 ÷ 2 = 9/20 m

So, the distance between two seedlings in a line = 9/20 m

The given distance between 2nd seedling and the last seedling = 6 3/4 m = 27/4 m

Starting from 2nd seedling:

9/20 * x = 27/4

Where x = the number of times 9/20 to reach the last seedling.

x = 15

So, from 2nd seedling we need to move 15 positions to get a distances of 27/4.

Moving from 2 to 17, we get 17 as the last seedling.

So, the total number of seedlings = 17

13. The force of repulsion, F newtons (N), between two particles is inversely proportional to the square of the distance, d m, between the particles. (i) Write down a formula connecting F and d. (ii) When the particles are a certain distance apart, the force of repulsion is 20 N. Find the force when the distance is halved.


Jawaban:

(i) The formula connecting the force of repulsion, F, and the distance, d, can be written as:

F = k/d^2

where k is the constant of proportionality.

(ii) When the distance is halved, the new distance becomes d/2. We can use the formula to find the new force, F'.

F' = k/(d/2)^2

Since F = 20 N, we can substitute this value into the equation:

20 = k/d^2

Now we can solve for the value of k:

k = 20 * d^2

Substituting this value of k into the equation for F':

F' = (20 * d^2) / (d/2)^2

Simplifying the equation:

F' = (20 * d^2) / (d^2/4)

F' = 80

Therefore, when the distance is halved, the force of repulsion becomes 80 N.

Pendapat lainnya, cinta adalah sebuah aksi/kegiatan aktif yang dilakukan manusia terhadap objek lain, berupa pengorbanan diri, empati, perhatian, kasih sayang, membantu, menuruti perkataan, mengikuti, patuh, dan mau melakukan apa pun yang diinginkan objek tersebut
