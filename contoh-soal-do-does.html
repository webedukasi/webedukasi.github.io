1. contoh soal kalimat do does ​


Jawaban:

Do : untuk they, we, i, you

Does : untuk she, he, it

Penjelasan:

Contoh :

Do =>

I do my homework at home

We don't need to go

Do you go to school by bus?

Does =>

She does her homework at home

He doesn't remember his own name

Does she go to school by bus?

In Syaa Allah seperti itu contohnya

Kalimat " DO "

1. Do you come to his party last night? (apakah kamu datang ke pestanya malam kemarin?)

2. Do they play basket everyday? (apakah mereka bermain basket setiap hari?)

3. Do you work everyday? (apakah kamu bekerja setiap h

kalimat " DOES "

1. Mr. Yudhis does the campaign in my town. (Dia selalu makan burger untuk makan siang)

2. She does it. (Dia melakukannya)

3. Erin does her duty. (Erin mengerjakan kewajibannya)

4. He does his homework. (Dia mengerjakan pekerjaan rumahnya)

5. Does he do his homework? (Apakah dia mengerjakan pekerjaan rumahnya)

Penjelasan:

Kata do dan does merupakan bentuk kalimat present tense dari kata kerja to do. Present tense menjelaskan peristiwa yang terjadi di waktu sekarang dalam bentuk sederhana atau suatu pekerjaan yang dilakukan (kebiasaan) hari-hari.

Cats eat fish.
