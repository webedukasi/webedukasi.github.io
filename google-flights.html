21. Aktive to passiveThey cancelled all Flights to day​


Jawaban:

|| ACTIVE VOICE → PASSIVE VOICE ||

Active : They cancelled all flights today.

Passive : All flights were cancelled by them today.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat diatas merupakan kalimat simple past tense, karena menggunakan verb-2 (kata kerja bentuk kedua atau kata kerja bentuk past) yaitu "cancelled".Berikut rumus kalimat active dan passive simple past tense :

|| SIMPLE PAST TENSE RUMUS ||

Active : S + verb-2 + O

Passive : S + was/were + verb-3 + O

Contoh :

Active : She wrote a letter last night.Passive : A letter was wrote by her last night.Active : We played football yesterday.Passive : Football was played by us yesterday.Active : My sister sang a song last night.Passive : A song was sung by my sister last night.Active : He spoke English.Passive : English was spoken by him.

Note :

Objek pada kalimat aktif merupakan subjek pada kalimat pasif, sedangkan subjek pada kalimat aktif merupakan objek pada kalimat pasif yang didahului dengan kata penghubung "by".Was digunakan untuk subjek "He, She, It, I" sedangkan were digunakan untuk subjek "They, We, You".

24. Jadikan ke klimat pasif. The canceled all flights because of the fog.


Subject : English
Class : X
Chapter : Passive voice
Keywords : past simple

Code : 10.5.13 [Kelas 10 Bahasa Inggris Bab 13 - Passive Voice]

→ Active voice : they cancelled all flights because of fog (mereka membatalkan semua penerbangan dikarenakan kabut)

→ Passive voice : all flights were cancelled by them because of fog (semua penerbangan dibatalkan oleh mereka dikarenakan kabut)

✐ formula past simple
(+) O + was/were + Verb 3 (by S)
(-) O + was/were + not + Verb 3 (by S)
(?) was / were + O + Verb 3 (by S)?

Berikut merupakan pola kalimat yang digunakan dalam bentuk ini, yaitu:

ଓ Bentuk Aktif (Active Voice)

(+) S + V2 + O(–) S + did + not + V1 + O(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

ଓ Bentuk Pasif (Passive Voice)

(+) S + was/were + V3 + by + agent(–) S + was/were + not + V3 + by + agent(?) Was/Were + S + V3 + by + agent + ?
