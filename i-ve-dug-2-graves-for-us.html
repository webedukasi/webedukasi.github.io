Eva : congratulations ! you ‘ve been awarded the first ranking

nana : …

(A)i'am happy for you.

(B) keep up your achievement eva

(C) i will, thanks

From that expression that Eva told to Ana, Eva wants to tell ana that she is amazing because Ana get awarded for the first ranking.

Kind of expression that Eva told to Ana is called by a compliment. Ana that a person from get compliment is just to have reply with a thank you.

So from the list, (C) i will thanks is the most suitable for replying Eva.

Dialogue is an exchange of written or verbal conversations between two or more people,  a form of literary and theatrical depiction of such an exchange. As a philosophical or doctrine device, it is  associated primarily with the Socrates dialogue developed by Plato in the West, but the  antecedents are also found in other traditions, including Indian literature.

Compliments should be a positive experience, but in most cases the process of giving and receiving compliments often causes a lot of anxiety for everyone involved.
