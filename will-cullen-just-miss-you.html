5. Try not to be absent from class again for the rest of the semester. You (miss, already) … too many classes. You (miss) … two classes just last week.


Try not to be absent from class again for the rest of the semester. You (miss, already) have already missed too many classes. You missed two classes just last week.

Jawaban:

Try not to be absent from class again for the rest of the semester. You have already missed too many classes. You missed two classes just last week.

Penjelasan:

You have already missed two classes merupakan bentuk kalimat Present Perfect Tense:

Subject + Have / Has + already + verb-3

Sementara You missed two classes just last week adalah bentuk Simple Past Tense dilihat dari kata keterangan waktunya yaitu last week.
