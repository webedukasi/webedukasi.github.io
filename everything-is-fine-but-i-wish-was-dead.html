Answering Question About Unreal Past - WishJawaban
Pendahuluan

I wish I did

Kita dapat menggunakan wish + past simple untuk membicarakan tentang sesuatu yang kita harapkan berbeda pada present atau future (yang mana hal tersebut sulit terjadi/tidak mungkin). Contoh:

I wish things were different, but this is the way they are.  We wish we had enough money to help you.I wish I could be there for you tomorrow.  
Pembahasan

1. I am not a millionaire, but I wish I were.

2. I don't know the weather forecast, but I wish I knew.

3. It is raining outside, but I wish it wasn't.

4. The library doesn't open until 7 o'clock, I wish it did.

5. I want to leave but I can't, I wish I could.

6. I don't have a care, but I wish I had.

7. The weather forecast says it will be windy, I wish it wouldn't.

8. The sun isn't shining. I wish the sun was shining right now.

9. It's cold today, I wish it wasn't.

10. I can't go with you, but I wish I could.

Jawaban:

S + infinitive (V1) (+s/es) / to be (is, am, are) + O.

Kalimat negatif: S + do/does not + infinitive (V1) + O.

S + to be (is, am, are) + not + O.

Kalimat tanya: Do/does + S + infinitive (V1) + O?

to be (is, am, are) + S + O?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) I go to school every day. / Aku pergi ke sekolah setiap hari.

(-) I don’t go to school every day. / Aku tidak pergi ke sekolah setiap hari.

(?) Do you go to school every day? / Apakah kamu pergi ke sekolah setiap hari?

(+) My sister is a kindergarten student. / Adikku adalah seorang murid TK.

(-) My sister isn’t a kindergarten student. / Adikku bukan seorang murid TK.

(?) Is your sister a kindergarten student? / Apakah adikmu seorang murid TK?

2. Present continuous tense

Present continuous tense biasa digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang sedang dilakukan atau sedang terjadi di masa sekarang.

Rumus:

Kalimat positif: S + to be (is, am, are) + Ving.

Kalimat negatif: S + to be (is, am, are) + not + Ving.

Kalimat tanya: to be (is, am, are) + S + Ving?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) Rani is writing a letter. / Rani sedang menulis surat.

(-) Rani isn’t writing a letter. / Rani tidak sedang menulis surat.

(?) Is Rani writing a letter? / Apakah Rani sedang menulis surat?

Baca juga: 16 Jenis Tenses Lengkap Beserta Contohnya

3. Simple past tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan peristiwa atau kejadian yang terjadi di masa lalu. Biasanya waktunya spesifik.

Rumus:

Kalimat positif: S + V2.

Kalimat negatif: S + did not + V1.

Kalimat tanya: Did + S + V1?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) Beni washed his car yesterday. / Beni mencuci mobilnya kemarin.

(-) Beni didn’t wash his car yesterday. / Beni tidak mencuci mobilnya kemarin.

(?) Did Beni wash his car yesterday? / Apakah Beni mencuci mobilnya kemarin?

4. Past continuous tense

Past continuous tense digunakan ketika kita berbicara tentang kejadian atau kegiatan yang sedang terjadi di masa laluBiasanya waktunya spesifik.

Rumus:

Kalimat positif: S + was/were + Ving.

Kalimat negatif: S + was/were + not + Ving.

Kalimat tanya: Was/were + S + Ving?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) Sinta was reading a novel at 5 p.m. this evening. / Sinta sedang membaca novel jam 5 sore tadi.

(-) Sinta wasn’t reading a novel at 5 p.m. this evening. / Sinta tidak sedang membaca novel jam 5 sore tadi.

(?) Was Sinta reading a novel at 5 p.m. this evening? / Apakah Sinta sedang membaca novel jam 5 sore tadi
