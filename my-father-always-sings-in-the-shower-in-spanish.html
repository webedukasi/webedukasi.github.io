8. (+)My father works in the hospital. (-) ? (?) ?


Jawaban:

(-)my father don't works in the hospital

(?)do my father works in the hospital?

Jawaban:

(+) My father works in the hospital.

(-) My father doesn't work in the hospital

(?) Does my father work in the hospital?

Penjelasan:

Simple present tense

(+) s + verb1-s/verb1 + o

(-) s + doesn't /don't + verb1 + o

(?) does/do + s + verb1 + o

Penggunaan do(verb-1) dan does(verb-1 + s/es)

Do (verb-1) → untuk They, we, I

Does (vetb-1 +es/s) → untuk he, she,it,serta untuk possesive pronoun (my...., her....,his...,our...,their...)

40. Arti my father and i always play basketbalk in the evening


Arti dari kalimat "My father and I always play basketball in the evening" adalah..

Saya dan Ayah saya selalu bermain bola basket di sore hari.

Penjelasan :

Struktur kalimat di atas merupakan bentuk dari simple present tense, dimana rumus nya :

S + V1 (s/es) + O

My father and I => Subject

Always => Adverb of frequency

Play => Verb-1

Basketball => Object

In the evening => Adverb of time
