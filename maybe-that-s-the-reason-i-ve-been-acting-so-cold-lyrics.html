28. if the blouse hadn't been so expensive , I ....... (buy) ....... it ​


║CONDITIONAL SENTENCE║

[tex]Answer[/tex]

If the blouse hadn't been so expensive , I would have bought it ​

[tex]Discussion[/tex]

Pertanyataan yang diberikan, termasuk kalimat bersyarat atau kalimat conditional tipe ke 3. Kalimat Conditional tipe ke 3 menjabarkan kejadian yang tak terealisasi di waktu lampau. Maka, penerapan struktur dari jenis conditional ini adalah :

If + S+had+verb 3 + Complement, S+would+have+verb 3 + C

Jadi, untuk mengisi titik-titiknya, dengan merubahnya ke bentuk kata kerja past future perfect => would have bought.

Sekali lagi, dalam pemahaman kalimat bersyarat/conditional sentence tipe apapun, harap di cermati konteks kalimat, sehingga dapat terlihat dengan jelas kondisi fakta yang terjadi.

"If the blouse hadn't been so expensive , I would have bought it "

Meaning => I didn't buy the blouse because its cost was expensive.

[tex]Explanation[/tex]

Conditional sentence tells about the condition that will be actualized ( possible condition ) or only dreaming ( intention/Andai-andai ) because of the particular condition. Conditional sentence structure consists -If clause and result clause ( main clause ).

→There are 4 types of Conditional Sentence←

1. ZERO CONDITIONAL

The Zero Conditional is used to describe the Phenomenon,  and General truth ( Fact  ). Zero Conditional uses the simple present tense form to reflect the truth.

Pattern :

IF+S+base verb (s/es ) + O/C , S+base verb (s/es ) + O/C

If + simple present , simple present

Example :

If we freeze water , it changes into an ice.

Meaning : It is proven that the water changes  when it is stored in the freezer.

2. TYPE 1 CONDITIONAL

The Type 1 Conditional is used to describe the truth  in the present/future ( The planning (prediction)  will be probably happened ). In fact, the speaker will actualize his/her dream now or soon .

Pattern :

If +S+ verb (s/es) + O, S+will+verb1+O/C

If+simple present, simple future

Example :

If it doesn't rain, I will come to your house.

Meaning : it's possible that I will come to your house later and I have to wait the good weather.

3. TYPE 2 CONDITIONAL

The Type 2 Conditional type  is used to describe  the untruth in the present / future. It is Contrary to the fact in the present or it could be in the future. The speaker has an intention ( dream ) but he/she can't afford his/her dream comes true in the present/future.

Pattern:

If+S+verb2+O/C , S+would+verb1+O/C

If+simple past, past future

Example :

If I were a bird, I would not take the plane.

Meaning :  I 'm not a bird, so, I take the plane.

4. TYPE 3 CONDITIONAL

The Type 3 Conditional  Is used to describe  the untruth in the pastIt is contrary to the fact in the past and It  reflects a regret for something that the speaker couldn't afford to do in the past.

Pattern :

If+S+Had+Verb 3+O/C  , S+Would+have+Verb 3+O/C.

If+past perfect, past future perfect

Example :

If I had had much time, I would have finished my articles.  

Meaning :  I didn't have much time, so I didn't finish my articles.

33. Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!​


Jawaban:

Kalimat "Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!" berarti "Temukan 2 lirik lagu yang menggunakan Present Perfect Tense dan 5 lirik yang menggunakan Simple Past Tense!"

Kalian dapat mencari lirik lagu yang dimaksud menggunakan pola kalimat berikut sebagai acuan:

1.Present Perfect Tense

(+) S + have/has + Past participle atau S + has/have + been + O

(-) S + have/has not + Past participle atau S + has/have not + been + O

(?) Have/has + S + Past participle? atau has/have + S + been + O?

2.Simple Past Tense

(+) S + Verb 2 + O atau S + to be (was/were) + O

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 atau S + to be (was/were) not + O

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 atau Was/Were + S + . . . ?

Salah satu contoh lirik lagu tersebut adalah:

Present Perfect Tense

"Or have you completely erased me from your memory?", lirik lagu Don't You Remember - Adele.

Simple Past Tense

"You gave me wings and made me fly", lirik lagu Because you love me - Celine Dion.
