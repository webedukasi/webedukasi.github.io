12. When the short hand is just past the twelve and the long hand is on the three. What time is it?


Jawaban:

12 past 15 o'clock atau 12.15

What time is it? It is a quarter past twelve (12.15).

Explanation:

Known:

The short hand is just past twelve and the long hand is on the three.

Asked:

What time is it?

Answer:

According to the statement, we know that the short hand is just past the twelve and the long hand is on the three. It means the time is 12.15 (a quarter past twelve).

So, the time is 12.15 or a quarter past twelve.

Hopefully this helps you:)
