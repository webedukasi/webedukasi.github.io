25. what the meaning of agree?NB: bukan terjermahkan...tolong jawab pertanyaan dalam bahasa inggris​


Soal:

What the meaning of agree?

Dalam Bahasa Indonesia:

Apa Arti dari setuju?

Penjelasan:

Setuju adalah suatu keputusan yang bisa dikatakan "iya" atau "Oke" atau "meng- iyakan" suatu keputusan.

Dalam Bahasa Inggris:

Agree is a decision that can be said to be "yes" or "OK" or "accept" a decision.

Hope This Helps!

Jawaban :

Short answer :

Agree is a verb that states the same views or thought (harmonize in feeling and opinion)

Long answer :

Agree can be used as verb. It states that the speaker has the same views, thoughts, opinions, or emotions with some objects, points, statements, or kind of from their interlocutor (harmonize or suitable). Agree often followed by the word 'with', as an example "I'm agree with your opinion".
