Ada 3 macam degree of comparison :

1. Positive

Pattern :

• as .... as

• no less .... than

• not more .... than

Example : Riska runs as fast as Claudia.

2. Comparative

Pattern :

• .... -er than

• more .... than

Example : Sunny is more diligent than Krystal.

3. Superlative

Pattern :

• the .... -est of ....

• the .... -est + noun

• the most .... of

• the most .... + noun

Example : Mrs. Erni is the most patient teacher.
