6. + galuh and Andi want to visit their grandparents in the village -?​


Simple Present Tense (+) Galuh and Andi want to visit their grandparents in the village (-) Galuh and Andi do not want to visit their grandparents in the village (?) Do Galuh and Andi want to visit their grandparents in the village?

PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

Sebuah tenses yg digunakan utk menunjukkan Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa sekarang (Present), Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan (habitual) dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

(-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?

Catatan:Subject jamak “ada 2 nama” maka menggunakan Verb-1 “want” dan Auxiliary DO

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Subject Pronouns (kata ganti subjek) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Subject Pronouns

I = aku / saya

You = anda / kamu / kalian

We = kami

They = mereka

He = dia (cowok)

She = dia (cewek)

it = itu (digunakan untuk benda / hewan)
