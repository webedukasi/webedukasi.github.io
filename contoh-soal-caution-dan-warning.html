30. Soal-soal tentang caution, notice, warning


Explanation:

A notice is a short text which is used to give an information, give an instruction, or warn public. Notice will not make any personal injury if it's violated.
For example:
NOTICE
No smoking

A caution is a short text which is used to indicate a hazardous situation which could cause a minor or moderate injury if not avoided.
For example:
CAUTION
Wet floor

A warning is a short text which is used to indicate a hazardous situation which could cause a serious injury or death if not avoided.
For example:
WARNING
Unsafe for swimming

Answer:
I will give you 5 examples of question which related to the topic.

1. NOTICE
Please return all books when you have finished using them
Where can we find the notice above?
a. In the zoo
b. In the library
c. In the hospital
d. In the school
The correct answer is b

a. We are invited to enter the area
b. We are allowed to enter the area
c. We are not allowed to enter the area
d. The area is for children only
The correct answer is c

No food and drink allowed

a. we are not allowed bring food and drink in the area
b. we have to buy food and drink in the area
c. we are allowed to bring food and drink in the area
d. The food and drink in the area are expensive
The correct answer is a

4. CAUTION! Hot surface
From the text, we know that ...
a. We must touch the surface
b. The surface can cause allergy
c. We must not touch the surface
d. The surface is safe to be touched

5. WARNING! Road construction is in progress
Who is the warning addressed to?
a. To construction workers
b. To pedestrians
