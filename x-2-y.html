32. Kategori : SBMPTN 2008 Mtk. Dasar (x^-2 - y^-2) : (xy)^-2 = ... (A) (x+y)(x-y) (B) -(x+y)(x-y) (C) (x-y)^2 (D) x(x-y) (E) -x(x-y)


Ini jawaban dan pembahasannya. Jika kurang jelas, silahkan bertanya(x^-2 – y^-2) / (xy)^-2
= (x^-2 – y^-2) × (xy)²
= (x^-1 – y^-1)(x^-1 + y^-1) × (xy)²
= (1/x – 1/y)(1/x + 1/y) × (xy)²
= ((y – x)/xy)((x + y)/xy) × (xy)²
