19. contoh dialog suggest dan offer​


Penjelasan:

Sure, here are examples of a suggestion (suggest) and an offer in English:

Suggestion:

Person A: "I'm not sure what movie to watch tonight."

Person B: "How about watching a comedy? It will cheer you up."

Offer:

Person A: "I have so much work to do around the house."

Person B: "Would you like some help with that? I can help you clean the house this weekend."

I hope these examples help illustrate the difference between suggesting and offering in English!

34. contoh dan gambar suggest and offer?


"Offer" means "do you want this?" or "how about this?". You are offering the other person a choice to do/have something or not to do/have it.

• Do you want __?
• Would you like a ___?
• Want any of ___?

"A offers tea to B= A says to B "do you want tea?".

"A offers to drive B home = A says to B "Do you want me to drive you home?".

"Suggest" means "I think you should do this". You are offering the other person advice.

• I think it's better for you to ___
• You better ___
• You should do___

"A suggests B drink tea" = A says to B "I think you should drink tea". A is not saying "I will give you tea", but "You should drink tea". B can take this advice or not.

"A suggests B consult a lawyer" - A says to B "you should consult a lawyer". A is not offering to be a lawyer, only telling B that B should consult a lawyer. B can take this advice or leave it.Contoh:

I suggest you to buy that book.
I think you should get a haircut.
Would you like a banana?
Do you mind if I gave you my sweatshirt?

Suggest itu seperti opini yang membujuk kita melakukan, atau mendapat sesuatu, walaupun kita tidak mau.
Offer itu lebih ke pertanyaan apa kamu ingin ini atau tidak.

36. contoh dialog suggest dan ofer


Suggest (Saran):

John: Hi, Jade!
Jade: Hi there, John!
John: Oh, what are you wearing?
Jade: It's a green dress I got from my grandfather! What do you think?
John: It's good, but you would look better is you wore socks.
Jade: Okay, thanks, John!
John: No problem!

*Kesimpulan: Jade mendapatkan gaun berwarna hijau dari kakeknya. John memberi saran kepada Jade bahwa Jade akan terlihat lebih baik jika dia memakai kaus kaki. Dari percakapan ini, John telah memberi saran.

Offer (Penawaran):

Dave: Hey, Rose.
Rose; Hello. Why are you still here? I thought everyone went home.
Dave: My dad still hasn't come yet to pick me up.
Rose: Why don't you walk home with me?
Dave: Is that okay?
Rose: It's okay.
Dave: Thanks, Rose.
Rose: My pleasure.

*Kesimpulan: Saat jam pulang, Dave masih tetap berada di sekolah dan belum pulang. Rose bertanya mengapa dia belum pulang. Ternyata, ayah Dave belum menjemputnya. Karena Rose adalah temannya, Rose mengajak Dave untuk pulang bersamanya. Dave dengan senang hati menerima tawarannya. Dari percakapan ini, Rose telah memberi penawaran.

Semoga bermanfaat.
