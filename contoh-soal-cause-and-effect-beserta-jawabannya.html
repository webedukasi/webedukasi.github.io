3. tuliskan 3 contoh teks cause and effect beserta soal dan jawabannya


Jawaban:

Contoh 1:

Teks: Cause and Effect of Air Pollution

Cause: Industrialization and urbanization

Effect: Respiratory problems, global warming, and climate change

Soal: What is the cause and effect of air pollution?

Jawaban: The cause of air pollution is industrialization and urbanization, while the effects are respiratory problems, global warming, and climate change.

Contoh 2:

Teks: Cause and Effect of Obesity

Cause: Sedentary lifestyle and poor diet

Effect: Health problems such as diabetes, heart disease, and high blood pressure

Soal: What is the cause and effect of obesity?

Jawaban: The cause of obesity is a sedentary lifestyle and poor diet, while the effects are health problems such as diabetes, heart disease, and high blood pressure.

Contoh 3:

Teks: Cause and Effect of Stress

Cause: Work pressure, family problems, and financial issues

Effect: Depression, anxiety, and physical illness

Soal: What is the cause and effect of stress?

Jawaban: The cause of stress is work pressure, family problems, and financial issues, while the effects are depression, anxiety, and physical illness.

Penjelasan:

Namun dari kasus diatas teks tersebut dapat diorganisir dengan mengidentifikasikan faktor dan penyebab (cause) serta diiringi dengan efek (effect). Yang mana dari tujuannya itu utk menjelaskan mengapa dan bagaimana suatu kejadian itu terjadi serta dampak yang akan ditimbulkan akan bagaimana.

Contoh nya teks cause and effect berupa polusi udara dan dampak terhadap kesehatan manusia.

Contoh 1

I was lost so I opened Google Maps (Saya tersesat sehingga saya membuka Google Maps)

Cause : I was lost

Effect : I opened Google Maps

Conjunction : so

Contoh 2

The journey was quite quick because the road was clear. (perjalanan cukup cepat karena jalanan bersih dari hambatan )

Cause : the road was clear.

Effect : the journey was quite quick

Conjunction : because

As it is raining again, I will have to stay at home. (karena hujan sedang turun lagi, saya akan tinggal di rumah)

Cause : it is raining again.

Effect : I will have to stay at home.

Conjunction : as

Since she had not paid the rent,she was told to vacate the flat. (Karena dia belum membayar sewa, dia diberitahu untuk mengosongkan ruangan.)

Cause : she had not paid the rent.

Effect : she was told to vacate the flat.

Pembahasan

Untuk mengungkapkan cause and effect dalam bahasa Inggris, kita menggunakan signal words berikut:

due tobecause ofowing tothanks toas a consequence ofas a result ofbecausesinceasfor

Nah, untuk menunjukan cause and effect dalam kalimat ada dua jenis yakni yang diikuti kata benda (followed by noun) dan yang diikuti kata kerja (followed by verb).

Followed by noun

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Due to Jen’s laziness, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: Due toCause: Jen’s lazinessEffect: She didn’t pass the test

He was sick as a consequence of his ignorance.

Signal phrase: As a consequenceCause: His ignoranceEffect: He was sick

Followed by verb

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Because Jen was lazy, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: BecauseCause: Jen was lazyEffect: She didn’t pass the Math test

As he ignored his health, he will be sick,

Signal word: AsCause: He ignoredEffect: He will be sick

Paragraf Cause and Effect adalah paragraf yang berisi tentang pikiran-pikiran penulis yang disajikan dalam bentuk sebab dan akibat. Paragraf ini dimulai dengan penyebab suatu fenomena atau masalah kemudian dilanjutkan dengan akibat-akibat yang ditimbulkan oleh hal tersebut.

Generic Structure Paragraf Cause-Effect:

Diawali dengan gagasan utama atau kalimat pokok tentang permasalahan yang akan dibahas.Terdapat kalimat-kalimat pendukung yang berisi tentang penyebab dari suatu masalah.Paragraf diakhiri dengan kesimpulan yang berisi akibat-akibat yang ditimbulkan.

Language features dari Paragraf Cause-Effect:

Tenses akan tergantung pada konteks waktu, jadi tidak selalu menggunakan sebuah tenses yang sama.Menggunakan kalimat aktif dan pasifMenggunakan conjunction sebab-akibat seperti: due to,  because, since, consequently, as a result, because, cause dan lain lainMenggunakan sudut pandang orang ketiga.

(A) drops of water which enter through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

2. Sinkholes are …

(A) the decorative dripstone features found in caves.

(B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(C) colorful layers of mineral deposits.

(D) None of the above

Jawaban : (B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

3. Which speleothem grows upward from the floor?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Sinkholes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (A) Stalagmites

4. An “inclined ceiling” is one which …

(A) is straight.

(B) is crooked.

(C) is slanted.

(D) is wet.

Jawaban : (C) is slanted.

5. Which of the following are NOT caused by dripping water?

(A) Stalactites

(B) Stalagmites

(C) Slopes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (C) Slopes
