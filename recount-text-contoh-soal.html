1. 10 contoh soal recount text dalam 1 text


Jawaban:

A Trip to the Zoo

Yesterday my family and I went to the zoo.  It was a big zoo in our city. We left home at about 9 in the morning. It was one hour away driving.

When we got at the zoo, it was still not many visitors, we went to the shop to buy some food to give to the animals.  After getting the food we went to the nocturnal house where we saw birds and reptiles which only come out at night.  There were a lot of animals: elephants, tigers, lions, meerkat and etc.

Before lunch we went for a ride on the elephant. It was a thrill to ride it. My little brother was scared at first but he enjoyed it eventually. During lunch we fed some birds in the park. In the afternoon we saw the animals being fed by the zookeeper.

When we returned home we were all tired but happy because we had so much fun. We arrived home at about 4 o'clock.

Penjelasan:

Recount Text adalah suatu teks yang ditulis untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau peristiwa yang sudah terjadi di waktu lampau.

Tujuan dari recount text adalah untuk memberi informasi atau menghibur pembacanya. Untuk menyusun recount text, kalimat yang ditulis menggunakan tenses berikut ini:

Simple Past Tense

Past Continuous Tense

Past Perfect Tense

Generic Structure dari recount text adalah:

Orientation: pada bagian ini menyebutkan siapa saja yang terlibat dalam cerita, kapan dan dimana cerita tersebut terjadiEvents: menceritakan kembali secara kronologisRe-orientation: penutup cerita atau

3. contoh soal recount text dan pembahasannya


Contoh soal recount text dan pembahasannya:

1. What is recount text?

Answer: A recount text is a text to retell a past experience.

2. What is the purpose of recount text?

Answer: The purpose of recount text is to informing or entertaining the reader

3. Mention the generic status of recount text!

Answer: The generic status of recount text:

Orientation: this part is to inform who involve in the story, where and when the story happenedEvents: the story in the chronological sequenceRe-orientation: ending or closing

4. What tenses do we use in recount text?

Answer: Because recount text is about an experience in the past so the tenses used in the story are Simple Past Tense, Past Continuous Tense or Past Perfect Tense.

5. Give an example of recount text!

Answer:

On Saturday morning, we went to a farm in Lembang. We left home early morning. We arrived there at 9 AM. When we got there, some farmers were milking the cows. They asked us to try, it was fun milking the cow but it wasn't easy. After that, we all had a very nice hot milk and bread for breakfast.

Teks Recount Bahasa Inggris – Mendaki Gunung Merapi

teks recount bahasa inggris - mendaki gunung merapi

Orientation

Two days ago I went to the Merapi Mountain. It was the first time I climbed the mountain. I did it with some of my friends.

We start climbing at 8 p.m. It was so dark and we only used flashlight to get the way.

I felt no worry because all of my friends were professional climber. I just followed their instruction.

Event

We climbed slowly and enjoy the night there.

After 7 hours walking in the dark, we could reach the top of the mountain. It was at 3 a.m. early in the morning.

We were not alone. There were a lot of people who reached that top before us.

We waited the sun rises by cooking some food and making some hot drink to get back our energy. We sang some songs together, shared stories and got acquainted with people there.

After seeing the sun raised, we had to go back home. Getting down the mountain was not as hard as the climbing process because we only spent a few energy and time even we had to be more careful to do this process.

However, it was great experience i had ever done so far.

Reorientation

After all, that was my great experience I had ever have.

Terjemahan Bahasa Indonesia (Arti) Teks Recount Bahasa Inggris – Mendaki Gunung Merapi

Orientasi (Pembuka)

Dua hari yang lalu aku pergi ke gunung Merapi. Itu merupakan pertamakalinya aku akan melakukan pendakian gunung. Aku melakukannya bersama dengan teman-temanku.

Kami mulai melakukan pendakian pada pukul 8 malam. Situasi sangat gelap gulita dan kami hanya menggunakan lampu senter untuk mencari jalur pendakian.

Aku tidak merasa khawatir karena teman-temanku merupakan para pendaki profesional. Aku hanya tinggal mengikuti instruksi mereka saja.

Event (Isi)

Kami mendaki perlahan-lahan dan menikmati waktu kami di sana.

Setelah 7 jam perjalanan naik, akhirnya kami sampai di puncak gunung. Jam menunjukkan pukul 3 pagi.

Ternyata kami tidak sendirian. Banyak orang yang ternyata sudah sampai di puncak gunung sebelum kami datang.

Kami menunggu matahari terbut sambil memasak makanan dan membuat minuman hangat untuk mengembalikan tenaga kami. Kami juga bernyanyi bersama, bertukar cerita serta berkenalan dengan orang-orang baru di sana.

Setelah menikmati matahari terbit, kami harus segera pulang. Turun gunung tidak secapek pendakian, kami hanya butuh sedikit tenaga dan waktu namun kami harus melakukannya dengan hati-hati.

Bagaimanapun juga, pengalaman tersebut merupakan pengalaman mengesankan yang pernah aku alami sejauh ini.

Reorientasi (Kesimpulan)

Selebihnya, pengalaman tersebut merupakan pengalaman hebat yang pernah aku alami.

18. Recount Text - Pengertian, Struktur, & Contoh Recount Text Lengkap - Brainly


PART = RECOUNT TEXT -GENERIC STRUCTURE-SOCIAL FUNCTION

-----------------------

Jawaban :

Example of Personal Recount text and its generic structure :

Last week, my sister and  i went to Yogyakarta for a routine visiting. My grandmother has stayed in Yogyakarta for many years, hence we visit her twice a month. We went there by using a car. We had decided  the journey before the weekend came. { ORIENTATION PART - STORY PLOT }

After we arrived in my grandmother's house, we spoke to each other while enjoying the served food. We asked the permission to my grandmother to join with us for sightseeing. Finally, my grandmother was agreed to our offering. For the first place, we visited the lord palace "Keraton Jogja". We could see many unique ornaments as the masterpiece of cultural Javanese heritage. My grandmother said that place kept the heritage of the palace relatives. We were there for only thirty minutes, then we decided to go home. { SEQUENCES OF EVENT }

In the following day, my grandmother and us went to Gembira loka zoo. Gembira loka zoo is the only great zoo in Yogyakarta. We could see variety of animals. We took pictures of tigers, giraffes and crocodiles. After we were satisfied in seeing animals, we went to the restaurant for lunch. We ordered the special menu of Yogyakarta depiction, Gudeg Jogja. Its taste was unique, sweet and chicken flavor. We loved it. { SEQUENCES OF EVENT }

Then, the closing trip, we went to Malioboro streets to buy souvenirs such as , batik clothes, beads bracelets and some t-shirts. We bought the local handmade  woven bag for my grandmother. We could find everything over Malioboro streets. The vendors offered variety of goods friendly to those who passed through over Malioboro streets. We  took my grandmother home before our leaving. We asked permission to my grandmother to go home. { SEQUENCES OF EVENT }

That was our relative visiting. Even though we were exhausted to explore the places in Yogyakarta, we were satisfied by its uniqueness. Yogyakarta is the exotic place where still keeps the Javanese culture. { RE-ORIENTATION - THE WRITER'S CONCLUSION }

Penjelasan:

RECOUNT TEXT  Genre teks ini menjelaskan kembali kejadian penting / pengalaman yang terlibat di masa lalu.

KINDS OF RECOUNT TEXT :

1. Personal

Jenis recount ini membahas kisah pengalaman pribadi si penulis

2. factual

Jenis recount ini berisi kejadian fakta bersejarah ( Historical ) yang terjadi di waktu lampau

3. Imaginative

Jenis recount ini berisi kisah fiksi dan alur cerita pentingnya, dirujukan pada perspektif masing-masing pembaca.SOCIAL FUNCTION / COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSETo give the important information for readers  ( untuk memberikan imformasi penting terhadap pembaca )To retell the past events (experience) to readers.  ( untuk menceritakan ulang kejadian / pengalaman masa lalu terhadap pembaca )GENERIC STRUCTURE  

1. Orientation

Paragraf awal sebagai perkenalan terkait dengan orang-orang yang terlibat, waktu dan lokasi yang ditentukan dalam cerita-nya.

2. Sequenced Events

Paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan runtutan-runtutan kejadiannya seperti apa.

3. Re -orientation

Paragraf terakhir sebagai closing (penutup) yang menjabarkan kesimpulan akhir dari cerita yang dijabarkan. Pada umumnya berisi ungkapan si penulis.LANGUAGE FEATURES ( UNSUR KEBAHASAAN )Penggunaan  tenses lampau ( Past Tense )  pada strukur kalimat nya.Penggunaan  kata kerja aksi  ( action verb )lampau, seperti : went, bought, paid...etcPenggunaan keterangan penghubung untuk merinci kejadian secara runtut, seperti : after ......, then......Dominasi  pada specific participants
