Maaf Kalo Salah:)Alkilasi dan Asilasi
Alkilasi Friedel-Crafts melibatkan alkilasi dari cincin aromatik dan alkil halida menggunakan katalis asam Lewis. Dengan menggunakan feri klorida sebagai katalis, gugus alkil melekat pada posisi ion klorida sebelumnya.
Asilasi Friedel-Crafts adalah asilasi cincin aromatik dengan asil klorida menggunakan katalis asam lewis yang kuat. Asilasi Friedel-Crafts dapat terjadi dengan asam anhidrida. Kondisi reaksi ini sama dengan alkilasi Friedel-Crafts di atas.

24. guys tolong buatin procedure tentang handy craft dari kertas ya pakai bahasa inggris... please.... beserta artinya....terimakasih....


Jawaban:

How to Make a Paper Flower

Materials :

• Colored paper

• Scissors

• Glue

• Pencil

• Ruler

Steps :

1. Choose a color of paper you want to use for your flower. Cut the paper into a square shape. The bigger the square, the bigger the flower will be.

2. Fold the square paper in half, diagonally. Make a crease with your fingers to form a triangle.

3. Take the top of the triangle and fold it down, aligning it with the bottom edge of the triangle. Make a crease with your fingers to form a smaller triangle.

4. Take the left corner of the smaller triangle and fold it towards the center of the triangle. Repeat this step with the right corner of the triangle.

5. Take the top of the triangle and fold it down again, about a third of the way down. This will form the petal of your flower.

6. Repeat steps 2 to 5 with more square papers until you have enough petals for your flower. The more petals you have, the fuller your flower will be.

7. Once you have all the petals, arrange them in a circular shape, with the tips of the petals pointing towards the center.

8. Use the glue to stick the base of each petal to the next one until all petals are attached.

9. Cut a small circle from a different color of paper to form the center of your flower.

10. Glue the center of your flower in the middle of your petal arrangement.

11. Allow the glue to dry completely.

The paper flower is now complete! You can make as many flowers as you like using different colors of paper to create a beautiful bouquet.
