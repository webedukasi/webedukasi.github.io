2. Why young komodos start living on the ground after 4 years?​


Because, to avoid being eaten by their mother or other Komodos.

Penjelasan:

Bukti teks :

There is no evidence that parents care for newly hatched Komodos, according to the Smithsonian Zoo. At birth, baby dragons are only 12 inches (30 centimeters) long. As soon as they hatch, the young will run away and climb up trees to avoid being eaten by their mother or other Komodos. When they are 4 years old and around 4 feet (1.2 m), the young Komodos will come down and live on the ground, according to the San Diego Zoo. Those that survive can look forward to a long life. A Komodo can live to more than 30 years.

14. The young lady has been living next door since i...that house to her? Pilihanua: Sell Sold Have sold Had sold Am selling


The young lady has been living next door since i ............that house to her.

a. sell  

b. sold

c. have sold

d. had sold

e. am selling

------------HOW TO BUILD PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS-----------

JAWABAN : B. SOLD

ALASAN :

Karena kejadian terhitung di waktu lampau dan masih dan sedang berlangsung saat ini  . Perempuan muda itu tinggal di rumah yang terhitung saat si penulis menjual rumahnya nya dan perempuan tersebut masih tinggal di rumah sampai saat ini.

PENJELASAN :

PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS TENSE

A tense is used to describe the duration of an unfinished activity in the past and still going on now ( in progress recently ).

Tenses ini digunakan menjabarkan waktu dari kegiatan yang belum diselesaikan terhitung di waktu lampau dan masih sedang berlangsung sampai sekarang.

ADVERB OF TIME :

for   ( it is used to describe a duration of time which began in the past until now )

since ( it is used to described the specific time )

PATTERN :

Subject + have/has + been + Verb-ing + Object/complement

Example :

I have been using Brainly for five months.   ( duration )

I have been writing  the short articles  since i was ten years old . ( specific time )

It has been raining all week. It is still running until now.

Past perfect continous
jawaban : c. had been livingJoni Has been living in Bandung For five Years before he moved in Semarang.

Jawaban B.

Penjelasan :

Joni Adalah Subjek Laki-laki yang dalam bahasa inggris termasuk "He"
Ini merupakan Perfect Tenses. dan Ketika Subjeknya adalah "he" maka Harus menggunakan "Has" sebagai tobe nya. 

Rumus untuk Kalimat ini (Perfect Tenses) :

Subjek + HAVE(gunakan "HAVE" jika subjek i,you,we,they) / HAS(gunakan "HAS" jika subjek He,She,It) + Past Participle / Verb 3
