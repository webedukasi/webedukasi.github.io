8. Millions of years ago, dinosaurus (roam).........the earth, but they (become) .............. Extinct by the time humankind first (appear)...............


Past Simple vs Past Perfect Tense

Penggunaan past simple dan past perfect tense sama-sama menunjuk pada kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lampau, namun dengan satu perbedaan. Jika kita ingin menyebutkan rangkaian kegiatan di masa lampau, kita cukup menggunakan past simple tense untuk semua kegiatan tersebut. Tapi, jika kita ingin menekankan bahwa suatu kegiatan dilakukan/terjadi setelah kegiatan lainya sudah terselesaikan, atau kegiatan yang lebih lampau dari kegiatan lampau lainnya, maka kegiatan yang terjadi lebih dulu dah sudah terselesaikan tersebut ditulis dalam bentuk past perfect tense.

Bandingkan dua contoh kalimat berikut:

Last Monday was a holiday, so I woke up and stayed in bed until almost 11 o'clock. Then I had a shower, got changed, and went to the diner for brunch. (semuanya simple past tense)Last Sunday, my wife and I woke up with a start. My mother-in-law came to visit us and we were supposed to take her on an excursion at 10 o'clock, and we were still in bed at 9. Fortunately, I had arranged everything a week ahead, so we only needed to get ready and set out. (kegiatan "had arranged" (mengatur) sudah dilaksanakan 1 minggu sebelum kejadian hari Minggu tersebut, jadi ditulis dalam bentuk past perfect tense)
Pembahasan

Jawaban yang benar untuk kalimat pada soal adalah:

Millions of years ago, dinosaurs roamed the earth, but they had become extinct by the time humankind first appeared.

Kalimat awal adalah simple past tense karena menunjuk pada waktu jutaan tahun yang lalu. Kalimat bagian kedua menunjuk pada waktu manusia pertama muncul, sehingga keterangan waktu ditulis dalam simple past tense namun kejadian punahnya dinosaurus terjadi sebelum kemunculan manusia, maka harus ditulis dalam bentuk past perfect tense.

Title: Preserving Animal Welfare: Why Zoos Should Be Closed Down and Animals Given a Chance to Roam Free

Thesis: It is imperative to close down zoos and provide animals with the opportunity to live freely, as it promotes their overall well-being, preserves biodiversity, and encourages conservation efforts.

Argument 1: Promoting Animal Well-being

Zoos, despite their intentions, often fail to adequately address the needs of animals. Animals are confined to limited spaces, deprived of their natural habitats, and subjected to constant scrutiny by visitors. This unnatural environment leads to stress, anxiety, and physical health issues. By closing down zoos, we can eliminate these detrimental conditions and offer animals the chance to experience freedom and exhibit their natural behaviors. Animals deserve to thrive in their natural habitats, where they can engage in instinctual activities, establish social bonds, and maintain a balanced ecosystem.

Argument 2: Preserving Biodiversity

Closing zoos would contribute to the preservation of biodiversity. Many animals kept in captivity are endangered species, and zoos often serve as a means of displaying them to the public. However, these displays do little to address the root causes of endangerment and do not actively contribute to species conservation. Instead, we should focus on protecting and restoring natural habitats, providing the animals with the opportunity to thrive in their native environments. This approach will have a more significant impact on preserving biodiversity and ensuring the long-term survival of endangered species.

Argument 3: Encouraging Conservation Efforts

Closing zoos would shift our focus towards alternative forms of conservation that prioritize habitat restoration, environmental education, and sustainable practices. Rather than confining animals for display purposes, we should invest in programs that educate the public about wildlife conservation, promote sustainable practices, and support local communities in protecting natural habitats. This approach would foster a deeper appreciation for wildlife and inspire individuals to take active roles in preserving our planet's biodiversity.

Recommendation: Embrace Responsible Conservation Practices

In light of these arguments, it is recommended that we close down zoos and redirect our efforts towards responsible conservation practices. Governments, wildlife organizations, and individuals should support initiatives that prioritize habitat preservation, implement stringent laws against wildlife exploitation, and promote sustainable tourism practices. 30. Millions of years ago, dinosaurus (roam) ............. The earth, but they (become) .......... Extinct by the time humankind first (appear) ...........


Past Simple vs Past Perfect Tense

Penggunaan past simple dan past perfect tense sama-sama menunjuk pada kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lampau, namun dengan satu perbedaan. Jika kita ingin menyebutkan rangkaian kegiatan di masa lampau, kita cukup menggunakan past simple tense untuk semua kegiatan tersebut. Tapi, jika kita ingin menekankan bahwa suatu kegiatan dilakukan/terjadi setelah kegiatan lainya sudah terselesaikan, atau kegiatan yang lebih lampau dari kegiatan lampau lainnya, maka kegiatan yang terjadi lebih dulu dah sudah terselesaikan tersebut ditulis dalam bentuk past perfect tense.

Bandingkan dua contoh kalimat berikut:

Last Monday was a holiday, so I woke up and stayed in bed until almost 11 o'clock. Then I had a shower, got changed, and went to the diner for brunch. (semuanya simple past tense) Last Sunday, my wife and I woke up with a start. My mother-in-law came to visit us and we were supposed to take her on an excursion at 10 o'clock, and we were still in bed at 9. Fortunately, I had arranged everything a week ahead, so we only needed to get ready and set out. (kegiatan "had arranged" (mengatur) sudah dilaksanakan 1 minggu sebelum kejadian hari Minggu tersebut, jadi ditulis dalam bentuk past perfect tense)

Pembahasan

Jawaban yang benar untuk kalimat pada soal adalah:

Millions of years ago, dinosaurs roamed the earth, but they had become extinct by the time humankind first appeared.

Kalimat awal adalah simple past tense karena menunjuk pada waktu jutaan tahun yang lalu. Kalimat bagian kedua menunjuk pada waktu manusia pertama muncul, sehingga keterangan waktu ditulis dalam simple past tense namun kejadian punahnya dinosaurus terjadi sebelum kemunculan manusia, maka harus ditulis dalam bentuk past perfect tense.
