5. contoh teks prosedur bahasa inggris membuat kue


How to Make Cake


Ingredients:
12 eggs
1/4 kg of butter
3/8 kg of sugar
2 sachet of vanilla
1/2 tbs of Ovalet
3 1/2 ons of wheat flour
5 tbs of milk powder
1 package of chocolate powder.
Steps:
Remove the egg yolk and albumen and place them in different bowl.
Mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and ovalet for 10 minutes.
Put the yolk into the dough and blend again at least 10 minutes.
Pour the wheat flour little by little into the dough and stir well.
Pour the milk powder little by little into the dough and stir well.
Pour the chocolate powder little by little into the dough and stir well.
Blend the albumen around 5 minutes.
Pour the albumen into the dough. Blend them around 10 minutes.
Prepare a baking pan lubricated with butter. Pour the dough. Put into the oven and bake around 40 minutes in 25 degrees celcius.
Lift the cake and put on the plate. Spread some sugar on it. The bolu cake is ready to serve.

How to Make Bolu Cake.


Ingredients:

12 eggs

1/4 kg of butter

3/8 kg of sugar

2 sachet of vanilla

1/2 tbs of Ovalet

3 1/2 ons of wheat flour

5 tbs of milk powder

1 package of chocolate powder.


Steps:

1. Remove the egg yolk and albumen and place them in different bowl.

2. Mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and ovalet for 10 minutes.

3. Put the yolk into the dough and blend again at least 10 minutes.

4. Pour the wheat flour little by little into the dough and stir well.

5. Pour the milk powder little by little into the dough and stir well.

6. Pour the chocolate powder little by little into the dough and stir well.

7. Blend the albumen around 5 minutes.

8. Pour the albumen into the dough. Blend them around 10 minutes.

9. Prepare a baking pan lubricated with butter. Pour the dough. Put into the oven and bake around 40 minutes in 25 degrees celcius.

10. Lift the cake and put on the plate. Spread some sugar on it. The bolu cake is ready to serve.

**How to Make a Cup of Coffee**

**Introduction:** Making a delicious cup of coffee is an easy process that can be done in a few simple steps. Here's how to do it.

**Materials Needed:**

- Freshly ground coffee beans

- Coffee maker

- Filter

- Water

- Coffee mug

- Spoon

- Optional: sugar, cream, or milk

**Procedure:**

1. **Prepare the Coffee Maker:**

- Open the top of the coffee maker.

- Place a clean filter into the filter basket.

2. **Measure Coffee:**

- For a standard cup of coffee, use one to two tablespoons of coffee grounds for every six ounces of water. Adjust based on your taste preferences.

3. **Add Coffee Grounds:**

- Put the measured coffee grounds into the filter.

4. **Add Water:**

- Pour cold, clean water into the coffee maker's water reservoir. Use the amount needed for the number of cups you want to make.

5. **Start Brewing:**

- Close the coffee maker's lid.

- Turn on the coffee maker. It will heat the water and drip it over the coffee grounds.

6. **Wait for Brewing:**

- Be patient. The coffee maker will take a few minutes to brew the coffee.

7. **Pour and Customize:**

- Once brewing is complete, carefully pour the hot coffee into your coffee mug.

- Add sugar, cream, milk, or any other additions to taste.

8. **Enjoy:**

- Stir your coffee and enjoy your freshly brewed cup of coffee!

Remember to be cautious when handling hot liquids, and ensure that your coffee maker is clean and in good working order for the best results.

2. 2carrots

3. 2 potatoes

4. 5 stalks celery

5. 1 red tomato

6. 1 cup of water

7. 2 spoons of cooking oil for sauteing

Spices:

1. 5 onions

2. 2 garlic

3. 3 candlenuts

5. ½ tablespoon of salt

6. ½ teaspoon of sugar

Procedures

1. Combine the chicken and water. Clean the chicken then drain it.

Making Compost Fertilizer

Compost is a fertilizer from the breakdown of animal and plant remains with the help of living organisms. Compost is a type of organic fertilizer.

Plants and gardens will become more fertile and make the soil more loose by using compost. Therefore, compost can be used as one of the best fertilizer choices.

Here's how to make your own compost easily:

Tool

Hoe

Gloves

Gunny sack

Shovel

Ingredients

Water

Cattle manure

Chopped straw

Leaf litter

husk charcoal

Sawdust

Sugar

EM4

Steps

First, prepare in advance the media to make compost that is safe from rain and sunlight.

Second, make a decomposer solution. The trick is to dissolve EM4 with sugar into water.

Third, make the first layer of compost. By stirring the mixture of manure and husk charcoal evenly. Then add the decomposer and mix until smooth.

Then create a second layer. Sprinkle leaf litter, sawdust, and chopped hay. Stir until smooth and water again with the decomposer.

Close tightly the fertilizer material with a burlap sack.

The next day, re-mix the ingredients until evenly distributed.

Check regularly in the morning and evening. The trick, put on gloves and put your hands into the compost mixture. If it feels hot and your hands can't handle the heat, it means that the fertilizer has not been finished and cannot be used.

Go back to check the fertilizer on the fourth day. Usually the fertilizer is ready to use on the fourth day.

If your hands are able to withstand the heat of the fertilizer mixture, it means that the fertilizer is ready to be used.

Homemade compost is finished.

How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Let’s learn how to make yummy scrambled eggs at your home with this easy recipe!

Ingredients:

2 eggs

Milk (if desired)

1 teaspoon of butter

A pinch of salt and pepper

Steps:

First, crack the eggs into a small bowl and add a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk until all well blended.

In a frying pan, add butter and let it melt.

Pour the eggs and milk, and for around 20 seconds, do not stir it.
