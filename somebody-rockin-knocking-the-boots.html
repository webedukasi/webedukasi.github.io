36. 1. I was sleeping when somebody knocked the door.A. I was slept when somebody knocked the door.B. Thad slept when somebody knocked the door.C. T have slept when somebody knocked the door.D. I have slept when somebody knock the door.Pakai rumus Past perfect​


Jawaban:

B. I had slept when somebody knocked the door.

Pembahasan

Pola Past Perfect:

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

(-) S + had not + V3 + Complement

(?) had + S + V3 + Complement

Keterangan pilihan ganda:

1. Past Continuous

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + V-ing + O

=> I was sleeping when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

Opsi ini salah bukan Past Perfect

A. Was + Verb 2

=> I was slept when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

Was + V2 = salah

B. Past Perfect

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

=> I had slept when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

C dan D. Present Perfect

(+) S + has/have + V3 + Complement

C. I have slept

D. I have slept

Penjelasan:

Ini kalimat Present Perfect, sedangkan yg diminta di soal adalah Past Perfect

Kesimpulan

Yang sesuai pola Past Perfect adalah B.

38. Do I hear something? "Yes, somebody . . . at the door!" Select one: a. knocks b. was knocking c. knocked d. has knocked e. is knocking


Penjelasan:

(Q) : "Do I hear something?"

(A) : "Yes, somebody knocks at the door!"

Dialog Q menyatakan interrogative sentence (kalimat tanya). Verb pada kalimat Q (hear) tanpa +s. Lalu pada kalimat tersebut terdapat "do" yang berada di depan subject. Ini termasuk simple present tense.

Jika ditanya dengan kalimat interogatif dengan menggunakan present, maka dijawab harus dengan menggunakan present. Kita tahu bentuk affirmative/positive adalah

S + V1 + s/es + O

yang memenuhi adalah :

Somebody + knocks + at the door

(somebody bisa diganti dengan it), dan posisi s/es juga didukung dengan subject kata ganti somebody dengan it.

Mapel : Bahasa Inggris

Kelas : 8

Materi : Simple Present Tense
