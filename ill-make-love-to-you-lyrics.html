9. If i (know)...............that you were ill,i(go)...........to see you


~ Answer:knew; would go

.~ If I knew that you were ill, I would go to see you. ~.

~ Introduction:

Conditional sentence artinya kalimat kondisional merupakan suatu pengibaratan terhadap suatu pekerjaan/keberadaan yang ditentang atau tidak sesuai oleh faktual, hanya diharapkan dapat terjadi menurut ekspektasi. Pada kalimat kondisional terdiri atas tiga tipe yaitu:

Conditional type 1—pengibaratan pada masa kini (simple present)Conditional type 2—pengibaratan pada masa lalu (simple past)Conditional type 3—pengibaratan pada peristiwa yang benar-benar sudah terjadi pada masa lalu (past perfect)

Serta ada conditional type 0 dimana ini menunjukkan suatu pengibaratan pada peristiwa faktual.

~ Discussion:

Berikut kunci untuk membuktikan tipe kalimat kondisional:

Type 1

if + simple present + simple future

Type 2

if + simple past + past future

Type 3

if + past perfect + past future perfect

Pernyataan if I ... that you were ill menunjukkan bentuk dari simple past maka if + simple past merupakan bentuk dari type 2 sehingga:

know menjadi simple past verb yaitu knewgo dengan dilengkapi would menjadi would go dinyatakan ke dalam bentuk past future

—> jawabannya: knew; would go

Task 3 Arrange these words to make good english

=> Susunlah kata-kata menjadi kalimat yg benar

No. 1 “Suggestion”

Digunakan untuk menyatakan saran untuk melakukan sesuatu. Rumus:

(+) S + should + V1 + O(-) S + should + not + V1 + O(?) should + S + V1 + O?

1.understand-you-lyrics-should-the

=> You should understand the lyric

Artinya: kamu harus memahami liriknya

No. 2 dan 3 “Obligation”

Digunakan untuk menyatakan larangan melakukan atau mewajibkan suatu tindakan. Rumus:

(+) S + must + V1 (-) S + must + not + V1 (?) must + S + V1?

2.littet-they-must not-

=> They must not litter

Artinya: Mereka tidak boleh membuang sampah sembarangan

3.low-keep-their voice-they-must-

=> They must keep their voice low

Notes for the Teacher

1. The song

Do a search on the Internet to find the song “I Believe I Can Fly” by R. Kelly. When you

search, be sure to include the title and the name of the artist.

2. Song background

R. Kelly is an R & B (rhythm and blues) singer and songwriter. He first sang in subway

stations in Chicago, Illinois, but now he is very famous. In fact, he was one of the most

successful R & B performers of the 1990s.

R. Kelly first sang this song, “I Believe I Can Fly,” in 1996. R. Kelly says that he wrote

the song for Michael Jordon, a very famous basketball player who jumps very high. The

song is inspirational -- it tries to help people succeed in life.

3. Grammar background

This song uses the modal verb can to expresses ability.

Write these examples on the board. Point out to students that can is followed by the

simple form of the verb.

A bird can fly, but a cow cannot fly.

A fish can swim, but a cow can’t swim.

Could is the past tense of can. In this song, it is used to expresses ability in the past.

Write these examples on the board:

When I was a very small baby, I couldn’t talk.

I am an adult now, so I can talk.

When I was a child, I couldn’t drive a car.

I am adult now, so I can drive a car.

You can find information on can and could on pages 354 and 362 in Chapter 12 of Basic

English Grammar
