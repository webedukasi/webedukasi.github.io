35. Mary, berumur 16 tahun dan empat kali umur saudara laki-laku Mary. Berapa umur Mary ketika umurnya dua kali umur saudara laki-laki nya?


umur Mary = 4 x umur adik
16 = 4 x umur adik
umur adik = 16 : 4 = 4 tahun

berapa umur Mary bila 2 kali umur adiknya?
(2 x usia adik) + usia Mary = (2 x 4) + 16  = 24 tahun

Misalkan, Mary = M, Saudara Mary = S

Mary berumur 16 tahun dan 4 kali dari umur saudaranya, persamaannya adalah :

[tex]$\begin{align} M&=4A\\16&=4A\\A&=\frac{16}{4}\\A&=4\ $tahun \end[/tex]

Umur Mary ketika umurnya 2 kali umur saudaranya, cari pertambahan umurnya :

[tex]$\begin{align} \frac{M+p}{A+p}&=\frac21\\\frac{4A+p}{A+p}&=2\\\frac{4\times4+p}{4+p}&=2\\16+p&=2p+8\\2p-p&=16-8\\p&=8\ $tahun \end[/tex]

Sehingga, umur Mary ketika umurnya 2 kali umur saudaranya adalah :

[tex]$\begin{align} M&=16+p\\&=16+8\\&=\boxed{24\ $tahun$} \end[/tex]

Passive Voice:

1. Fried chicken is cooked by Mary.

2. Fried chicken is being cooked by Mary.

3. Fried chicken will be cooked by Mary.

4. Fried chicken has been cooked by Mary.

5. Fried chicken was cooked by Mary.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat pasif dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3
