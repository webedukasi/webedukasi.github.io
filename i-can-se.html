- Can you speak English? = Bisakah Anda berbicara menggunakan bahasa Inggris?

- Yes, I do = Ya, ...

The " ... " is in accordance with the question in question. For example: Do you want ... (asking if he wants/mau). Do you understand the ... (ask him if he understands/mengerti).

- No, I don't = Tidak, saya tidak melakukannya/tidak, saya tidak mau.

- Yes, I can = Ya, saya bisa.

- Yes, he can = Ya, dia (lelaki) bisa.

40. Membuat 10 kalimat yang menggunakan kata I can dan I can not 


- Can

Dalam bahasa Indonesia berarti bisa. Contoh penggunaannya dalam kalimat adalah sebagai berikut.

1. I can train you how to operate this machine, if you want.

2. Now I can make delicious cookies after I read the recipes in this book.

3. I think I can repair this pen for you, wait a minute.

4. Can I borrow your book?

5. I can swim better than my father.

- Can't (singkatan dari cannot)

Dalam bahasa Indonesia berarti tidak bisa. Contoh penggunaannya dalam kalimat adalah sebagai berikut.

1. I can't help you. You should do it your self.

2. I'm so sorry, I think I can't go to you party. I need to pick my mom up at the airport.

3. I can't do the dance rehearsal; I just got an accident.

4. I can't see anything, it's very dark here.

5. I can't use my card; it's expired.
