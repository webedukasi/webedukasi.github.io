1. Berikan Contoh Affixes dalam bentuk 10 kata dan 10 Kalimat​


1. incapable

(the affix is the prefix in)

sentence: I am incapable of walking right now.

2. ex-President

(the affix is the prefix ex-)

sentence: The current President, Donald J. Trump, is taking advice from the ex-President, Barrack Obama.

3. laughing

(the affix is the suffix -ing)

sentence: laughing is good for your mental health.

4. dislike

(the affix is the prefix dis)

sentence: I dislike his virtues.

5. reflect

(the affix is the prefix re)

sentence: I often reflect on things I do.

6. unknown

(the affix is the prefix un)

sentence: an unknown caller called your phone today.

7. lovely

(the affix is the suffix -ly)

sentence: the flowers in your garden look lovely.

8. paved

(the affix is the suffix -ed)

sentence: normality is a paved road.

9. awfully

(the affix is the suffix -y)

sentence: to live would be an awfully big adventure.

10. foxes

(the affix is the suffix -es)

sentence: there were some foxes in your backyard yesterday.

7. contoh affixes, sufixes, prefixes digabung noun/verb/adverb etc bagaimana perubahannya ?


happy (adjective) + -ness (suffix) = happiness (noun)
happy + -ly (suffix) = happily (adverb)
drive (verb) + -er (suffix) = driver (noun)
un (prefix) + touch (verb) + able (suffix) = untouchable (adj)



8. What are the characteristics of derivational affixes? ​


Penjelasan:

Derivational affixes are subdivided into two groups: class-changing and class-maintaining. Class-changing derivational affixes change the word class to which they are added. Thus, the verb achieve and the suffix –able create an adjective achievable.

Jawaban:

A derivational affix is an affix by means of which one word is formed (derived) from another.

18. contoh affixes,praffixes,saffixes itu apa sebutkan minimal 9 contoh ya ..... :) dan terdapat synomim dan antonymnya ya


Answer:

Affix is several joint letters called morphemes (like -ed, -es, -ness, un-, pre-) which are attached either before a word or after a word. Affix before a word is called prefix, while the one after the word is called suffix. Affixes can also be derivational like -ity, -ness or inflectional -ed, -s. Here are the nine example of them.
Affix 
- faded / antennas / benefactor / mouthful / enlighten / enrichment / impossible / likely / likelihood.
Suffix (after the words)
sadness / happiness / stupidity / creativity / acting / lying / careful / careless / normalization
Prefix (before the words)
undo/ undress / disengage / disarm / dispossess / preteach / endanger / encourage
For the synonym and antonym, you can see it from the suffix parts. 

Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kata kunci: preffix, suffix, word
