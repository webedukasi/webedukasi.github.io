Penjelasan:

Asking and Giving Suggestion

JAWABAN:

Asking for suggestion (Meminta saran)

What do you think I should do? (Menurut mu apa yang harus saya lakukan?)

Do you have any suggestions for me? (Apakah kamu punya saran untuk saya?)

Please tell me what should I do? (Tolong beri tahu saya apa yang harus saya lakukan?)

What is your recommendation about this? (Apa rekomendasi mu tentang ini?)

Can you tell me what I should do? (Bisakah kamu memberi tahu saya apa yang harus saya lakukan?)

Giving suggestion (Memberi saran)

I think you should/ ought to go to the doctor. (Saya pikir kamu harus pergi ke dokter)

You had better tell the truth to your parents. (Kamu sebaiknya mengatakan yang sebenarnya kepada orang tua mu)

You need to change your bad habits. (Kamu perlu mengubah kebiasaan buruk mu)

I think you should choose this way. (Saya pikir kamu harus memilih cara ini)

I suggest you to go and meet your teacher for asking her advice. (Saya sarankan kamu untuk pergi dan menemui guru mu untuk meminta nasihatnya)

 

PEMBAHASAN:

Asking and giving suggestion merupakan ungkapan-ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan bagaimana cara meminta dan memberi saran kepada orang lain.

20. what is the meaning of "down on my knees" ?


Answer:

The meaning of idioms 'down on my knees' is 'to beg'. Such idiom is used to express the emphasis when saying a request. Here is the possible example.

'Please, sweetie, I am down on my knees to ask for your forgiveness for the mistake I have done.'

In literal meaning, such idioms can be considered as the kneeling position.

'Get down on your knees or I'll kick your legs with this long stick!!'
