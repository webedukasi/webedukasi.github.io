sebelum dibuat kalimat, hendaknya memahami struktur simple present tense. (positive, negative, interrogative)

(+) S + V1 +s/es + Object
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + Object
(?) Do/does + subject + V1

Olivia dan sahabatnya suka permen
(+) Olivia and her bestfriend likes candy
(-) Olivia and her bestfriend do not like candy
(?) Do Olivia and her bestfriend likes candy?
