7. My little brother ........(not sleep)now


Jawaban:

My little brother Isn't sleep now

Penjelasan:

isn't stands for is not. isn't is also the negative form of is. isn't is a form of to be which is usually used in present tense forms and is only used in singular subjects (he, she, it) or third person singular.

while aren't (are not) is used for negative sentences in nominal sentences with the subjects You, They, and We. Example:

- You aren't a student.

- Aren't (are not) used for negative sentences in the "Continuous Tense" followed by V-ing with the subject You, They, and We.

Example:

- We aren't playing football.

I hope this helps.
