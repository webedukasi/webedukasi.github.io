In any kind of writing, you always need to write introduction. It's the essential part of the writing. The main purpose of introduction is mainly to state the reason for you to write, normally on business letters like inquiry, complaint or confirmation. The other functions of it is to give a glimpse of issue based on our point of view, for essay or articles. 8. what is the generic structure of introduction yourself!​


Self introduction atau perkenalan diri biasanya dilakukan di awal pertemuan kelompok. Itu bisa dilakukan di seminar, konferensi, grup komunitas atau di awal pertemuan kelas baru. Tujuannya bisa bermacam - macam.

Pembahasan

Generic structure of introducing self :

Stating your name clearlyStating your origin, address, or your position of organisationSharing your particular skillSharing your passion, goal, or interestStating your personal details, such as hobbies, favorite food, etc

Here is the example of self introduction in the class:

Hello everyone, my name is Kiara Larasati but you can call me Kiara.

I am from Tulungagung but my family and I move to Surabaya recently. I live in Jalan Hayam Wuruk, 11 Surabaya. When I was kid, I join a dance academy   near my house so I can dance a little bit. I am really interested in dance and music, that is why I will continue to study them in this city. My hobby is reading and cooking. I like to make cookies and bread with my mother.

That's all about me. Thank you for giving me a chance to introduce myself.

1. What is self introduction?

A self-introduction explains who you are, what you do and what others need to know about you. You should provide a self-introduction any time you meet someone new and don't have a third party to introduce you. Offer a self-introduction when you are: Beginning an interview.

Atau simplenya introduction bisa kita artikan sebagai "Perkenalan".

2. What are the expressions of self introduction (formal)?

- I would like to introduce myself. My name is…

- May I introduce myself. My name is…

- Let me introduce myself. My name is…

- Would you mind if I introduce myself. My name is…

Perkenalan (introducing) formal ini biasanya digunakan pada acara atau forum resmi, seperti perkenalan pada acara seminar dimana diacara tersebut terdapat tokoh atau orang penting

3. What are the expressions of self introduction (informal)?

- Hi, Good morning. I’m Shasya from Jakarta

- Hey bro/sis, how do you do? I’m Aulia, nice to see you.

Perkenalan (introducing) formal ini biasanya digunakan pada acara atau forum tidak resmi, seperti perkenalan pada saat ditempat umum bertemu dengan saudara teman atau teman baru. 

4. What are the responses of self introduction (formal)?

- I am very pleased to meet you.

- Glad to meet you too.

5. What are the responses of self introduction (informal)?

- I'm so happy to talk with you

- Lovely to meet you too
