Jawaban:

1. You CAN BE MADE [By me] to love me

2. Your family HAD BEEN INVITED [By us] to our party

Penjelasan:

1. Modal (can)

Active:

(+) S + CAN + VERB 1 + O

= i can make you to love me

Passive:

(+) S + CAN BE + VERB 3 + By (agent)

= You + CAN BE + MADE [By me] to love me

2. Past Perfect

(+) S + HAD + VERB 3 + O

= we had invited your family to our party

Passive:

(+) S + HAD BEEN + VERB 3 + By

= Your family + HAD BEEN + INVITED + [By us] to our party
