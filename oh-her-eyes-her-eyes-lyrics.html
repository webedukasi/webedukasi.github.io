25. The doctor is checking her eyes. (make into passive sentence)​


Jawaban

The doctor is checking her eyes. (make it into a passive sentence!)

Her eyes are being checked by the doctor.Penjelasan

Passive voice adalah kalimat yang subjeknya diberi suatu tindakan ataupun diberi kata kerja. Rumus untuk kalimat pasif dalam Present Continuous Tense adalah:

Object + am/is/are + being + Verb 3 + by Object Pronoun.

Contoh:

The grass is being cut by her.The floor is usually being cleaned by my brother.

Rumus di atas berlaku untuk transitive verb, dimana memang kata kerja tersebut yang umum dipasifkan.
