23. 3. The poison took ... (instant / instantly) effect.​


ADJECTIVES & ADVERBS

The Answer:

The poison took instant effect.

Explanation

Kata yang tepat untuk mengisi titik-titik pada kalimat soal adalah instant. Ini karena kata instant merupakan sebuah kata sifat (adjective) yang fungsinya untuk mendeskripsikan kata benda (noun).

-

Adjectives (dalam Bahasa Indonesia adalah kata sifat) merupakan salah satu kelas kata dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk mendeskripsikan hanya kata benda.

Adjectives dalam Bahasa Inggris dapat dikelompokkan berdasarkan bentuknya sebagai berikut.

Positives, misalnya: happy, glad, good, energetic, instant, dll.Determiners, misalnya: the, an, a, some, a few, a little, much, many, dll.Comparisons, misalnya: more, most, last, as much as, better, faster, dll.Numbers, misalnya: one, first, two, second, third, three, fourth, dll.Attributive Nouns, misanya: telephone number, assistant manager, mentor helper, dll.Participles, misalnya: boring, bored, excited, interesting, well-built, dll.Infinitives, misalnya: It's time for you to wake up, dll.

-

Adverb (dalam Bahasa Indonesia adalah kata keterangan) adalah salah satu kelas kata dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk mendeskripsikan kata sifat, kata kerja, dan kata keterangan lain.

Ingat bahwa kata keterangan tidak pernah digunakan untuk mendeskripsikan kata benda. Pada kalimat soal, kata yang tepat untuk diisi di titik-titiknya adalah sebuah kata sifat karena kata tersebut akan mendeskripsikan kata benda setelah kata sifat tersebut.

Kata benda yang dideskripsikan dalam kalimat soal ialah effect. Karena instant merupakan kata sifat, maka kata sifat tersebutlah yang mendeskripsikan kata benda effect.

Dengan demikian, The poison took instant effect adalah jawaban dari soal tersebut.

-

Kalimat di soal juga dapat dimodifikasi agar kata keterangan instantly dapat digunakan, yaitu:

The poison instantly took effect. (kata keterangan instantly mendeskripsikan kata kerja took.)The poison took effect instantly. (kata keterangan instantly mendeskripsikan kata kerja utama dalam kalimat, yaitu took.)

24. Do people have the same life style from time to time


Jawaban:

No, people's lifestyles can vary greatly over time. Lifestyle is a broad term that encompasses many aspects of an individual's life, including their habits, behaviors, daily routines, and overall way of living. These factors can be influenced by various factors such as age, culture, personal beliefs, socioeconomic status, and life events.

For example, a person's lifestyle in their 20s may differ significantly from their lifestyle in their 50s or 60s. In their 20s, they may be more focused on their career, social life, and personal growth, while in their 50s or 60s, they may prioritize their health, family, and retirement planning.

Moreover, changes in technology, societal norms, and economic conditions can also affect people's lifestyles. For instance, the rise of technology has greatly impacted the way people work, communicate, and spend their leisure time.

In summary, people's lifestyles are constantly changing and evolving over time, influenced by various factors.

We Don’t Have the Same Amount Time in a Day

We all have the same 24 hours in a day.

You’ve heard this phrase a thousand times in your lifetime. It has become a mantra to motivate people to make good use of clock time. One of the few absolute truths in which we believe.

But is it true?

Have you ever asked yourself that?

In this first issue of TheShift, I aim to demonstrate why we don’t have the same amount of time in our days. Then, share a holistic approach to organize life around time, for a more productive and fulfilling life.

But, what is the definition of time?

The Oxford Languages defines time :

First, as the indefinite continued progress of existence and events in the past, present, and future regarded as a whole.

Second, as a point of time as measured in hours and minutes past midnight or noon.

You’ll agree with me. Whenever we say “we all have the same amount of time in a day”, we are referring to the second part of the above definition. Let’s shift gear a bit and consider part one. Time is the indefinite progress of existence and events in the past, present, and future.

Each of our past, present, and future events affect the progress of existence. Hence, our sense of time affects us more than time on the clock. A point of view that’s also shared by philosopher Immanuel Kant.

26. A rhyme is when....... A. a word from two words or phrases ends in the same sounds B. two words from phrases begins with the same letter C. two words from two line ends with different sounds D. a word from two lines are the same


Answer:

To answer a question which doesn’t have any further explanation or given clue, we don’t have any other choice except collecting some information regarding to the keyword. We can get the information about the keyword by looking for it on the internet. The keyword is rhyme.

Rhyme is a repetition of similar sounds at the end of each line in a poem. The example of rhyme:

Humpty Dumpty sat on a wall,
Humpty Dumpty had a great fall.

From the explanation above we can conclude that both lines are ended with the similar sound.

So the best answer is: A. a word from two words or phrases ends in the same sounds
