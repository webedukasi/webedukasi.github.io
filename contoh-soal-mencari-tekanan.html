Elderly Vulnerable to Experience Severe Pain When Affected by Covid-19

As we know, the transmission of Covid-19 knows no age limit. Anyone can get infected. However, the elderly (elderly) are more prone to suffer serious illness. Especially people with pre-existing medical conditions, such as asthma, diabetes, heart disease, or high blood pressure.

The elderly are a vulnerable group because the immune system decreases with age. As you age, your body will experience various decreases due to the aging process. Almost all organ function and movement decreased. This is followed by a decrease in immunity as a body protector that does not work as well as when he was young. This is why the elderly are susceptible to various diseases, including Covid-19.

A weak immune system, not to mention the illness that was suffered previously, can increase the risk of death from Covid-19 in the elderly.
