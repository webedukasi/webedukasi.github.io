Jawaban :

X : Why don't you do your homework?

Y : I'm too lazy to finish it

Penggunaan kata "too" lalu diikuti kata sifat ( lazy ) menyatakan kemampuan yang melebihi batas. Ini menjelaskan bahwa rasa malas nya berlebihan dan bermakna negatif dimana adanya ke tidak mampuan mengerjakan PR sama sekali.

X : Why don't you do your homework?

Y : I'm tired enough .

Penggunaan kata "enough" lalu diawali kata sifat ( tired ) menjelaskan bahwa  kemampuan yang  hanya seperlunya saja. Jadi, dalam artian bahwa meskipun lelah tetapi ada usaha untuk mengerjakan PR nya walaupun hanya seperlunya saja mengerjakannya.

11. When do you need to use "too" and "enough”?​


Jawaban:

Too and enough can modify both nouns, adjectives, and adverbs.

Too indicates that there is too much of a quality, or too much or too many of some object.

Enough means that there is no need for more of a quality or object

semoga membantu. (^_^)

When do you need to use "too" and "enough"?

Too : You use too when there is a subject + have/has + Word work + too + much/many + object.Example: Joe have eaten too much sugar.

Enough : You use enough when there something that is limited.Example : There is enough bread for the stock.

40. I usually get up early in the morning, bit my little brother . . .a. Is notb. Do notc. Are notd. Does notbeserta penjelasan!!​


Jawaban:

d. Does Not

Penjelasan:

Because if you use Is not or Do not or Are not, it won't make sense.

Jawaban:

D. Does not.

Penjelasan:

Does not biasanya digunakan pada suatu aktifitas (Misal: Ari Does not come to school).

Do not biasanya digunakan pada kalimat (Misal: Do not touch the painting!).

Are not digunakan pada kalimat / pertanyaan yang ada 'are' nya juga. (Misal: Are this dogs sleeping? No, they are not sleeping).

Is not digunakan pada kalimat yang ada 'Is' nya juga. (Misal: Is your dog sleeping? No, my dog is not sleeping).
