26. I an paid the food in dollars ubah menjadi active voice​


Jawaban:

The sentence in passive voice is

"The food was paid in dollars".

To change it to active voice, we need to make the subject of the sentence the doer of the action.

Therefore, the active voice sentence would be: "I paid the food in dollars".

In the active voice sentence, the subject "I" performs the action of paying the food in dollars. The object "the food" receives the action. The sentence is clearer and more direct in active voice.

36. On a day, the exchange rate between the Singapore dollars (SGD) and the US dollars (USD) is 1 SGD = 0.816 USD, correct to 3 decimal between the places. The exchange rate Australian dollars (AUD) and the US dollars is 1 AUD = 1.073 USD, correct to 3 decimal places. (a) Find the exchange rate, correct to 3 decimal places, between Singapore dollars and Australian dollars in AUD/SGD. (b) Mr Lim has a son studying in a university in Sydney. He has to remit 25 000 AUD to him. Find the required amount of Singapore dollars correct to the nearest dollar.​


Jawaban:

(a) To find the exchange rate between Singapore dollars and Australian dollars in AUD/SGD, we can use the given exchange rates:

1 SGD = 0.816 USD

1 AUD = 1.073 USD

To convert from SGD to AUD, we can first convert SGD to USD, and then convert USD to AUD:

1 SGD = 0.816 USD

1 SGD/0.816 USD = 1.22549019608 SGD/USD (using the reciprocal of 0.816)

1 AUD = 1.073 USD

1.073 USD/1 AUD = 0.93121440823 AUD/USD (using the reciprocal of 1.073)

Now, to get the exchange rate between SGD and AUD, we can divide the AUD/USD exchange rate by the SGD/USD exchange rate:

0.93121440823 AUD/USD ÷ 1.22549019608 SGD/USD ≈ 0.759 AUD/SGD

Therefore, the exchange rate between Singapore dollars and Australian dollars in AUD/SGD is approximately 0.759, correct to 3 decimal places.

(b) To find the required amount of Singapore dollars to remit 25,000 AUD, we can use the exchange rate we found in part (a):

1 AUD = 0.759 SGD (approximately)

Therefore, 25,000 AUD is equivalent to:

25,000 AUD × 0.759 SGD/AUD ≈ 18,975 SGD

Rounding to the nearest dollar, the required amount of Singapore dollars is 18,975 SGD.
