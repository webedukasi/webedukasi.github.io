10. Help English Class 10❗❗​


Jawaban:

1. We have lived in this house since 2005.

2. Paul has got/gotten married a week ago.

3. We have just completed Unit 1.

4. I have been here for two days.

5. I have not finished reading this book yet.

Penjelasan:

Present prefect tense --> menunjukkan aktivitas yang terjadi di masa lampau (tapi waktunya tidak spesifik), yang berhubungan di masa kini/hasilnya bisa dilihat di masa kini.

Penanda waktu: just, yet, since, for, etc.

Rumus:

(+) S + have/has + V3 + O.

(-) S + have/has + not + V3 + O.

(?) Have/has + S + V3 + O?
