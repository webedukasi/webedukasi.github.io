8. a "If you'd never looked my way I would've stayed on my knees And I for sure never would've danced with the devil" apakah realita/fakta dari pengandaian di atas?


Terjemahan:

Jika kamu tak pernah memandang ke arahku,

Aku akan tetap berlutut,

dan Aku yakin bahwa aku tak akan pernah menari dengan iblis.

Penjelasan:

Makna tersirat dari pengandaian lirik di atas adalah bahwa seandainya seseorang yang memikat hati kita tak pernah menaruh harapan pada kita, tentunya kita akan tetap menunduk/tak membuka hati kita untuk dirinya sehingga kita tak akan jatuh dalam kegelapan (sedih/putus asa).

Jawaban:

Conditionals:

The reality is that the sentences are just hypothetical and unreal because they show an impossible condition and its probable result in the past that don't even exist in reality.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Conditionals (pengandaian / bersyarat) tipe-3 dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Realita / Fakta dari kalimat pengandaian pada soal adalah bahwa kalimat-kalimat tersebut hanya bersifat hipotetis dan tidak nyata karena menunjukkan kondisi yang tidak mungkin beserta kemungkinan hasilnya di masa lalu yang bahkan tidak ada dalam kenyataan.

Hipotesis adalah penjelasan berupa pengandaian imajinatif yang diajukan untuk suatu kondisi.

Conditional Type-3:

If+ subject + had + V3/past perfect Tense + subject + would have + V3/past perfect Tense

4. The song is called "Dear Mama" by Tupac Shakur. The message of the song is about the rapper's love and appreciation for his mother and how she has always been there for him through thick and thin. The song is a tribute to all mothers who have sacrificed so much for their children.

5. The verse is the part of the song where the lyrics are spoken rather than sung. In "Dear Mama," the verses are the parts where Tupac raps about his childhood and his mother's struggles.

The chorus is the part of the song that is repeated after each verse. In "Dear Mama," the chorus is the part where Tupac sings "Mama I love you, Mama I care, Mama I love you, Mama my friend."

6. The line "Every other day I crossed the line I didn't mean to be so bad" is an example of hyperbole, which is a figure of speech that uses exaggeration to make a point. In this case, Tupac is exaggerating how often he got into trouble as a child.
