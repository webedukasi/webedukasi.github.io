5. 1 They don't ride their bikes very often (+) & (?) 2 Does the photographer take nice photos? (+) & (-)3 Emily and Anna are swimming right now. (-) (?) 4 mr. smith is fixing his washing machine now (-) (?) ​


1. They don't ride their bikes very often

(+) They ride their bikes very often

(?) Do they ride their bikes very often?

2. Does the photographer take nice photos?

(+) the photographer takes nice photo

(–) the photographer doesn't take nice photo

PENJELASAN ( NO.1 DAN 2 )

→ Rumus Simple Present Tense

(+) S + V1(-s/es)

(–) S + Do/Does + not + V1

(?) Do/Does + S + V1

note :

(-s/es) dan does hanya digunakan she, he, it

__________________________

3. Emily and Anna are swimming right now.

(–) Emily and Anna are not swimming right now.

(?) Are Emily and Anna swimming right now?

4. mr. smith is fixing his washing machine now

(–) Mr. Smith is not fixing his waxing machine now

(?) Is Mr. Smith fixing his waxing machine now?

PENJELASAN ( NO. 3 DAN 4 )

→ Rumus Present Continuous Tense

(+) S + am/are/is + V1-ing

(–) S + am/are/is + not + V1-ing

(?) Am/are/is + S + V1-ing
