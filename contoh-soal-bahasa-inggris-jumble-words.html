21. Apakah yang dimaksud dengan jumble word, jumble sentence, close text? Jelaskan dalam bahasa inggris


jumble word is the sequence of words from a randomized sentence
jumble sentence is writing skills that train students to compose a random sentence into a unified paragraph or text
cloze test is an exercise, test, or assessment consisting of a portion of language with certain items, words, or signs removed (cloze text), where the participant is asked to replace the missing language item.
