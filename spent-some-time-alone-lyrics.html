22. 2. It wasn't my first time to go alone. (+)............. (?)​


Jawaban:

(+) It was my first time to go alone

(?) Was it my first time to go alone?

Penjelasan:

Soal ini merupakan past tense

Rumus past tense :

(+) S + tobe (was/were) + O/C

(-) S + tobe (was/were) + not + O/C

(?) tobe (was/were) + S + O/C?

Mohon dijadikan jawaban tercerdas yaa
