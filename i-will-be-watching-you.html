Here are some future continuous tense sentences:

Right now, I am watching TV. Tomorrow at this time, I will be watching TV as well. Tomorrow after school, I will go to the beach. I am going on a dream vacation to Tahiti. While you will be doing paperwork and talk to annoying customers on the phone. We will be hiding when Tony arrive at his surprise party tomorrow. We work out at the fitness center every day after work. If you will come over while we work out, we will not be able to let you into the house.

Pembahasan:

Future continuous tense is sentence form which used to expresses action at a particular moment in the future. Future continuous tense merupakan pola kalimat dalam bahasa inggris yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan kejadian yang pasti akan terjadi di masa yang akan datang. Bentuk future continuous tense tersusun dari dua unsur yaitu simple future tense dari kata kerja to be + present participle (kata dasar + ing). Adapun rumus dari future continuous tense adalah sebagai berikut:

(+) Subject + wi/shal + be + verb-ing + complement

(-) Subject + sill/shall + not + be + verb-ing + complement

(?) Will/ shal + subject + be + verb-ing + complement

She will be taking an exam at 2 p.m. tomorrow, so don't call her then. I will be waiting for you when you arrive tonight. 2. They'll be coming to visit us next week. (+) Subject + will be + Verb-ing (-) Subject + will + not + be + Verb-ing (?) Will be + Subject + Verb-ing? He'll be coming to the contest, I expect. I hope we won't be doing much on Sunday. 8. Will you be staying here long? 9. Will you be coming at 7 0'clock tonight? I'll probably be eating dinner. 10. Will she be going to the party tonight? C. Let's Write and Practice​


Jawaban:

Sure! Here is an exercise to practice writing and using the future continuous tense:

Exercise: Write five sentences using the future continuous tense.

1. This time next month, I will be traveling to Europe with my family.

2. At 8 o'clock tomorrow evening, they will be attending a concert in the city.

3. By the end of the year, she will be completing her master's degree.

4. Next summer, we will be exploring the beautiful beaches of Hawaii.

5. While you are studying for your exams, I will be preparing dinner for us.

Now, let's practice by turning the following sentences into the future continuous tense:

1. He is reading a book.

- He will be reading a book.

2. I am working on a project.

- I will be working on a project.

3. They are playing soccer in the park.

- They will be playing soccer in the park.

4. She is cooking dinner for her family.

- She will be cooking dinner for her family.

5. We are watching a movie at the cinema.

- We will be watching a movie at the cinema.

Remember to follow the pattern for the future continuous tense: subject + will be + verb-ing. Keep practicing to become more comfortable with using this tense correctly.
