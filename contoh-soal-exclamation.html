13. Jelaskan pengertian macam- macam dari language features berikut. - Exclamation - Question - adjective phrase - Prepositional phrase - Simple present tense


Jawaban:

Exclamation -

A sentence that makes a statement that conveys strong emotion or excitement.

Question -

A question is an utterance which typically functions as a request for information,

Adjective Phrase -

An adjective phrase is a phrase the head of which is an adjective, e.g. fond of steak, very happy, quite upset about it, etc. The adjective can initiate the phrase, conclude the phrase, or appear in a medial position

Prepositional Phrase -

An adpositional phrase, in linguistics, is a syntactic category that includes prepositional phrases, postpositional phrases, and circumpositional phrases

Simple Present Tense -

The simple present, present simple or present indefinite is one of the verb forms associated with the present tense in modern English. It is commonly referred to as a tense, although it also encodes certain information about aspect in addition to present time.

28. Teman" minta tolong yaa barikan contoh contoh kalimat exclamation


1. What + a/an + adjective + noun

Penggunaan exclamation menggunakan “What … “ diikuti oleh kata a/an untuk benda yang bermakna tunggal atau singular, misal “What a beautiful girl!”. Untuk benda yang bermakna  jamak atau banyak, atau benda yang tak bisa dihitung atau uncountable noun, kata a/an dihilangkan, misal “What beautiful ladies!”, “What nice coffee!”, dan sebagainya. Selain itu, kita juga bisa menggunakan “What …” tanpa kata sifat, misalnya “What a fool!”

2.2. What + Noun + Subject + Verb

Pada bentuk exclamation ini, “What …” diikuti Noun atau kata benda dalam bahasa Inggris, Subject dan Verb. Sebagai contoh yaitu “What silly things she made!”.

32. Exclamation Kalimat Eksklamasi
