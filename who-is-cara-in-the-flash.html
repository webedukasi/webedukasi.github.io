2. What is the moral lesson that you get from the movie "the flash"​


Life is about progress. Those who accept their mistakes and move forwards are those who succeed.

» PEMBAHASAN

Barry Allen is a human, and he makes mistakes too. He might have unmatched abilities compared to the rest of humanity, but therein the problem lies - initially he thought that by being fast he would be able to solve all of his problems and succeed at life. The opposite occurs. He gradually begins to realize that this thought-process is mistaken and that each circumstance faced in life will present a new problem, one where he will not be able to always make the correct decisions. An example of this at the end of Season 2, when he decides to run back in time to the night of his mother’s murder to take preventative measures which he thinks will grant (See the joke there? As in Grant Gustin? Never mind) him inner peace, albeit this is later revealed to be a disastrous decision once he returns to Earth-1 to find out that he has brought some of Flashpoint’s metas with him, including The Rival and especially Dr Alchemy, who serves the self-proclaimed God of Speed, Savitar. You see here, Barry thought everything would be just the way it had been once he returned from Flashpoint. However, all actions have consequences. Now, Cisco’s brother Dante is dead, Iris doesn’t talk to Joe any longer and as mentioned above, a mysterious Dr Alchemy has entered the scene and is causing unparalleled levels of chaos by granting certain individuals inhuman abilities. Barry is now not only distanced from his friends but has just allowed entry of who is the possibly the most powerful villain yet - one who moves fast enough to be invisible to ordinary humans. Imagine what Barry feels. His world is crumbling around him yet thankfully, he allows his sense of moral and social responsibility to persuade him to accept his situation and act on it, even if everyone is not exactly the same as they were pre-Flashpoint. We, as the viewers, are essentially taught to take responsibility for our actions and accept the outcomes. That is what we are instructed on a personal level. The underlying message through all of the seasons though is that we all must work together in order to make a positive impact on the world, and that being static to the change around us will only ruin us.

Reading Comprehension:

1. What is the report about?

The report is about flood.

2. What are the natural causes of flood?

The most common natural cause is because of the over capacity of the body of water, e.g. river or lake. As a result some of the water flows outside of the body of water. A flood from sea may be caused by a heavy storm, a high tide, a tsunami, or a combination the three.

3. What are the human causes of flood?

A flood can also be caused by blocked sewage pipes and waterways, such as the Jakarta flood. The sewage pipes and waterways are blocked because people keep throwing rubbish into the river. That's a flood caused by humans.

4. What is periodic flood?

Periodic floods occur naturally on many rivers, forming an area known as the flood plain. These river floods usually result from heavy rain, sometimes combined with melting snow, which causes the rivers to overflow their banks.

5. What is flash flood?​

A flood that rises and falls rapidly with little or no advance warning is called a flash flood. This flood usually occurs because of heavy rain in a very long time period.
