12. I __(look) Out of the Window last nightbecause I __ (Hear) some noises​


I looked out of the window last night because I heard some noises.

PEMBAHASANSimple Past Tense

Yaitu bentuk tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi pada masa lalu.

RUMUS

Positive Sentences:

Subject + Verb 2 + ObjectSubject + was/were + Object

Negative Sentences:

Subject + did + not + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + was/were + not + Object

Interrogative Sentences:

Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + ?Was/were + Subject + Object + ?

Contoh:

(+) I bought Indonesian foods when I went there about a week ago.

(-) I didn't buy Indonesian foods when I went there about a week ago.

(?) Did I buy Indonesian foods when I went there about a week ago?

~ Based on Question

I (look) out of the Window last night because I (hear) some noises.

➡ I looked out of the window last night because I heard some noises.

Aku mengintip keluar jendela semalam karena aku mendengar suara.

~ Verbs on Question

Irregular Verbs

Yaitu kata kerja tidak beraturan. Bentuk past tense dan past participle-nya tidak ditandai dengan adanya akhiran -d atau -ed.

Hear - Heard - Heard

Mendengar

Regular Verbs

Yaitu kata kerja beraturan. Bentuk past tense dan past participle-nya ditandai dengan adanya -d atau -ed.

Look - Looked - Looked

Melihat, mengintip
