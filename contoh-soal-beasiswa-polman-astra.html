Soal Psikotes Tes Sinonim

Dalam kategori soal ini, diharuskan memilih kata yang memiliki arti sama atau paling mendekati. Perhatikan contoh di bawah ini.

1) KALEIDOSKOP

A. Seragam

B. Berwarna-warni

C. Fleksibel

D. Ringkasan

E. Kalender

Pembahasan:

Kaleidoskop aneka peristiwa yang telah terjadi yang disajikan secara singkat. Jawaban: D

2) MOBILITAS

A. Kendaraan

B. Motor

C. Gerak

D. Diam

E. Transportasi

Pembahasan:

Mobilitas kesiapsiagaan untuk bergerak; gerakan berpindah-pindah. Jawaban: C

3) ACUAN

A. Pegangan

B. Pedoman

C. Pemacu

D. Rujukan

E. Dasar

Pembahasan:

Acuan = menunjuk (kepada); Merujuk. Jawaban D

7) ... : WISUDA= PERTUNANGAN: ...

A. toga-cincin

B. gelar-pelaminan

C. berhasil-cinta

D. sarjana - mempelai

E kuliah-pernikahan

Pembahasan:

Pembahasan: Wisuda adalah tujuan akhir dari kuliah, pernikahan tujuan dari pertunangan. Jawaban: E

8) SARUNG TANGAN: ... = ... :BAKTERIOLOG.

A. steril-stetoskop

B. ahli forensik-mikroskop

C. dokter-apoteker

D. kotor-kuman

E. bersih-bakteri

Pembahasan:

Sarung tangan adalah alat yang digunakan oleh ahli forensik, mikroskop adalah alat yang digunakan oleh bakteriolog. Jawaban: B

9) SAKIT :... = .... :MAKAN

A. dokter-sakit

B. makan-lemah

C. istirahat-lapar

D. berobat gemuk

E. pasien-gizi

Pembahasan:

Jika sakit maka harus istirahat, jika lapar maka harus makan. Jawaban: C

15. apa saja informasi baru tentang usung keberagaman , peserta karnaval Polman Tampilkan budaya Nusantara ​


Jawaban:

Karnaval budaya yang mengusung tema keberagaman budaya nusantara dalam rangka peringatan Hari Kemerdekaan Ke-72 Republik Indonesia berlangsung meriah di Polewali Mandar, Sulawesi Barat, Minggu (20/8/2017).

Ribuan anak-anak dari berbagai sekolah TK dan SD di Polewali mandar tampil cantik dan gagah dengan warna warni budaya nusantara.

Beragam kesenian tradisional dan pakaian adat nusantara yang dikenakan para peserta membuat mereka tampak bangga dan senang menjadi sorotan ribuan pasang mata di sepanjang rute karnaval yang dilalui peserta.

Karnaval budaya yang menampilkan warna warni budaya nusantara sebagai salah satu kekayaan budaya ini dipusatkan di desa Tonyamang Polewali Mandar.

Peserta karnaval yang mempertontonkan keindahan warna budaya nusantara berkeliling dari lapangan sepak bola Tonyaman, melintasi jalan Andi Depu, kemudian berkeliling kota hingga akhirnya finish di SDN 015.

Dalam pawai karnaval ini, para peserta menggunakan berbagai pakaian adat seperti adat bugis, mandar, jawa, toraja, mamasa dan pakaian adat nusantara laionnya.

Selain pakaian adat, ada pula peserta yang menggunakan kostum seperti tentara, polisi, nelayan dan petani.

Jawaban:

I am writing to apply for the ICCR scholarship. My name is Rehita Hasian Batubara. I graduated from English Literature in September 2010 from State University of Medan, Faculty of Languages and Arts in Medan, North Sumatra.

Traffic jams are a terrible problem in the capital city of my country of origin, Jakarta of Indonesia. So far, there has not been any solution to handle this long-lasting problem.

I have big interest and passion in foreign languages. Since i was a little girl, i have been shifting from one city to another city with different culture background. Those cities have different local languages. I learned the local languages autodidact. From those local languages, I learned they have their own pattern and are unique linguistically. English has been my favorite subject since I got English on 3rd grade. My highest score would be English. That’s why i chose English Literature major for my Bachelor Degree. It turned out that I learned Linguistics more than Literature. Linguistics has drawn my attention. Those forming of sounds, words, and sentences were interesting. So were the connection of languages with circumstances, brain, etc. So with these reasons above I am giving a try for myself to be part of the General Cultural Scholarship Scheme Program.

I believe by applying to this program will certainly change my life as I put myself in a more challenging environment and leave my comfort zone in Jakarta. I always want to strive for the best out of myself and learn for more knowledge as much as I can. Since I was at school and university or even in a working environment, I have been very active in some organizations and extra-curricular activities. I like to be socialized and take part as a leader or other important roles. Furthermore, I pursued my Bachelor Degree in Malaysia which has multi-cultural and international environment which forced me to be independent and also team-player at the same time. I am very enthusiastic with the program that correlates me to broader networking and opens more opportunities for my future. There are a lot of questions in my head about how the knowledge shared and intimacy among students and lectures and also companies are done in European countries where mostly best leaders around the world are made. It was my dream to experience it directly and I want to realize it through this program.
