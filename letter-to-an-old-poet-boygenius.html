32. Change the following into the passive 1. teacher is writing a letter to his old friend in malaysia


Jawaban:

A letter is being written by Teacher to his old friend in Malaysia

PEMBAHASAN

Teacher is writing a letter to his old friend in Malaysia

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => teacher To Be => is Verb-ing => writing Object => a letter Complement => to his old friend in Malaysia

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat menggunakan Present Continuous karena setelah Subjek memiliki pola ToBe + V-ing (is+writing)Kalimat soal adalah Kalimat Aktif, karena Subjek melakukan aksi/tindakan dalam kalimat.Sedangkan kalimat Pasif, Objek yg diberikan aksi/tindakan oleh Subjek dalam kalimat.

Berikut Rumus yg digunakan

Active: S + is/am/are + V-ing + O

=> Teacher is writing a letter to his old friend in malaysia

Passive: O + is/am/are + being + V3 + By S

=> A letter is being written by Teacher to his old friend in Malaysia
