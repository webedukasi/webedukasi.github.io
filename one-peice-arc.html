36. Kuis ⁽¹³⁷⁾ : Jika sudut lancip, arc tg 4/3 = arc sin berapa?


trigonometri

tan a = 4/3

sisi x, y, r → 3, 4, 5

sin a = y/r = 4/5

a lancip

tan a , sin a , positif

arc tan (4/3) = arc sin (4/5)

Fungsi invers trigonometri

arc tan 4/3 = arc sin x

tan x = depan/samping

Triple pythagoras: 3, 4, 5

sin x = depan/miring  →  sudut lancip

arc tan 4/3 = arc sin 4/5
