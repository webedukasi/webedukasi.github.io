4. Can you explain Time Expression with Reported Speech and how do time expressions change in reported speech ?​


Jawaban: maap kalo beda jawaban/salah/salah semua yah>-<"

Penjelasan:

Phrase in direct speech=Equivalent in reported speech

today=that day

"I saw him today", she said.She said that she had seen him that day.

yesterday-the day before

"I saw him yesterday", she said.She said that she had seen him the day before.

The day before yesterday -two days before

"I met her the day before yesterday", he said.He said that he had met her two days before.

Tomorrow-the next/following day

"I'll see you tomorrow", he said  He said that he would see me the next day.

The day after tomorrow-in two days time/ two days later

"We'll come the day after tomorrow", they said.They said that they would come in two days time/ two days later.

Next week/month/year-the following week/month/year

"I have an appointment next week", she said.She said that she had an appointment the following week.

Last week/month/year  the previous/week/month/year

"I was on holiday last week", he told us.He told us that he had been on holiday the previous week.

ago before

"I saw her a week ago," he said.  He said he had seen her a week before.

this (for time)-that

"I'm getting a new car this week", she said.She said she was getting a new car that week.

this/that (adjectives)  the

"Do you like this shirt?" he asked-He asked if I liked the shirt.

here-there

He said, "I live here". -He told me he lived there.

In general, personal pronouns change to the third person singular or plural, except when the speaker reports his own words:

I/me/my/mine, you/your/yours = him/his/her/hers

we/us/our/ours, you/your/yours = they/their/theirs

He said: "I like your new car." = He told her that he liked her new car.

I said: "I'm going to my friend's house." = I said that I was going to my friend's house.

21. How to change 9,6061479 into standard form (scientific notation) ?


Jawaban:

To change a number into standard form or scientific notation, we need to express it as a number between 1 and 10 multiplied by a power of 10.

For the number 9.6061479, we can express it in scientific notation as follows:

9.6061479 = 9.6061479 × 10^0

Since the number is already between 1 and 10, the coefficient remains the same, and the exponent is 0.

Therefore, the standard form or scientific notation of 9.6061479 is 9.6061479 × 10^0.

24. Change the word in the bracket below!If we have time, we ….(go) to your home​


If we have time, we will go to your home.

Penjelasan:

Conditional sentence merupakan kalimat pengandaian atau bersyarat. Conditional sentence terdiri dari if clause dan main clause. Conditional sentence terbagi menjadi 3, yaitu :

↪Conditional sentence type 1 [Future Possible]

digunakan pada masa sekarang dan menunjukkan sesuatu yang masih sangat mungkin terjadi. Rumus :

▶ If Simple present + Simple future.

contoh : If I study hard, I will get high score.

↪Conditional Sentence type 2 [Present Unreal]

digunakan pada masa sekarang dan menunjukkan sesuatu yang tidak mungkin terjadi (berlawanan dengan fakta). Rumus :

▶If simple past + would simple form.

contoh : If I studied hard, I would get high score.

↪Conditional Sentence type 3 [Past Unreal]

digunakan untuk menunjukkan sesuatu dimasa lampau dan tidak terjadi (berlawanan dengan fakta). Rumus :

▶ If past perfect + would have past participle

contoh : If I had studied hard, I would have gotten high score.

==============

Dari pembahasan diatas diketahui bahwa if clause [If we have time] menggunakan simple present sehingga diketahui bahwa ini merupakan conditional sentence type 1. Jadi jawaban yang tepat adalah will go.

▶If we have time, we will go to your home.

25. how does liquid change to gas, explain using particle theory​


Jawaban:

The particles need energy to overcome the attractions between them. As the liquid gets warmer more particles have sufficient energy to escape from the liquid. Eventually even particles in the middle of the liquid form bubbles of gas in the liquid. At this point the liquid is boiling and turning to gas...

32. father puts car in to garage(change in to nwgative present continious)​


Negative Present Continuous:(-) Father is putting car in to garage

PEMBAHASAN

Kalimat “Father puts car in to garage” menjadi negative menggunakan tenses present continious.

Mari kita bahas:

PRESENT CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung saat ini atau sedang terjadi sekarang. Rumusnya:

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing + O + C(-) S + is/am/are + not + V-ing + O + C(?) is/am/are + S + V-ing + O + C?

KESIMPULAN

Father puts car in to garage

=> Father is putting car into garage

Keterangan:Subject => FatherBe => isV-ing => putting Object => carComplement => into garage
