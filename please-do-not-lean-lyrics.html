22. Are lyrics and music equally important to a song or not


Jawaban:

important

Penjelasan:

Jawaban:

Music can stand beautifully on its own without lyrics. There are a great number of songs that have no lyrics and yet proven to bring inexplicably calming vibes to the ears as in those of Mozart. Such songs are believed to be able to help people concentrate and sleep better. Having said that, lyrics are not necessarily less important than music. In fact, lyrics and music complement one another in a song. Moreover, lyrics in a song can be the reason why listeners listen to the same song over and over again. In this case lyrics are something they can relate to; they are more than just a bunch of words.
