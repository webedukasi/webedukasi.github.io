30. choose the correct verb1. I come comes from Padang2.he come comes from Bandung3.they come comes from Surabaya4.mr.dani come comes from Kupang5.she come comes from Medan6.we come comes from Bali7.rina come comes from Madura8.you come comes from Palembang9.mrs.yuda come comes from Yogyakarta10. I come comes from bogor​


Jawaban:

comecomecomescomecomecomescomecomecomecome

Akhiran S hanya digunakan untuk objek jamak, atau lebih dari 1

➊ I come from Padang

➋ He comes from Bandung

➌ They come from Surabaya

➍ Me. Dani comes from Kupang

➎ She comes from Medan

➏ We come from Bali

➐ Rina comes from Madura

➑ You come from Palembang

➒ Mrs. Yuda comes from Yogyakarta

➓ I come from Bogor

Pembahasan

Pada bentu Simple Present Tense, pada kalimat positif jika subjeknya tunggal selain dari (Aku (I) dan Kamu (You)), maka kata kerja ditambah s/es. Contoh:

She studiesHe studiesEndah studiesRangga studies

Sebaliknya, pada bentuk positif jika subjeknya jamak termasuk (Aku (I) dan Kamu (You)), maka kata kerja tidak perlu ditambah s/es. Contoh:

I studyYou studyWe studyThey studyEndah and Rangga study
