2. today i don't feel like doing anything tensenya apa?​


Pakai present tense karena kejadiannya berlangsung "hari ini".

CMIIW ya

Soal:

Today i don't feel like doing anything tensenya apa?​

Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense

Alasannya:

diketahui dari

Adverb of time = Today

Auxiliary = don't

(-) S + do/does + not + VERB 1 + O

= I + don't + feel + like doing anything

Catatan:

Don’t = Do not

=== Semoga Membantu ===

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Present Tense

Kode Mapel: 5
