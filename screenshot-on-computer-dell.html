9. How to create a password on computer


First, turn on your computer.
Then, click on a "Windows" button on keyboard or press the "Start Button" on your desktop.
After that, click on the user profile box and then a window should pop up.
Then, look for "add a password to this user" or something like that, and then put your desired password and press "Apply"

You should have a new password for your computer
