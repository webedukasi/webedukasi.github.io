5. KARAKTRISTIK WILAYAH BUNAKEN


Taman nasional bunaken adalah taman laut yang terletak di Sulawesi Utara, Indonesia. Taman ini terletak di segitiga terumbu karang. Menjadi habitat bagi 390 spesies terumbu karang dan mamalia laut. Taman nasional bunaken merupakan perwakilan ekosistem laut Indonesia, meliputi padang rumput laut, terumbu ng karang dan ekosistem pantai.
Taman nasional ini didirikan pada tahun 1991 dan meliputi wilayah seluas 890.65km2. 97 persen dari taman nasional ini merupakan habitat laut sementara 3 persen sisa nya merupakan daratan, meliputi lima pulau yaitu bunaken, Manado tua, mantehaga, naen dan siladen

8. TOLONG GUNAKEN CARE!​


Jawab:

Luas ≈ 1232 cm²

Keliling ≈ 37,71 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Kalau bentuk ini digabungin, akan membentuk 1/2 lingkaran

d = 56 cm

Luas 1/2 lingkaran = 1/2 × π × d × d × 1/4 =

1/8 × π × d × d

maka luas 1/2 lingkaran ≈

1/8 × (22/7) × 56 × 56 ≈

Luas ≈ 1232 cm²

Keliling = keliling 1/2 lingkaran + 2(keliling lingkaran kecil)

Keliling = π×d/2 + 2(π×d/2)

Keliling = π×d/2 + (π×d)

Keliling = π×d×(1/2+1)

Keliling ≈ (22/7)×56×(1/2+1)

Keliling ≈ (22/7)×8×(3/2)

Keliling ≈ (22/7)×4×3

Keliling ≈ (22/7)×12

Keliling ≈ 37,71 cm

It is part of Manado town, capital city of North Sulawesi. This place can be reach by speed boat or boat charter and need about 30 minutes from harbor Manado. It is surrounding Bunaken island we can find Bunaken National Park. This marine park has a marine biodiversity one of the highest in the world. Bunaken National Park has 20 dive spot with variations in depth. Of the 20 point dive, dive 12 points of which are located around Bunaken Island. Twelve point dive is the most frequently visited by divers and lovers of the beauty of the underwater scenery. Because it has a panoramic underwater great walls. Underwater cliff has many niches, crevices and fissures, hiding various types of marine vertebrates and invertebrates. In addition to hard corals, marine life there, starfish, teriping, etc. Approximately there are about 58 species of coral and more than two thousand species of fish in Bunaken. The types of fish that are common among other wrase, dansel, trigger, sweetlip, unicorn etc. And Ila Gasi, Lulusi yellow tail, Goropa, Oci White and rare animals such as whales, turtles and dugong. Bunaken is a wonderful place for divers foreign tourist and lokal tourist. Because it has a good view of the sea that can make eyes comfortable. Moreover, this place has fress air and far from polution.
