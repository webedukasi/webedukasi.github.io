3. Analyse the ing and find the messeges byond the lyrics dari lagu let it go


Answer:

The song 'Let it go', which is the original soundtrack of the phenomenal animated movie, 'Frozen' has been popular among children, young adult and adult. Here are some -ing words in the lyric. 
- howling (as a verb for present continous)
- swirling (gerund)
- spiraling (as a verb for present continous)
- going (same as above)
The first three Ving is a quite descriptive word. 

The message beyond the song is that you need to embrace who you are along with your potential. Never mind about others say that you are not ideal or perfect as others see, but ideal in your own way. Such can be seen visually to get the meaning actually if you see the movie. This song was played in the full length for the first time at the scene where the older sister has been aware of her power to be the ice queen and start embracing herself as well as controlling it instead of being fear about it.

24. how it is formed snow


Snow is formed when temperatures are low and there is moisture - in the form of tiny ice crystals - in the atmosphere. When these tiny ice crystals collide they stick together in clouds to become snowflakes. If enough ice crystals stick together, they'll become heavy enough to fall to the groundthe formation of the snow begins to gather water vapor in Earth's atmosphere, then the collection of water vapor cools to the point of condensation, the temperature at which the gas turns into a liquid or solid form until then coagulates to form clouds.

At the beginning of the formation of clouds, its mass is much smaller than the mass of air that the clouds float in the air. However, once the vapor collection continues to grow and merge into the clouds, its mass also increases, so that at some point the air can no longer hold it. The cloud burst and water particles fell to Earth.

Particles falling water is pure water that is not contaminated by other particles. Pure water does not freeze at a temperature of 0C directly, because at that temperature changes phase from liquid to solid. To make pure water needed frozen temperatures lower than 0C.

During the formation of snowfall, usually just below the air temperature is below 0C cloud (air temperature depends on the altitude above sea level). However, low temperature alone is not enough to create snow. When particles of pure water is in contact with air, the pure water is contaminated by other particles. There are certain particles that function accelerates the freezing phase, so that the pure water quickly become ice crystals.

Impurity particles involved in this process is called nukleator, which serves as an accelerating phase of freezing and water vapor so that the adhesive between particles of water (which is no longer pure) water particles join together with others to form larger crystals.

Let it be, let it be, let it be, let it be

Whisper word of wisdom

Let it be

(Let It Be, by The Beatles)

The verse above uses assonance (E).

Penjelasan:

A. alliteration : the occurrence of the same letter or sound at the beginning of adjacent or closely connected words.

B. simile :  a figure of speech that compares two different things in an interesting way. A simile uses the words "like" or "as" to draw a comparison.

C. Hyperbole: a figure of speech that uses extreme exaggeration to make a point or show emphasis. It is the opposite of understatement.

D. Metaphor: a metaphor simply states the comparison without using "like" or "as".

E. Assonance: the repetition of vowel sounds in nearby words.
