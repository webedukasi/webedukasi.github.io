7. An excellent basketball player, sarah ….. misses her basketball shots


Jawaban:

rarely or hardly ever

An excellent basketball player, sarah rarely misses her basketball shots

or

An excellent basketball player, sarah hardly ever misses her basketball shots

Penjelasan:

Ini adalah salah satu adverb of frequency yaitu kata keterangan frekuensi (tingkat ke-sering-an)

Kita pakai rarely (jarang) atau hardly ever (hampir tidak pernah) karena walaupun Sarah seorang excellent player, pastilah dia pernah misses the shot, jadi kita tidak pakai never (tidak pernah), karena itu tidak logis.

18. 1. we look after dogs 2. andi and hendra play basketball tolong di buat kalimat continuous tense yaitu kalimat negatif dan positif


positif :
1. We are looking after dogs
2. Andi and hendra are playing basketball
negatif :
1. We are not looking after dogs
2. Andi and hendra are not playing basketball
Untuk past continuous tense, 
1. We were looking after dogs / we were not looking after dogs
2. Andi and Hendra were playing basketball / andi and Hendra were not playing basketball

untuk present continuous tense
1. We are looking after dogs / we are not looking after the dogs
2. Andi and Hendra are playing basketball/ andi and hendra are not playing basketball

26. the shoe store gives special discount in its anniversary. a basketball shoes sold with a 50% discount. the price of the shoes from the factory is 140.000. the store already got 20% profit even if 50% discount was applied. the selling price of the shoes are​


Price from factory = 140.000

% provit = (selling price - buying price)/buying price x 100%
20% = (selling price - 140.000)/140.000 x 100%
20%/100% x 140.000 = selling price - 140.000
2 x 14.000 = selling price - 140.000
28.000 = selling price - 140.000
28.000 + 140.000 = selling price
168.000 = selling price -> after discount

Final price = selling price - discount
168.000 = selling price - (50% x selling price)
168.000 = sp - (50/100 x sp)
168.000 = sp - 1/2 sp
168.000 = 2/2 sp - 1/2 sp
168.000 = 1/2 sp
168.000 x 2/1 = sp
336.000 = sp -> selling price before discount
