1. Why does the singer feel sorry?"Songs for A Dying Planet"Song by: Joe Walsh​


In the song titled "Songs for A Dying Planet", the singer expressed his sorry throughout the lyrics. They (the singer) feel sorry because they realized that they're killing the living things on the planet, therefore leaving them to live on a dying planet (option C).

Pembahasan

"Songs for A Dying Planet" adalah sebuah lagu yang dinyanyikan oleh Joe Walsh. Lagu ini merupakan lagu yang berisi pesan kritik dan kesedihan akan kondisi yang sedang terjadi pada Planet Bumi yang manusia tinggali sekarang ini.

Berikut ini adalah lirik dari lagu tersebut:

Is anyone out there?
Does anybody listen or care anymore?
We are living on a dying planet
We're killing everything that's alive
And anyone who tries to deny it
Wears a tie

And gets paid to lie
So I wrote these songs for a dying planet
I'm sorry but I'm telling the truth
And for everybody trying to save it
These songs are for you, too
Is anyone out there?

Makna yang terkandung dalam lagu tersebut adalah pesan yang membuka mata pendengarnya akan realitas hidup manusia di Planet Bumi saat ini dan mengajak manusia untuk lebih peduli pada lingkungan.

3. The singer of the song above is Joe Walsh. Tell me the biography of Joe Walsh!

(Penyanyi dari lagu di atas adalah Joe Walsh. Ceritakan biografi tentang Joe Walsh!)

Answer: Joe Walsh (Joseph Fidler Walsh) was born on November 20,1947 in Wichita, Kansas. He is a singer, song writer, and an American rock guitarist. He has been a member of three successful rock bands: James Gang, Eagles, and Ringo Starr.

"Hotel California" is his first album with the band Eagles in 1975. In 1998, the guitar solos on the track "Hotel California" by Walsh and Don Felder was selected as the best guitar solos of all time.

In 1992, Walsh released what appeared to be his final album (until 2012), Songs for a Dying Planet, his tenth solo studio album.
