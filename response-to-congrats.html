26. spellingcongratz atau congrats?


Jawaban:

The correct spelling of such word is 'congrats', which is the shortened form of 'congratulation'.

Pembahasan:

The word 'congratulation' is uttered to express your appreciation on someone else's achievement. It also means that you acknowledge other party's success, whether sincerely or not.

In writing or formal situation, 'congratulation' is often used, but in the informal one, it can be changed or shortened into 'congrats'. It is usually uttered among friends, family, relative or close to the one who gain the success.

Among the young people or teenagers, the word 'congratulation' is even shortened and altered a bit into 'congratz' as to sound cool and modern. The replacement of 's' to 'z' is inspired by the pop culture or songs around the world around the 1990s and 2000s. The other examples of it are 'girls' become 'gurlz', the band names in that era 'Boyz 2 Men' and 'Gorrillaz' instead of 'Boys to Men' and 'Gorrillas'.
