are studying

Penjelasan:

If the time adverb = right now

Use present continuous tense (the tense to describe an activity that is currently happening/ongoing)

Present continuous tense:

I + am + verb-ing

He/she/it/(someone/thing) + is + verb-ing

We/you/they(2 people/things) + ARE + verb-ing

You and I = 2 people, we use "ARE" then "speaking" as the verb

= You and I are studying English in the classroom right now.

Pembahasan

Adverb secara bahasa adalah kata keterangan. Adverb ada beberapa bagian:

Adverb of place ➡️ kata keterangan tempat Adverb of time ➡️ kata keterangan waktuAdverb of manner ➡️ kata keterangan caraAdverb of frequency ➡️ kata keterangan kuantitas/jumlahAlways = selalu 100%Usually = biasanya 90%Frequently = berkali-kali 80%Often = sering 70%Sometimes = kadang-kadang 50%Occasionally = kadangkala 30%Seldom = jarang 10%rarely = sangat jarang 5%Never = ga pernah 0%

---

Adverb of frequency biasanya digunakan dalam PRESENT TENSE Karena menyatakan kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang dengan rumus:

(+) S + adv.freq + V1 (-s/es) (-) S + do/does + not + adv.freq + V1(?) Do/does + S + adv.freq + V1?Catatan:

Ada beberapa juga yang biasanya digunakan di awal dan akhir kalimat.
