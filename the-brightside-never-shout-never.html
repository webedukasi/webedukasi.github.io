12. Della never plays computer games. The interrogative form is :A. Is Della never playing computer games ?B. Does Della never play computer games ?C.Does Della never plays computer games​


Della never plays computer games. The interrogative form is

Jawaban:

B. Does Della never play comouter games ?

Penjelasan:

simple present tense adalah kalimat yang menyatakan kejadian langsung atau rutinitas

formula for introgative tenses is

do/does + subject + verb + object + ?

Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense:

B. Does Della never play computer games ?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Interrogative (pertanyaan) dari Simple Present Tense karena menanyakan fakta dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Sebenarnya lebih tepat untuk bentuk pertanyaan menggunakan kata keterangan (Adverb) yaitu "ever" menjadi:

Does Della ever play computer games?

Simple Present Tense:

(?) Do/Does + Subject + Adverb (ever) + Verb-1 + Object + ?
