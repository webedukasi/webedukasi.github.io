I and my friends weren’t close enough. Were you and your friends close enough?


Nominal , Present perfect  :
(+) S + has/have + been +
(-) S + has/have + not + been
(?) have/has + S + been

 
I have been in Production house last month.
She has not been a script writer.
have you been a script writer three years ago?

I have been there but you did not see me.
When I have been a child, I have used to cry.
Have the case been hard to solve?

He has been a teacher.
He has not been a teacher.
Has he been a teacher?

He has been ugly when I met him in the first time.
He has not been ugly when I met him in the first time.
Has he been ugly when you met him in the first time?

I and my friends have been close enough.
I and my friends have not been close enough.
Have you and your friends been close enough?

11. bahasa indonesianya i met boy and girls from different part of this town. i was very happy when i first wore my blue-and-white uniform


SPEAKING

I met boy and girls from different part of this town mean "saya bertemu anak laki-laki dan banyak anak peremphan dari bagian yang berbeda dari kota ini". Then, I was very happy when I first wore my blue and white uniform mean Aku sangat senang pada saat pertama kali mengenakan pakaian biru-putih.

I met boys and girls from different part of this town.

I : saya

Met : bertemu (lampau), verb 1 nya adalah meet

Boys and girls : anak-anak laki-laki dan anak-anak perempuan

From : dari

Different part : berbagai bagian

Of this town : (dari) kota ini

Terjemahan :

Saya bertemu (pada masa lampau) dengan anak-anak laki-laki dan perempuan dari berbagai bagian di kota ini.

============

I was very happy when I first wore my blue-and-white uniform.

I : aku

Was very happy : sangat bahagia (lampau)

When : ketika

First : pertama

Wore : memakai / menggunakan (lampau)

My : possesive adj. dari "I"

Blue-and-white : biru-putih / putih-biru

Uniform : seragam

Terjemahan :

Saya sangat senang ketika memakai seragam biru-putih untuk pertama kalinya.

13. I. Buying a novel when I met Fitri at a bookstore.


Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense:

I was Buying a novel when I met Fitri at a bookstore.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Past Continuous Tense karena menjelaskan dua aktivitas kegiatan di mana suatu kegiatan menginterupsi kegiatan lainnya di masa lampau.

Subjeknya adalah "I" jadi Tobe yang kita gunakan adalah "was"

Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (Was/Were) + Verb-ing + Object + When + Subject + Verb-2 + Object
