Simple present tense

The simple present tense of all verbs except be is based on the simple form. The present tense form of all verbs (except be) is the simple form for all persons except the third person singular.

I seeYou seeHe, she, it seesWe seYou seeThey see

The third singular of almost all verbs is made by adding s or es to the simple form.

(1) The ending es is added in the following cases :

a. To a simple form ending in the letter ch, s, sh, x, or

pass -> passes

push -> pushes

b. To a simple form ending in y, preceded by a consonant (after changing y to i)

study -> studies

try -> tries

c. To do and go

do -> does

go -> goes

(2) The ending s is added to the simple form of all verbs (except have)

see -> sees

buy -> buys

BUT have -> has

Category : Conditional Sentence
Keyword : If

Question :
If you...............................her mother, you would have loved her.

Ini adalah Kalimat Pengandaian Tipe 3 (If + past perfect, S + would have + past participle)

Jadi kata-kata yang tepat sesudah subject 'you' harus berpola past perfect (had + V3)
