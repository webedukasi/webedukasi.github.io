20. Assalamualaikum students..... *I hope you are doing well. Alright, before we begin our lesson today, Let's saya Basmalah... * Last week we have studied about CAUSE AND EFFECT, And today we are going to continue our material about CAUSE AND EFFECT Do you still remember, what is the meaning of CAUSE? * Sebab Akibat And what is the meaning of EFFECT? * Sebab Akibat ​


Jawaban:

1. Cause

Pada noun, berarti seseorang atau sesuatu yang menyebabkan sesuatu terjadi. Pada verb berarti tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi.

In English :

In nouns, 'cause' means someone or something that causes something to happen. In verbs, 'cause' means the action that makes something happen.

2. Effect

Pada noun, berarti perubahan yang merupakan akibat dari suatu tindakan atau sebab. Sedangkan dalam verb, effect ditulis menjadi affect yang berarti menghasil sebuah efek atau menyebabkan perubahan pada sesuatu.

In English :

In nouns, 'effect' means changes that are the result of an action or cause. Meanwhile in the verb, 'effect' is written as affect which means to produce an effect or to cause a change in something.
