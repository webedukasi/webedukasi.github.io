2. What is algae in the ocean provided much for Earth?​


Jawaban:

oxygen.

Penjelasan:

Photosynthesizing algae in the ocean produce around 70% of oxygen in the atmosphere.

Jawaban:

Algae in the ocean provides a lot of benefits for the Earth. Here are some ways in which algae contributes to the planet:

Oxygen production: Algae is responsible for about 50% of the world's oxygen production. Through photosynthesis, algae produces oxygen that is essential for the survival of many organisms on Earth, including humans.

Carbon sequestration: Algae helps to absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and store it in the ocean. This is important for mitigating climate change as carbon dioxide is a greenhouse gas that contributes to global warming.

Food source: Algae is a food source for many marine organisms, including fish, shellfish, and whales. It is also used as a food source for humans, especially in Asian cuisine.

Economic benefits: Algae has many commercial uses, including as a source of biofuels, pharmaceuticals, and nutraceuticals. The global market for algae-based products is expected to grow significantly in the coming years.

Biodiversity: Algae is a key component of marine ecosystems and contributes to the overall biodiversity of the planet. Many species of algae have not yet been discovered, and their potential benefits to humans and the environment are still being explored.

Overall, algae in the ocean plays a vital role in maintaining the health and sustainability of the planet.
