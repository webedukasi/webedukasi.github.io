31. tolong jawab Osama caranya


Pecahan kontinual:
[tex]$\begin{align}3\frac{4}{9}&=a+\frac{1}{b+\frac{1}{c}} \\ &=a+\frac{1}{\frac{bc+1}{c}} \\ &=a+\frac{c}{bc+1}\end{align}[/tex]
Maka,
a = 3
c = 4
Dan,
bc + 1 = 9
b(4) = 9 - 1
4b = 8
b = 2

Sehingga,
a + b + c = 3 + 2 + 4 = 9
