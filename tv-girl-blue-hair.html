29. I like the girl, her hair is black and long Bagaiamna Kalimat adjective clausen


Jawaban:

I like the girl whose hair is black and long.

Jawaban:

Her hair is black and long

Penjelasan:

Adjective clause atau biasa disebut anak kalimat merupakan sebuah kalimat yang berfungsi sebagai kata sifat dan menjelaskan suatu noun atau pronoun. posisi adjective clause selalu mengikuti noun (kata benda) atau pronoun (kata ganti) yg ingin dijelaskan. macam macam adjective clause :

Who = person

example = He is the man who works in my store every sunday

which =animal, object

example = my cats which very naughty and noisy but so cute and royal

when = time

example = at 09.00 am when i bought a book in book store, she call me about her aunty

whose = possesive (kepunyaan)

example = the doctor whose car is red, will meet me on friday
