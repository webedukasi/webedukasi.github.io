Ungkapan giving instruction biasanya pakai imperative sentence atau kalimat perintah yang bernada positif. Contoh:

Peel the carrots and cut into cube.

(Kupas wortel-wortelnya dan potong kotak-kotak.)

Wash the spinach then boil for 2 minutes.

(Cuci bayam lalu rebus selama 2 menit.)

Dalam percakapan, kita bisa menambahkan kata 'please' supaya bisa sopan.

Supaya berurutan, kita bisa pakai kata-kata berikut:

First, ...Second, ...Third, ...Then...Next...After that...Finally, ...

Contoh lengkap giving instruction:

How to cook instant noodles

1. First, boil 2 glasses of water in a pot.

2. Second, add noodles, breaking up if desired.

3. Then, cook for 3 minutes or until noodles are tender, stirring occasionally.

4. After that, remove from heat and stir in seasoning from flavor.

5. Finally, enjoy your noodle.

17. how to open a bank account teks procedure ​


Jawaban:

Opening a bank account typically involves a series of steps and requirements. Here's a general procedure to open a bank account:

1. Research and choose a bank: Start by researching different banks in your area or online. Consider factors like the bank's reputation, services offered, fees, and convenience.

2. Gather necessary documents: Banks require certain documents to open an account. Generally, you'll need the following:

- Proof of identity: Valid government-issued identification such as a passport, driver's license, or national ID card.

- Proof of address: Documents like utility bills, rental agreements, or bank statements that confirm your residential address.

- Social Security Number (SSN) or Tax Identification Number (TIN).

- Employment details: Information about your employer, job title, and income source.

3. Visit the bank: Once you've chosen a bank, visit the nearest branch in person. Schedule an appointment if required, especially during busy times.

4. Speak to a bank representative: Inform the bank representative that you want to open a bank account. They will guide you through the process and provide you with the necessary forms to fill out.

5. Fill out the application form: Complete the application form provided by the bank. The form will require personal details such as your name, address, date of birth, occupation, and contact information.

6. Submit the required documents: Present your identification documents, proof of address, and any other required documents to the bank representative. They will verify the information and make copies for their records.

7. Choose an account type: Banks offer various types of accounts, such as savings accounts, current accounts, or specialized accounts. Discuss your requirements and select the type of account that best suits your needs.

8. Deposit initial funds: Some banks require an initial deposit to activate the account. Determine the minimum deposit amount and provide the funds to the bank representative. They will credit it to your new account.

9. Review account terms and sign agreements: Carefully read any account agreements, terms, and conditions provided by the bank. Seek clarification if you have any questions. Once satisfied, sign the necessary documents to finalize the account opening process.

10. Receive account details: After completing the paperwork, the bank will provide you with your account details, including the account number, debit card, and any other relevant information.

11. Set up online banking (optional): If the bank offers online banking services, inquire about the procedure to set up online access. This will allow you to manage your account online and perform various banking transactions.

Personal data can only be shared if there is a clear legal basis to do so or if the data subject has given their clear consent.

Personal information can be shared without consent if it is justified in the public interest or required by law. Do not delay disclosing information to obtain consent if that might put children or young people at risk of significant harm.
