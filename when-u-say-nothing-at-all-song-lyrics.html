17. what are the elements of a song lyrics​


Jawaban:

The elements of the song lyrics are divided into two, namely:

(1) The elements of form that can be referred to as physical structures, include among others:

- diction

- figuratively

- Pengimajian

- concrete words

- rhythm

- as well as typography.

(2) The elements of content or inner structure consist of:

- theme

- feelings

- tone

- and mandate.

Penjelasan:

terjemahannya :

Unsur lirik lagu dibagi menjadi dua, yakni:

(1) Unsur bentuk yang dapat disebut sebagai struktur fisik, unsur tersebut antara lain:

- diksi

- kiasan

- pengimajian

- kata konkret

- ritme

- serta tipografi.

(2) Unsur isi atau struktur batin terdiri atas:

- tema

- perasaan

- nada

- serta amanat.
