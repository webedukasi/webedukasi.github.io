9. They hung up their coatstolong cari prosal verb nya​


Jawaban:

"They hung up their coats"

Phrasal verb: Hung up

Pembahasan:

Kata kerja phrasal sangat umum dalam bahasa Inggris, terutama dalam konteks yang lebih informal. Mereka terdiri dari kata kerja dan partikel atau, kadang-kadang, dua partikel. Partikel sering mengubah arti kata kerja.

Contoh:

I called Jen to see how she was. (call = to telephone)They've called off the meeting. (call off = to cancel)

Dalam hal urutan kata, ada dua jenis utama kata kerja phrasal: dapat dipisahkan dan tidak dapat dipisahkan.

Dapat dipisahkan

Dengan kata kerja phrasal yang dapat dipisahkan, kata kerja dan partikel dapat terpisah atau bersama-sama.

They've called the meeting off.

OR (Atau)

They've called off the meeting.

Namun, kata kerja phrasal yang dapat dipisahkan harus dipisahkan ketika Anda menggunakan kata ganti orang.

The meeting? They've called it off.

Berikut adalah beberapa kata kerja phrasal umum yang dapat dipisahkan:

I didn't want to bring the situation up at the meeting.

(bring up = start talking about a particular subject)

Please can you fill this form in?

(fill in = write information in a form or document)

I'll pick you up from the station at 8 p.m.

(pick up = collect someone in a car or other vehicle to take them somewhere)

Tidak dapat dipisahkan

Beberapa phrasal verbs tidak dapat dipisahkan.

Who looks after the baby when you're at work?

Bahkan ketika ada kata ganti orang, kata kerja dan partikel tetap bersama.

Who looks after her when you're at work?

Berikut adalah beberapa kata kerja phrasal umum yang tidak dapat dipisahkan:

I came across your email when I was clearing my inbox.

(come across = to find something by chance)

The caterpillar turned into a beautiful butterfly.

(turn into = become)

It was quite a major operation. It took months to get over it and feel normal again.

(get over = recover from something)

13. 3/4 of the mass pack A is equal to 2/5 of the mass of pack B. Pack A is 7/8kg lighter than pack B. Find the total mass of both packs, express your answer in its simplest form


Jawab:

3 1/7 kg

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

3/4 of the mass pack A is equal to 2/5 of the mass of pack B :

[tex]\frac{3}{4}A=\frac{2}{5}B\\\\A=\frac{2}{5}B.\frac{4}{3}\\\\A=\frac{8}{15}B~~~~~~...(i)[/tex]

Pack A is 7/8kg lighter than pack B :

[tex]A=B-\frac{7}{8}\\\\\frac{8}{15}B=B-\frac{7}{8}\\\\B-\frac{8}{15}B=\frac{7}{8}\\\\\frac{7}{15}B=\frac{7}{8}\\\\B=\frac{7}{8}.\frac{15}{7}\\\\B=\frac{15}{8}kg\\\\\\A=\frac{8}{15}B\\\\A=\frac{8}{15}.\frac{15}{8}\\\\A=1kg\\\\\\so~total~mass~of~both~packs=1+\frac{15}{7}=\frac{22}{7}=3\frac{1}{7}kg[/tex]
