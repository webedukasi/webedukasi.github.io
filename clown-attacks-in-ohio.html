7. 1. Aktif : The man attacks herPassive:​


Jawaban:

|| ACTIVE VOICE → PASSIVE VOICE ||

Active : The man attacks her.

Passive : She is attacked by the man.

Penjelasan:

|| KALIMAT AKTIF DAN PASIF SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE ||

Active : S + verb-1 + O

Passive : S + to be (is/am/are) + verb-3 + by + O

Contoh :

Active : She writes a letter.Passive : A letter is written by her.Active : He speaks English.Passive : English is spoken by him.Active : Miss Lena teaches English.Passive : English is taught by Miss Lena.

The clown, however, continued to weep and wail even after the guards had left.
''Stop it! said the irritated caliph.
