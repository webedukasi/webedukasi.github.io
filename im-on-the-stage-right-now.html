Jawaban:

1. (+) I am riding a bicycle right now.

(-) I am not riding a bicycle right now.

(?) Am I riding a bicycle right now?

2. (+) I am drinking coffee now.

(-) I am not drinking coffee now.

(?) Am I drinking coffee now?

3. (+) Andika is playing the mobile legends game right now.

(-) Andika is not playing the mobile legends game right now.

(?) Is Andika playing the mobile legends right now?

4. (+) My Dad is washing the car right now.

(-) My Dad is not washing the car right now.

(?) Is My Dad washing the car right now?

5. (+) My Mom is cooking cakes in the kitchen right now.

(-) My Mom is not cooking cakes in the kitchen right now.

(?) Is My Mom cooking cakes in the kitchen right now?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Present Continuous Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan yang sedang dilakukan sekarang.

Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)

(-) Subejct + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + Subject + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)?

Terkadang Adverb of Time juga disebut sebagai Time Signal.

Penjelasan:

Dalam bahasa inggris kata "ing" memiliki arti sedang dikerjakan. Maka dancing artinya sedang menari. Jadi arti dari kalimat di atas adalah:

Dina is dancing on the stage now Dina menari di atas panggung sekarang

Kenapa kata yang dipilih dancing? Karena pada kalimat tersebut terdapat kata "now" yang artinya sekarang, Jadi, jika melakukannya sekarang maka yang digunakan adalah kata yang diakhiri "ing" yaitu dancing.

39. the driver stops the car near the school ? - my father have me the money last morning. ? - Miss Maria dances on the stage now ? -​


ྀ༘ Present, Past, Continuous ྀ༘

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Nomor ❶

(+) The driver stops the car near the school.(–) The driver doesn't stop the car near the school. (?) Does the driver stop the car near the school?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

Nomor ❷

(+) My father gave me the money last morning.(–) My father didn't give me the money last morning. (?) Did y father give me the money last morning?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

Nomor ❸

(+) Miss Maria is dancing on the stage now. (–) Miss Maria isn't dancing on the stage now. (?) Is Miss Maria dancing on the stage now?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

PEMBAHASAN

Nomor ❶ merujuk pada penggunaan Simple Present Tense yang memiliki pola kalimat sebagai berikut:

—♛ Pattern:

⚜ Active Voice:

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O + (C) (–) S + do/does + not + V1 + O + (C) (?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O + (C) ?

⚜ Passive Voice:

(+) S + is/am/are + V3 + by agent + (C)(–) S + is/am/are + V3 + by agent + (C)(?) Is/am/are + S + V3 + by agent + (C) + ?

—♛ Keterangan:

⚜ Active Voice:

Pada bentuk positif, jika subjeknya nama makhluk/benda tunggal (he, she, it, Anh, Dys, the cat, or the plate) maka kata kerja harus ditambah "s/es". Contoh: (1) Ahn studies English everyday. (2) He eats apple twice a week. "Do" digunakan untuk subjek: I, you, we, they dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates). "Does" digunakan untuk subjek: He, she, it dan nama makhluk/benda tunggal (Anh, Dys, the cat, or the plate)

⚜ Passive Voice:

"Am" digunakan untuk subjek: I (saya). "Is" digunakan untuk subjek: He, she, it dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates)."Are" digunakan untuk subjek: You, we, they dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates).

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

Nomor ❷ merujuk pada penggunaan Simple Past Tense yang memiliki pola kalimat sebagai berikut:

—♛ Pattern:

⚜ Active Voice:

(+) S + V2 + O + C(–) S + did + not + V1 + O + C(?) Did + S + V1 + O + C ?

⚜ Passive Voice:

(+) S + was/were + V3 + by agent + C(–) S + was/were + V3 + by agent + C(?) Was/were + S + V3 + by agent + C + ?

—♛ Keterangan:

"Was" digunakan untuk subjek: I, he, she, it dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates)."Were" digunakan untuk subjek: You, we, they dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates).

[Note]

Sepertinya ada typo di bagian verb/kata kerjanya, seharusnya "gave" bukan "have".

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

Nomor ❸ merujuk pada penggunaan Simple Present Continuous Tense yang memiliki pola kalimat sebagai berikut:

—♛ Pattern:

⚜ Active Voice:

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing + O + C(–) S + is/am/are + not + V-ing + O + C(?) Is/am/are + S + V-ing + O + C ?

⚜ Passive Voice:

(+) S + is/am/are + being + V3 + by agent + C(–) S + is/am/are + being + V3 + by agent + C(?) Is/am/are + S + being + V3 + by agent + C + ?

—♛ Keterangan:

"Am" digunakan untuk subjek: I (saya). "Is" digunakan untuk subjek: He, she, it dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates)."Are" digunakan untuk subjek: You, we, they dan nama makhluk/benda banyak (Anh and Dys, the cats, or the plates).

[Note]

Dari keterangan waktu yang digunakan pada soal (now/sekarang), biasanya bentuk waktunya merujuk pada penggunaan Simple Present Continuous Tense bukan Simple Present Tense.
