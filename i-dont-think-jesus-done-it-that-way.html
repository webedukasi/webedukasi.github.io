14. im working on my projetct paper it is due tomorrow i dont think i will be able to finish it


I understand that it can be stressful to have a project paper due tomorrow and feeling like you might not be able to finish it on time. Here are some tips to help you manage your time and make progress on your project:

1. Stay Calm: Take a deep breath and try to stay calm. Panicking will not help you complete the paper faster. Stay focused and positive.

2. Prioritize: Identify the most important sections or tasks that need to be completed. Prioritize those and focus on getting them done first.

3. Break it Down: Divide the remaining work into smaller tasks. This will make it feel more manageable and less overwhelming.

4. Set Goals: Set specific goals for what you want to achieve in certain time frames. For example, aim to finish a certain section or complete a certain number of pages by a certain time.

5. Eliminate Distractions: Minimize distractions as much as possible. Find a quiet and comfortable place to work where you can stay focused.

6. Take Short Breaks: Take short breaks every now and then to give your mind some rest. But make sure to get back to work after the break.

7. Seek Help: If you encounter challenges or have questions, don't hesitate to seek help from your teacher, classmates, or friends.

8. Don't Aim for Perfection: Remember that your project paper doesn't have to be perfect. Focus on getting the main points across and meeting the requirements.

9. Proofread Later: If you're running out of time, focus on completing the content first. You can do the proofreading and editing later if necessary.

10. Stay Positive: Believe in yourself and your ability to finish the paper on time. A positive attitude can make a big difference.

Remember that doing your best is what matters most. Even if you can't finish everything, completing a significant portion of the paper is still an accomplishment. Good luck with your project paper!

Identify the expressions below!

Identifikasi ungkapan di bawah ini!

Pembahasan

1. Asking and Giving Opinion

➡ Asking opinion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menanyakan pendapat. Berikut ungkapannya:

what do you think about...?what do you feel/think about....?do you think that.....?do you feel that...?what's your opinion about that...?how do you feel about...?don't you think that...

➡ Giving Opinion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan pendapat.

in my opinion...i think that...i believe that....if you ask me,i think that...i'm sure that...personally,i believe...I really think that...

2. Expression of Agreement

➡ Asking Agreement adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menanyakan persetujuan.

do you agree that....?how about you...

➡ Agreement adalah ungkapan yang digunakan memberikan persetujuan.

I agree with you...i think you're right...i feel the same way...that' what i think...absolutely,i agree...that's a good point'...

➡ Disagreement adalah ungkapan yang digunakan memberikan ketidaksetujuan.

sorry, I don't think that's truee...i don't agree with you...i don't feel the same way...yes,but...i see it differently.
