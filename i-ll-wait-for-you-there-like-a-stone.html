3. He read a book full of death and contemplated the idea of dying alone. He also mentioned praying to god and angels on his deathbed and hoping to be taken to heaven.

4. He seemed to be reflecting on his past actions and regretting some of them, "And on I read until the day was gone, and I sat in regret of all the things I've done, for all that I've blessed and all that I've wronged."

5. It is not specified what he plans for living, as the focus of the lyrics is more on contemplation of death and waiting.

39. arti dari I hope you get better soon and I can't wait for your comics ???​


Jawaban:

Saya berharap kamu segera membaik dan saya tidak sabar untuk komik-komik kamu.

Penjelasan:

Kosakata

I = saya

hope = harap

you = kamu

get = mendapat

better = lebih baik

soon = segera

and = dan

can't = tidak bisa

wait = menunggu

for = untuk

your = (kepunyaan) kamu

comic = komik

Catatan:

can't wait = tidak bisa menunggu.

tapi di kalimat ini artinya tidak sabar

semoga membantu :)

EXPRESSION HOPE

Kalimat dengan jenis seperti ini memiliki fungsi, yaitu untuk menyampaikan harapan atau keinginan dari sang pembicara. Contoh kalimat dari expression hope, yaitu:

-Good luck

-I wish you win

-I hope she doesn't mad

Arti dari kalimat "I hope you get better soon and I can't wait for your comics", yaitu "Aku harap kamu cepat baikan/cepat sembuh dan aku tidak bisa menunggu/tidak bisa sabar untuk komikmu".
