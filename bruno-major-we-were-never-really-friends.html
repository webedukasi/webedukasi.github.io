20. Apakah kalimat negative dan introgative dari they were never late


Jawaban:

Rumus:

Positive (+): s+v2

Negative (-): s+did not/didn't+v1

Introgative (?): did+s+v1+?

Penjelasan dengan langkah langkah:

(+): they were never late

(-): they were didn't never late

(?): did they were never late?
