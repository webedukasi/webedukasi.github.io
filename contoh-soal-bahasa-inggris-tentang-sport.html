2. soal menjodohkan bahasa inggris tentang recount text about sport events


Last year, my school and some schools collaborated with health department to held sports events. It is held close to the Guesan ulun museum.

when the day come i can see crowds with many students from other schools from afar, so to not get apart we had to group together.

My group and I looked around
for a bit and then parted in half, I grouped together with my friends to the registration booth, to join the game. I choose too join the rope skipping game and hula hoop game because the other games is full of participants so that’s the only option left.

After we finished the registration we group back with our whole group in the hall. we get education about the importance of exercise regularly, and
And what kind of food is good for your body.

After we finished we went to the game places, some people in the group just watch from afar and some of them go inside the arena to participate in the games,and I’m included.
When it’s my turn to play the hula hoop I lost within 3 seconds. It’s a bit embarrassing but i already knew it will eventually happen, so I wear a mask.but it turned out different with the rope skipping game. I win!
The reason why i join 2 games is simple by joining a game you already gained point but if you win a game you get more points! And you can exchange it for tote bags,keychain,and plushy! I exchanged my point for a keychain with a mini plushies on it it’s so cute.

after a day full of activity i return home by bus.
it was great, I have seen many students from different schools, learning something new and winning a game in the same time, it might be a bit embarrassing for the hula hoop game but it will always be a precious memory that I’ll always cherish.

Maaf kalau ada yg salah

24. Buatkan cerita tentang waktu luang(leisure time) yang terjadi di masa lampau dalam bahasa inggris(berkaitan dengan play sport)


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: experience and narrative
Pembahasan:

Summer in 1994 was probably a regular holiday for other people, but not for me. It was the year when I knew football for the first time. Back then, we had not cellphone or computers yet. Everything was so much different than now. So, the most fun thing I could do was to play football almost every afternoon before maghrib after watching the world cup matches in the morning previously. Me and my friends at school were playing against each other in two teams or against kids from the other neighbourhood. That could end up in a strike if one team felt being treated unfair. We used to pretend that we were football stars in those years like Romario, Roberto Baggio, Bebeto, Hristo Stoichkov, Tomas Brolin or Paolo Maldini and Gabriel Batistuta. The latter was my favourite. If we got bored with football, we would just go cycling around, finding shortcuts or going on an uncharted streets in the morning at the weekends. We usually got back in the afternoon. Things were more so physical and technology-unattached in the past, which was good as we could experience something real.
