38. Men calico cat is rare, have chromosome genitype XBXbY. The cromosome composition in men calico cats caused by


Jawaban Bahasa Indonesia:
Kucing kaliko (kucing belang tiga) hanya terjadi jika kucing punya dua kromosom X dan alel O serta o (X⁰X°), sehingga memang banyak terjadi di kucing betina. Namun, adapula kucing jantan yang memiliki warna belang tiga. Kucing jantan ini juga memiliki 2 kromosom X dan 1 kromosom Y, sehingga rumus kromosomnya adalah X⁰X°Y. Artinya terjadi kelainan peristiwa gagal berpisah kromosom Y saat pembelahan meiosis, atau sering disebut juga sindrom klinefelter.

Keterangan:
(⁰ adalah O besar, ° adalah o kecil)

English answer:
Calico cat (cat that have three different colors), happens if the cat has two X chromosomes, allele O and allele o (X⁰X°), this event frequently happens in female cat. But, it can also happen in male cat. Male cat that have three different colors also has 2 X chromosomes and 1 Y chromosome, so the formula of the chromosomes will be X⁰X°Y. This incident called nondisjuction because one of homologue chromosomes fail to separate during meiosis division. The male cat disorder often called by klinefelter syndrome.

Notes:
(⁰ represents big O, ° represents small o)
