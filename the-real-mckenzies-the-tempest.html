13. The height of the shadow is 5 cm and the real height of the object is 2 cm. The magnification of the object is


Jawaban:

M = 2,5 x

Penjelasan:

hi = 5 cm

ho = 2 cm

M = hi/ho = 5/2 = 2,5

Because the magnification of shadow is 2,5. So, the shadow is 2,5 times bigger than the object's height.

Explanation :

Magnification (M) is the ability of an optical device to be able to change its actual size at its shadow. If the magnification of the shadow more than 1 ( M > 1), the size of shadow will bigger than the actual size. If the magnification is 1 (M = 1), the shadow will show actual size of the original. If the magnification is under 1 (M < 1), the shadow will smaller than the actual size. For calculate the magnification of the shadow, we can use this formula :

[tex] \boxed {\bold{|M| = |\frac{h'}{h}|}} \:\:\: or \:\:\:\boxed {\bold{|M| = |\frac{s'}{s}|}} [/tex]

If :

|M| = magnification => always positive h' = shadow height (cm or m) h = actual height (cm or m) s = distance the actual object to optical device (cm or m) s' = distance the shadow to optical device (cm or m) Work Steps :

We know that :

h' = shadow's height = 5 cm h = object's height = 2 cm

Question : |M| = magnification = ... ?

Answer :

[tex] |M| = |\frac{h'}{h}|[/tex]

[tex] |M| = |\frac{5}{2}| = \boxed {2,5}[/tex]

23. Type l : if he us a bird,he may fly awayType ll :The real fact :Type lll :The real fact :​


^_hai, brainly fellows!_^

Let me try to help you change into a right conditional type!

Jawaban:

Apabila diketahui:

Type 1:

If he is a bird, he may fly.

Maka dapat dijawab:

Type 2:

If he was a bird, he might fly.

In fact:

He may not fly because he wasn't a bird.

Type 3:

If he had been a bird, he might fly.

In fact:

He may never fly because he wasn't a bird.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas semua berupa dalam bentuk conditional.

Kalimat kondisional terbagi dalam 3 bentuk:

Conditional Type 1

Merupakan kalimat dengan bentuk sesuatu apa yang terjadi pada saat ini apabila subyek berupa subyek kedua (obyek) benar-benar dapat terjadi atau apabila subyek mengharapkan sesuatu dapat terjadi/dimiliki olehnya maka sesuatu dijamin akan subyek rasakan. Selain itu, sesuatu yang diibaratkan dapat ada pada masa kini (present)

Penggunaan:

If + S + simple present + O + will/can/may + V1 (be) + ROS

ROS = rest of sentence

Contoh lainnya:

If I have much money, I will buy a car.She will not give you a gift if you are naughty.

Conditional Type 2

Merupakan kalimat yang mencirikan sesuatu yang tidak sesuai dengan kenyataan pada saat ini melainkan fakta yang diharapkan dapat benar-benar terjadi dengan yang dapat dihubungkan dengan dunia nyata dalam kondisi masa lampau (past) atau masa lampau yang bakal terjadi pada masa depan (past future)

Penggunaan:

If + S + simple past + O + would/could/might + V1 (be) + ROS

Contoh lainnya:

If I had much money, I would buy a new house in Jakarta.If you weren't good she wouldn't give you a gift.

Conditional Type 3

Merupakan kalimat dengan bentuk harapan (expectation) yang pada dasarnya sudah terjadi/terdapat sebelumnya. Dengan suatu harapan yang diinginkan benar-benar terjadi pada masa lampau namun tidak dapat dirasakan atau tidak ada keberadaannya karena tidak sesuai atau benar-benar tidak terjadi (hanya sebuah harapan) dengan kejadian masa lampau yang mutlak (past perfect)

Penggunaan:

if + S + past perfect + O + would/could/might + have (been) V3 + ROS

Contoh lainnya:

If I had graduated at the right time, I would have travelled Paris.You would have given she a letter if she had been sick.

Catatan:

ROS (rest of sentence) adalah sisa dari kalimat yang digunakan dapat berupa object dan complement

30. How we see reflections of objects pencil is reflected on the surface of a metal spoon.How is the reflection of the pencil similar to the real pencil? How is the reflection of the pencil different to the real pencil?​


Jawaban:

When we see reflections of objects, we are actually seeing the light that bounces off the surface of an object and is reflected back to our eyes. In the case of a pencil reflected on the surface of a metal spoon, the light that bounces off the pencil travels to the surface of the spoon and reflects back to our eyes.

The reflection of the pencil in the metal spoon appears similar to the real pencil in terms of its shape and size. The reflection shows a mirrored image of the pencil, which means that if the pencil is facing right in real life, it will also appear to be facing right in the reflection.

However, there are some differences between the reflection of the pencil and the real pencil. The reflection of the pencil is reversed or inverted, which means that the right side of the pencil appears on the left side of the reflection and vice versa. Additionally, the reflection of the pencil is two-dimensional and lacks depth, unlike the real pencil which is three-dimensional and has depth.

40. scale of the map is 1:4,000,000 if the distance between 3 cm then the real distance is?...​


Jawaban:

120 km

Penyelesaian

Diketahui:

Map scale: 1:4.000.000

Map distance: 3 cm

Ditanya:

Real distance: ???

Dijawab:

Real distance: Map scale × Map distance

Real distance: 4.000.000 × 3

Real distance: 12.000.000 cm

Real distance: 120 km

Semoga Membantu

Maap kalo salah

Jawaban:

Jawaban

B. Inggris : Scale of the map is 1 : 4.000.000. If the distance between 3 cm then the real distance is 120 km.

B. Indonesia : Skala peta adalah 1 : 4.000.000. Jika jarak antara 3 cm maka jarak sebenarnya adalah 120 km.

Pembahasan

SK = 1 : 4.000.000

JP = 3 cm

JS = JP/SK

JS = 3/1/4.000.000

JS = 3 × 4.000.000

JS = 12.000.000 cm

JS = 120 km

Jadi, jarak sebenarnya adalah 120 km
