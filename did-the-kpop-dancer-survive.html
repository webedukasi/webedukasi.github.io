Over the years, humans have developed various technologies and strategies for survival, such as farming, raising animals, and building construction. Communities have also formed social and economic structures that assist in managing resources and the division of labor to meet their needs.

In addition, human intelligence and cultural development also played an important role in survival. Humans use their knowledge and creativity to overcome challenges, solve problems, and adapt to changing environments.

Overall, early survival involves humans' ability to seek out and satisfy their basic needs using available knowledge, creativity, and resources.

22. The costume that dancer use in Sirih Kuning performance is.."​


Even though the name has an element of "yellow" color, the costumes for this dance can vary, ranging from red, green, and yellow. Even now, there are many variations of costumes for this dance.

The dancers will use flowers as a headdress which is a symbol of a woman's happiness, prosperity and maturity. If you pay attention, the elements of Chinese culture are so thick

Dancers also use scarves as a tool to invite the audience to dance together. Dancers also use Betawi cloth with horn or zigzig motifs as their trademark or wear pants.

Arti:

Walaupun namanya memiliki unsur warna “kuning”, tetapi kostum tarian ini bisa beragam, mulai dari merah, hijau, dan juga kuning. Sekarang pun sudah banyak variasi kostum untuk tarian ini.

Para penari akan menggunakan bunga sebagai hiasan kepala yang merupakan lambang kebahagiaan, kemakmuran, dan kedewasaan seorang perempuan. Jika diperhatikan, begitu kental unsur budaya Tionghoa dalam kostum tarian ini, terutama dari hiasan kepala. Kadang juga menggunakan cadar yang menjadi simbol menutupi dan melindungi wajah cantik.

Penari juga menggunakan selendang sebagai alat untuk mengajak penonton menari bersama. Penari juga menggunakan kain betawi dengan motif tanduk atau zig-zig sebagai ciri khasnya atau menggunakan celana yang warnanya senada dengan baju.
