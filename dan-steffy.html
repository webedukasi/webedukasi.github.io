Jawaban:

1. (+) Nadya had left the room before the meeting was over.

(-) Nadya hadn't left the room before the meeting was over.

(?) Had Nadya left the room before the meeting was over?

2. (+) We had arrived at home when the rain fell down.

(-) We hadn't arrived at home when the rain fell down.

(?) Had we arrived at home when the rain fell down?

3. (+) Pedro had finished his report by the end of the deadline.

(-) Pedro hadn't finished his report by the end of the deadline.

(?) Had Pedro finished his report by the end of the deadline?

4. (+) You had decided your next move when Mr. Rahmat called

(-) You hadn't decided your next move when Mr. Rahmat called.

(?) Had you decided your next move when Mr. Rahmat called?

5. (+) Antonio had given your money back before you asked.

(-) Antonio hadn't given your money back before you asked.

(?) Had Antonio given your money back before you asked?

6. (+) After the employees had accomplished their job, they left the factory.

(-) After the employees hadn't accomplished their job, they left the factory.

(?) Had after the employees had accomplished their job, they left the factory?

7. (+) I and my friends had gotten the salary when Mrs. Hela asked me for the rent.

(-) I and my friends hadn't gotten the salary when Mrs. Hela asked me for the rent.

(?) Had I and my friends gotten the salary when Mrs. Hela asked me for the rent.?  

8. (+) Erika had gone to toilet before you arrived.

(-) Erika hadn't gone to toilet before you arrived.

(?) Had Erika gone to toilet before you arrived?

9. (+) Rocky had fixed my motorcycle by the time I got to the shop.

(-) Rocky hadn't fixed my motorcycle by the time I got to the shop.

(?) Had Rocky fixed my motorcycle by the time I got to the shop.?  

10. (+) Winnie had washed the sleeping bag when Steffy asked for it.

(-) Winnie hadn't washed the sleeping bag when Steffy asked for it.

(?) Had Winnie washed the sleeping bag when Steffy asked for it?

Penjelasan:

Past perfect tense:

(+) Subject + had + past participle verb (verb 3) + object.

(-) Subject + had + not + past participle verb (verb 3) + object.

(?) Had + subject + past participle verb (verb 3) + object?
