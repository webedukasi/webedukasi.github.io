1. Analyse the ing and find the messeges byond the lyrics dari lagu let it go


Answer:

The song 'Let it go', which is the original soundtrack of the phenomenal animated movie, 'Frozen' has been popular among children, young adult and adult. Here are some -ing words in the lyric. 
- howling (as a verb for present continous)
- swirling (gerund)
- spiraling (as a verb for present continous)
- going (same as above)
The first three Ving is a quite descriptive word. 

The message beyond the song is that you need to embrace who you are along with your potential. Never mind about others say that you are not ideal or perfect as others see, but ideal in your own way. Such can be seen visually to get the meaning actually if you see the movie. This song was played in the full length for the first time at the scene where the older sister has been aware of her power to be the ice queen and start embracing herself as well as controlling it instead of being fear about it.

12. Kapan let us dan let me digunakan


let us digunakan saat memberi perintah ke suatu sekelompok orang termasuk pengajak.ex : let us go
let me digunakan untuk untuk menginstruksi satu orang

Penggunaan kata let dalam imperatives – let merupakan kata kerja (verb) dan juga termasuk kata kerja causatives (masih ingatkah kalian readers apa itu kata kerja causatives?). Ternyata readers, kata let dapat digunakan untuk memperkenalkan saran dan perintah, ketika tidak ditujukan ke pendengar atau pembaca.

Adapun struktur dalam kalimat dengan kata let untuk bentuk imperative adalah:

Let + object + main verb

– Untuk bentuk formal = Let us; bentuk informal = let’s; untuk membuat saran atau memberi perintah ke suatu sekelompok orang termasuk pengajak.

Contoh:

a. Let us go. (ayo pergi)Let’s have a meal. (ayo cari makan)Well, let’s all keep moving forward. (baik, ayo tetap maju)

– Masih ingat materi question tags? Ternyata khusus bentuk let’s menggunakan question tag ‘shall we?”, berikut contohnya:

Let’s have a meal, shall we? Yes, let’s (ayo kita cari makan, yak an? ayo)Let’s go for a walk, shall we? Yes, let’s (ayo kita jalan jalan, yak an? ayo)

– Perlu readers ketahui, ada dua bentuk kalimat negative dengan kata let ini, yakni berupa let us not (let’s not) dan do not let us (don’t let’s).

33. Prabu: "Ratu, are you going to let me go without you?" It means ... A. Prabu asked whether Ratu is going to let him go without her me B. Prabu asked Ratu to let him go without me C. Prabu told Ratu that she was going to let him go without her D. Prabu asked Ratu if she was going to let him go without her E. Prabu asked if she was going to let Ratu go without him


Prabu: "Ratu, are you going to let me go without you?"

It means Prabu asked Ratu if she was going to let him go without her (D).

Pembahasan

Soal di atas adalah mengubah kalimat langsung (direct sentence) menjadi kalimat tidak langsung (indirect sentence).

Pada kalimat langsung yang menggunakan interrogative question (yes/no question), menggunakan if atau whether. Dua-duanya dapat digunakan.

Yang perlu diperhatikan adalah pada indirect sentence menggunakan bentuk lampau. Pada soal mengunakn present continuous tense, sehingga indirect speech-nya adalah past continuous tense (was/were + going to).
