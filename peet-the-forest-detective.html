9. Jelaskan bentuk tenses dari kalimat di bawah ini! ❀ The case has been solved by the detective.​


Jawaban:

Kalimat tersebut adalah Kalimat Present Perfect Tense dalam bentuk Passive Voice.

Penjelasan:

Jika ditulis kedalam rumus menjadi (Passive Voice):

Object + has/have + been + V3 + by + Subject.

The case has been solved by the detective.

Jika dirubah menjadi Active Voice menjadi:

The detective has been solved the case.

Jawaban:

﻿Passive Voice:

﻿The sentence in question is in the form of Present Perfect Tense (Passive Voice).

Penjelasan:

﻿Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Passive Voice dari Present Perfect Tense dalam Bahasa Inggris.

﻿

Bentuk kalimat aktif dan pasif nya dalam bentuk Present Perfect Tense:

Active Voice:  (+) The detective has solved the case.

﻿Passive Voice: (+) The case has been solved by the detective.

﻿

﻿Present Perfect Tense:

Active Voice:  (+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

﻿Passive Voice: (+) Subject + Have/Has + been + Verb-3 + Object

15. job for the detective is?


The word detective is taken from English "Detective" which means a person, especially a police officer, whose job is to investigate and solve criminal cases.

In a large Indonesian dictionary, the word detective refers to a secret police or so-called detective. Police investigating cases or searching for confidential information. No wonder they are often called secret police.

In his deeper definition a detective is an investigator, who mostly works as a law enforcement officer. In some developing countries some detectives are also working alone without an agency ties to the legal establishment. They are usually referred to as private detectives or private investigators / private eye.

In his unformal language, especially in fiction, the detective is a person whether he has a license or without a license to investigate a criminal case. In some police departments, a detective is a rank or position obtained by a police officer who passes a written test. But in some other police systems, detectives are college graduates who join without the need to enroll in police schools like other police officers. There are other arguments that the detective work in the police department is different and gets different training, qualifications, qualities, and abilities with other uniformed police officers.

In the opinion of one who has long been in the world of investigation, the definition of a detective is a job of solving a case or problem that has not been revealed, using systematic and planned methods, basing on the existing evidence, and assemble it into a whole fact, and can be accounted for .Detective is a person, especially a police officer, whose occupation is to investigate and solve crimes.

33. HOTS: how is the process of forest restoration after forest fire occurs? Write the answer in here and research about primary succession of the forest after merapi eruption :D


Jawaban:

Secondary succession is one of the two types ecological succession of a plants life. As opposed to the first, primary succession, secondary succession is a process started by an event (e.g. forest fire, harvesting, hurricane, etc.) that reduces an already established ecosystem (e.g. a forest or a wheat field) to a smaller population of species, and as such secondary succession occurs on preexisting soil whereas primary succession usually occurs in a place lacking soil. Many factors can affect secondary succession, such as trophic interaction, initial composition, and competition-colonization trade-offs.[1] The factors that control the increase in abundance of a species during succession may be determined mainly by seed production and dispersal, micro climate; landscape structure (habitat patch size and distance to outside seed sources);[1] bulk density, pH, and soil texture (sand and clay).[2]

An example of Secondary Succession by stages:

1. An area of growth.

2. A disturbance, such as a fire, starts.

3. The fire destroys the vegetation.

4. The fire leaves behind empty, but not destroyed soil.

5. Grasses and other herbaceous plants grow back first.

6. Small bushes and trees begin to colonize the public area.

7. Fast-growing evergreen trees and bamboo trees develop to their fullest, while shade-tolerant trees develop in the understory.

8. The short-lived and shade-intolerant evergreen trees die as the larger deciduous trees overtop them. The ecosystem is now back to a similar state to where it began.
