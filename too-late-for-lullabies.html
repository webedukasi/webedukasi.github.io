5. make the taq form each of the follow ing sentences A. he was rever late for school B.the snow wasn't falling too heavily


Tag question adalah pertanyaan singkat yang terdapat pada akhir kalimat. Dalam Bahasa Indonesia, tag question berarti kan?/ya?/yuk?/bukan?

Ketentuan dalam penulisan tag question antara lain:

Jika kalimatnya positif, maka tag questionnya adalah negatif

Contoh:

Wulan is smart, isn't she?

Andika likes anime, doesn't he?

You are an English teacher, aren't you?


Jika kalimatnya negatif, maka tag questionnya akan positif.

Contoh:

Wulan is not smart, is she?

Andika doesn't like anime, does he?

You are not an English teacher, are you?


Jika kalimatnya berupa larangan atau perintah, maka tag questionnya adalah 'will you?'

Contoh:

Close the door, will you?

Don't disturb me, will you?

Don't be noisy, will you?


Jika makna kalimatnya adalah negatif (mengantung kata-kata seperti no one, none, seldom, rarely, nothing, barely, dan lain-lain), maka question tagnya akan positif.

Contoh:

No one come to school, do they?

She never seems to care, does she?


Jika subjectnya adalah everything, something, nothing, maka question tag akan menggunakan subject it.

Contoh:

Something is moving, isn't it?

Everyhing will be okay, won't it?


Pembahasan

make the tag question from each of the following sentences


A. he was never late for school = He was never late for school, was he?


B.the snow wasn't falling too heavily = The snow wasn't falling too heavily, was it?

26. mesya was late coming to school too days ago ( + ) ( - ) ( ? )​


Jawaban:

mesya was late coming to school too days ago ( + )

Penjelasan:

Because  this sentence didn't have negative/interrogative sentence

Jawaban:

Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Mesya was late coming to school two days ago.

(-) Mesya was not late coming to school two days ago.

(?) Was Mesya late coming to school two days ago?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Past Continuous Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kata "late" dalam kalimat berfungsi sebagai Adverb (kata keterangan) yang memodifikasi Verb (kata kerja) "coming" dan posisi yang benar dalam kalimat: Mesya was coming late to school two days ago.

Jawaban di atas hanya mengikuti apa yang dihadirkan pada soal.

Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-ing + Adverb (late) + Object + Time Signal

(-) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + not + Verb-ing + Adverb (late) + Object + Time Signal

(?) Tobe (was/were) + Subject + Verb-ing + Adverb (late) + Object + Time Signal + ?
