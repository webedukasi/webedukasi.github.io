Are you talking about one chair or many chairs? That (obviously) determines whether you choose the plural or not. PEMBAHASAN Present Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung saat ini atau sedang terjadi sekarang.

Waktu Present Continuous => Now => Right now ✔️=> At the moment => today

Rumus Present Continuous

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + V-ing

(-) S+ Be (is/am/are) + not + V-ing

(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + V-ing?
