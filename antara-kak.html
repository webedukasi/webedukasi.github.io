[tex]x_1+x_2=-\frac{b}{a}[/tex], maka

[tex]\begin{aligned}{x_{1}+x_{2}}&={-\frac{b}{a}}\\{\,}&={-\frac{-13}{3}}\\{\,}&={\frac{13}{3}} \end{aligned}[/tex]
