How to make:

-First, make chicken noodle broth soup first, by boiling the boiled water again with chicken meat added with pepper, salt, and chicken bones to taste.

-Both, make chicken topping.

Boiled chicken is then chopped into small pieces or finely chopped.

Blend the seasoning consisting of garlic, onion, coriander, turmeric, candlenut, and ginger.

Saute the chicken with mashed spices.

Add salt and flavoring to taste.

After the chicken noodle soup and chicken topping are ready, boil the prepared noodles.

When boiling the noodles, prepare a bowl.

Then add 1 tablespoon of chicken oil and 1 teaspoon of pepper.

Put the cooked noodles in the bowl.

Then stir the noodles until they are completely mixed with chicken oil.

Add chicken topping and chicken soup.

Chicken noodles are ready to be served.

-mie basah atau mie

5. perbedaan volp dengan megaco​


Jawaban:

Secara singkat, VoIP adalah layanan komunikasi suaranya, sedangkan Megaco adalah protokol pendukung agar VoIP bisa di-routing antara jaringan IP dan jaringan telepon. Keduanya saling melengkapi dalam implementasi VoIP.

Penjelasan:

Perbedaan utama antara VoIP dan Megaco:

VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) adalah teknologi yang memungkinkan percakapan suara jarak jauh melalui jaringan internet, bukan melalui jaringan telepon konvensional.Megaco (H.248) adalah protokol yang mengatur komunikasi antara Media Gateway Controller (MGC) dan Media Gateway (MG) dalam jaringan VoIP. Megaco berfungsi sebagai protokol signaling dan control untuk memfasilitasi interkoneksi antara jaringan telepon PSTN dan jaringan berbasis IP.VoIP merupakan teknologi/layanan komunikasi suara itu sendiri, sedangkan Megaco hanyalah salah satu protokol pendukung agar VoIP dapat berjalan. VoIP bisa menggunakan protokol lain seperti SIP, H.323, dll.VoIP mentransmisikan sinyal suara ke dalam paket data melalui jaringan IP. Megaco berfungsi mengontrol gateway agar dapat merutekan paket data VoIP tersebut dari jaringan IP ke jaringan telepon dan sebaliknya.
VoIP melibatkan perangkat endpoint seperti IP Phone, ATA, Softphone. Megaco melibatkan perangkat jaringan seperti Media Gateway dan Media Gateway Controller.VoIP menyediakan layanan komunikasi suara/panggilan. Megaco menyediakan interkoneksi antar jaringan dan kontrol sinyal.

30. Cara membuat resep mie ayam pake bahasa inggris


⚫Ingredients

½ a bunch of fresh coriander

3 cloves of garlic

5 cm piece of ginger

1 bunch of spring onions

1 fresh red chilli

1 lime

2 carrots

1 head of broccoli

2 skinless higher-welfare chicken breasts

250 g medium free-range egg noodles

50 g unsalted cashew nuts

vegetable oil

freshly ground black pepper

1-2 tablespoons low-salt soy sauce

1 tablespoon fish sauce

⚫Method

1.Pick the coriander leaves and finely slice the stalks, then keep aside for later. Peel and finely slice the garlic, then peel and matchstick the ginger.

2. Trim and finely slice the spring onions, deseed and finely slice the chilli and cut the lime into wedges. Trim, peel and thinly slice the carrots at an angle.

3.Cut the broccoli into small florets, then finely shred the stalk, discarding any gnarly bits. Cut the chicken into 1cm strips.

4.Cook the noodles according to packet instructions, then drain and refresh under cold water. Drain again, toss in a little oil and put to one side. Lightly toast the cashew nuts in a non-stick frying pan until golden, then tip onto a plate to cool.

5.Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large frying pan or wok. Season the chicken with a pinch of pepper, then add to the pan and stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes, or until golden. Add the coriander stalks, garlic and ginger and cook for a further minute.

6.Add the spring onions, carrots and broccoli and stir-fry for a further 2 minutes, then add the cooked noodles. Keep stir-frying until the noodles are warm and the chicken is cooked through. Stir through the soy and fish sauces, then remove from the heat.

7.Divide between bowls, sprinkle over the nuts, sliced chilli and the reserved coriander leaves, then serve with the lime wedges for squeezing over.

Some common forms of procedural texts are.  

Directions - (menanyakan tujuan/arah menuju suatu tempat) How do I get somewhere? Very specific instructions including location names and titles. Instructions (petunjuk untuk melakukan sesuatu) - How do I do something? Your language must meet the needs of your audience and you may need to include a diagram if there are complex elements to complete.  Recipes (petunjuk untuk memasak) - How do I cook something? There is a very clear expectation of the audience so never stray from the essentials. Ingredients, method and a few visuals are essential.  Rules for games (perintah sebuah permainan) - How do I play this? Be conscious of your audience and write in a style and language they will understand. You are almost guaranteed to require visuals in this style of writing.  Manuals (petunjuk penggunaan barang) - How do I operate this? Are there any warnings I need to be aware of before proceeding? Be very specific in your explanation.  Agendas - What are we doing? When are we doing it? Who is responsible?
