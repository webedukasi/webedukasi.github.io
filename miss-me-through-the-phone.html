20. Change the following sentences into present perfect continuous tense. 1. We make the delicious food. 2. Yadi repair the broken machine. 3. Miss Bella call me by phone. 4. They buy the new car.5. You give me the happiness. ​


Present Perfect Continuous/Progressive Verb Tense

In this verb tense, an action starts in the past but it’s still continuing now. You have been performing the action and still are performing the action in the present.

1. We have been making the delicious food.

2. Yadi has been repairing the broken machine.

3. Miss Bella has been calling me by phone.

4. They have been buying the new car.

5. You have been giving me happiness.
