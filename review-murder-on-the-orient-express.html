20. The school had the students ... their lessons before the exam. A. To review B. Reviewing C. Review D. Reviewed E. They review


CAUSATIVE MOOD

The school had the students review their lessons before the exam.

-------------

EXPLANATION

Causative mood adalah salah satu modus dalam bahasa Inggris.

Pada kalimat yang menggunakan causative mood, subjek kalimat bukanlah pelaku aksi melainkan yang menyebabkan orang lain melakukan aksi yang dimaksud.

Kata kerja yang biasa digunakan dalam causative mood antara lain:

letmakehaveget

Kalimat active causative mempunyai konstruksi:

S + let/make/have + person + infinitive (v₁)

atau

S + get + person + to infinitive

Contoh:

I let my mother cut my hair.

I had the barber cut my hair.

I got the barber to cut my hair.

--------------------

EXPLICATION

Soal merupakan kalimat dalam active causative mood dan causative verb yang digunakan adalah have, maka kata kerja non-finite yang kita gunakan dalam bentuk infinitive (v₁).

Jadi, opsi yang tepat adalah C. review.

There are only a few musical numbers in The Jungle Book, all of which are rearranged from the original: Kaa's transfixing "Trust in Me" (which really doesn't show up until the credits), Baloo's "Bare Necessities," and "I Wanna Be Like You," which Walken sings as the ambitious Gigantopithecus (an extinct giant orangutan) King Louie. That character's portrayal was problematic in the original Disney film, prompting criticism for being racist, and it's wonderful that director Jon Favreau's interpretation of the story isn't culturally insensitive (just scary -- Louie is huge!).
