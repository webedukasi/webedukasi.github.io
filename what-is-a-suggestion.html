1. What is a suggestion?


A suggestion is a notion you give or say to be considered by other people. Suggestion may be given with or without being asked. If someone does not ask for any suggestion, we can ask politely whether we can suggest something to that person or not.

Pembahasan

Suggestion menurut Oxford Learner's Dictionaries adalah sebuah ide atau rencana yang disebutkan kepada orang lain agar orang tesebut memikirkannya. Dalam bahasa Indonesia disebut sebagai saran dan memiliki definisi yang serupa di dalam Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia. Berikut adalah frasa/kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk memberi dan meminta saran dalam bahasa Inggris:

Memberi saran (giving for suggestions)Can I make a suggestion?I would like to offer a suggestion.I suggest you to...I think we should...You need to...Meminta saran (asking for suggestions)Do you have any suggestions?We welcome any suggestions.I'd like to hear your suggestions.Any suggestions?What should I do?
