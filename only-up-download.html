38. Wisnu:what was the result of the pencak silat competition? jenny:I was only runner up. wisnu: you were runner up, but you have displayed your best. well done!​


Congratulating expression is an expression used to congratulate someone for his achievement/success.

Wisnu: What was the result of the pencak silat competition?

Apa hasil dari kompetisi pencak silat?

Jenny: I was only runner up.

Aku hanya menjadi juara kedua

Wisnu: You were runner up, but you have displayed your best. Well done!​

Kamu menjadi juara kedua, tetapi kamu sudah menampilkan hal terbaikmu. Kerja bagus!

Pembahasan

Dari dialog diatas, Well done!​ merupakan contoh ungkapan congratulating expressions.

Contoh ungkapan congratulating expressions lainnya:

Best Wishes!

Congratulations on your graduation!  

Congratulations on your promotion!

That was excellent. Congratulations!

Well done!

Good Job!

I'd like to congratulate you on....

Respon yang diberikan:

Thank you very much.

Thank you

Oh, thanks

Thank you for saying so.

Thanks. I'm glad you think so.

It's very kind of you to say so. Thank you.​
