1. contoh 10 soal beserta jawabannya dalam bahasa inggris tentang analytical exposition


Analytical Exposition adalah teks yang berisi tentang pendapat-pendapat penulis terhadap suatu fenomena atau kejadian tertentu.

Pembahasan

Generic Structure of Analytical Exposition

Thesis: Berisi tentang ulasan singkat tentang topik yang akan dibahas oleh penulis.Arguments: Berisi pendapat-pendapat penulis yang mendukung topik yang sudah disebutkan pada bagian Thesis. Biasanya dalam satu teks Analytical Exposition, terdapat minimal dua arguments.Reiteration/Conclusion: Berisi tentang penegasan kembali tentang pandangan atau pendapat penulis. Bagian ini juga berisi tentang kesimpulan penulis dari argumen-argumen yang dijabarkan di atas.

Language Features dari Analytical Exposition:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan kata hubung seperti besides, in addition, on the other hand, vice versa, as a result, consequently, dan lain-lain

Contoh 10 soal tentang Analytical Exposition:

Cars should be banned in the city

Cars should be banned in the city. As we all know, cars create pollution, and cause a lot of road deaths and other accidents.

Firstly, cars, as we all know, contribute to most of the pollution in the world. Cars emit a deadly gas that causes illnesses such as bronchitis, lung cancer, and ‘triggers’ of asthma. Some of these illnesses are so bad that people can die from them.

Secondly, the city is very busy. Pedestrians wander everywhere and cars commonly hit pedestrians in the city, which causes them to die. Cars today are our roads biggest killers.

Thirdly, cars are very noisy. If you live in the city, you may find it hard to sleep at night, or concentrate on your homework, and especially talk to someone.

In conclusion, cars should be banned from the city for the reasons listed.

Argumentation is one type of paragraph development in writing written for the purpose of convincing or persuading the reader. In the argumentation of the content can be an explanation, proof, reason, and an objective review which includes examples, analogies, and causes.
The goal is for the reader to believe that the idea, idea, or opinion is true and proven.

Well, still want to try smoke? Think the facts before trying! If you are not smoker, you should never and never try to smoke.

13. Buatlah contoh Hortatory exposition tentang covid 19,dengan bahasa sendiri​


Recommendation

Therefore, we need to continue to protect ourselves from exposure to the virus for the good of ourselves and others. To overcome fatigue in the midst of a pandemic, we can try to keep in touch with the people closest to us so that we do not feel bored. We should remember that everyone can be exposed to viruses and vaccines still cannot be used, so it is important to always implement health protocols, especially when there is an urgent matter that requires us to leave the house.

Dari teks di atas, kita bisa melihat bahwa penulis bermaksud memaparkan topik atau opininya tentang penerapan protokol kesehatan yang masih harus dilakukan karena pandemi belum berakhir.

Kemudian di bagian arguments, kita bisa melihat bahwa penulis memaparkan bahwa orang-orang sekarang kebanyakan tidak mematuhi protokol kesehatan karena lelah dengan pandemi yang tak kunjung berakhir. Namun penulis mencoba mempengaruhi pembaca bahwa protokol kesehatan harusnya tetap diterapkan untuk menghindarkan diri dari infeksi virus Covid-19.

Di bagian recommendation, penulis menuliskan bahwa kita harus tetap menerapkan protokol kesehatan dengan saling terhubung dengan orang terdekat dan mengingat bahwa semua bisa terinfeksi, maka kita harus tetap menerapkan protokol kesehatan terutama ketika pergi keluar.

They keep the economy in order and look after like defense.  Similarly, the state governments look after the middle sized things.
