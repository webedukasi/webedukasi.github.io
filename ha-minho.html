[tex]{\boxed{ \tt P \: = \: \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}}}[/tex]

= 70 × 7(10) ÷ 7(10) + 7(10) - 7(10)

= 70 × 70 ÷ 70 + 7(10) - 7(10)

= 70 × 70 ÷ 70 + (7 × 10) - 7(10)

= 70 × 70 ÷ 70 + 70 - 7(10)

= 70 × 70 ÷ 70 + 70 - (7 × 10)

= (70 × 70) ÷ 70 + 70 - 70

= 4.900/70 + 70 - 70

= (70 + 70) - 70

= 140 - 70

= 70
