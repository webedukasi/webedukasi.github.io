Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Cause and Effect

If we look at the sentence, we see Cause and Effect relationship. The first clause is the Effect and the second clause is the Cause.

The Formula: [Effect] because/since/as [Cause].

We can also inverse the statement into: Since it rained hard all night, the road in front of my house is flooded.
