23. use "such.....that"the food was spicy foodit Made my tongue like in fireanswer:......​


SUCH .....THAT

The food was SUCH spicy food (THAT) it made my tongue like in fire.

[Makanannya pedas banget sampai bikin lidah sy terbakar]

Explanation

Such...that adalah correlative conjunction yang digunakan untuk mengekpresikan sebab-akibat (cause & effect), melebihkan kualitas dari benda yg dijabarkan sehingga memberikan dampak yg tidak seperti biasanya.

Rumus

.....SUCH+Adjective+Noun+THAT+Clause

~ [Such+an/a+Adj+Singular Noun..]

~ [Such+Adj+Plural noun....]

~ [Such+Adj+Uncountable noun....]

Contoh :

It's a very hot weather. I drink much water several times.

~ Dari kalimat di atas, terlihat jelas untuk klausa yg menunjukan penyebab/Cause adalah it's a very hot weather.

~ Dan klausa yg menunjukkan akibat/effect adalah I drink much water several times.

~ Maka, jika digabung menggunakan "Such...that" menjadi :

It's SUCH a hot weather (THAT) I drink much water several times [It means, because it's a very hot weather, I drink much water several times]

Note :

~ Dalam kalimat yg menunjukkan hubungan sebab-akibat menggunakan "such...that", adverb "very" dihilangkan karena "very" digunakan untuk menguatkan kualitas kata sifat.

~ Modifier "such" diposisikan sebelum kata benda atau frasa nomina. Jika terdapat articles "an/a", modifier "such" diletakkan sebelum articles tersebut.
