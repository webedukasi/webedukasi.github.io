19. there are some expressions when making suggestions and offers write it down jawab yang benar ya kk ​


Jawaban:

Expression of Giving Suggestion

You should go home now, it's getting late.It is better to tell the truth about what had happened.I suggest you to solve the problem before leaving for a holiday.Why don't you visit your parents this coming holiday?

Penjelasan:

Pembahasan

Giving suggestion expressions adalah ekpresi yang digunakan untuk menyampaikan saran kepada seseorang. Untuk memberikan saran biasanya menggunakan should/had better/ought to/how about atau why don't. Beberapa phrase yang sering digunakan untuk memberikan saran antara lain:

Why don't you visit your grandmother?You should tell her the truth.You had better go home early, your kids are waiting for youHow about going to the cinema this weekend?It is better to fix the car now before it's getting worst

Beberapa phrase yang sering digunakan untuk asking suggestion expressions adalah:

Do you have any suggestions about this matter?What do you suggest?Any suggestions?What do you think about...?What is your comments about this?

Responses

Ada dua cara untuk merespons apabila seseorang memberikan suggestion, yaitu menerima/accepting atau menolak/rejecting.

Phrase yang bisa digunakan untuk menerima suggestion:

That's a good idea.That's great!Thanks, that's awesomeAwesome idea!

Phrase yang bisa digunakan untuk menolak suggestions:

I'm not sure about that.I don't think it's a good idea.Thank you but...

Sorry if I was wrong
