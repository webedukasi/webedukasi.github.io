1. buatkan contoh soal pg dan essay dari such as


Jawaban:

Such as adalah frasa yang digunakan untuk memberikan contoh atau ilustrasi dari sesuatu yang disebutkan sebelumnya. Contohnya:

- I like fruits such as apples, oranges and bananas.

- She has many skills such as writing, painting and singing.

- D) Menyatakan tujuan atau niat

- Jawaban: C

3. contoh dialog such as (didalam dialog ada kata such as)


Explanation:

Such as is used to give an example or more of something that we mention before. It can be placed in the middle of a sentence or at the end of a sentence.
For example,
- I like to eat Japanese food, such as sushi, ramen, udon, and mochi (at the end of a sentence)
- Fruits such as orange and strawberry are rich in vitamin c (in the middle of a sentence)
Please note that, if such as is placed in the middle of a sentence, we will not put a comma before and after it, but if it is placed at the end of a sentence, we will put a comma before it. This is necessary for writing. Such as can be replaced by "Like"

Answer:
Here's an example dialogue using such as.

A: Hey, B!
B: Hello, A! What's up?
A: I want to ask something.
B: What is it?
A: What fruit that is rich in vitamin C?

A: Why?
B: I ever read a journal about it. According to the scientist, there are many other fruits such as guava and strawberry which are richer in vitamin C than yellow fruits. Vegetables such as broccoli are also rich in vitamin C.
A: Oh, I see. Thank you, B.
B: Don't mention it.

12. apa perbedaan 'such as' dan 'for example'? dan berikan contohnya!


Jawaban:

The difference between 'such as' and 'for example', which actually has the same meaning, lies on the position in the sentence. Here is the example:

Several notable football managers begin to lose the opportunity to lead big teams. For example, Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez can only be in the non elite teams this season.Several notable football managers such as Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez begin to lose the opportunity to lead big teams.

Penjelasan:

The phrase 'for example' and 'such as' have the same meaning, that is to state the examples related to the content in the sentence. Yet, they are quite different in terms of their position in a sentence.

'For example' can be used in the middle of the sentence as well as at the beginning of it. Moreover, it has to be between two commas when it is put in the middle of the sentence. Here is how: The students have less interest in social science, for example, history, geography, sociology. The students have less interest in social science. For example, they tend to be lazy in learning history or geography.

'Such as' can only be used in the middle of the sentence but not at the beginning. Yet, this phrase does not need two commas in between. Here is how:                                      

Several notable football manager such as Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez begin to lose the opportunity to lead elite teams

17. tuliskan 10 contoh such as​


He likes playing sports such as tennis, football and swimming.

(Dia suka berolah raga seperti tenis, sepakbola dan berenang)

We should eat a lot fruits such as banana, apple, and papaya.

(Kita harus banyak makan buah seperti pisang, apel dan pepaya)

Such

Kegunaan such adalah sebagai berikut:

1) Dalam situasi formal, such + noun berarti “tersebut” dan diletakkan sebelum a / an. Namun apabila kata bendanya adalah “Uncountable Nouns”, maka artikel “a/an” tidak digunakan.

I don’t like such an idea.

(Saya tidak suka ide itu)

Such honesty is really needed.

(Kejujuran seperti itu sangat dibutuhkan)

2) Such digunakan sebelum Adjective + Noun.

I do like such beautiful girl.

(Saya sangat menyukai perempuan yang cantik seperti itu)

I agree with such brilliant idea.

(Saya setuju dengan ide brilian itu)

3) Dalam situasi tidak resmi, such dapat juga digunakan untuk memberikan informasi baru, ketika pembicara ingin menekankan apa yang dikatakannya.

He’s such an idiot!

(Dia memang bodoh)

She has such a marvelous voice!

(Suaranya memang luar biasa)

34. buat contoh kalimat for example and for instance :isi dimana letak komabuat contoh such as and like:isi dimana letak koma


Jawaban:

1. For example adalah kata yang sering digunakan untuk menujukkan contoh, namun ada beberapa hal yang perlu diperhatikan yaitu:

A. Jika for example terletak di awal kalimat, harus diikuti dengan koma:

There are many activities in the morning. For example, the student goes to school.

B. Jika for example terletak di tengah kalimat, umumnya antara subjek dan predikat :

Social security, for example, is the responsibility of our government.

2. For instance memiliki fungsi yang sama dengan for example. Namun, umumnya for instance lebih sering digunakan di tengah kalimat.

In the economic sector, for instance, Indonesia is still better than Thailand.

3. e.g. juga dapat digunakan untuk memberikan contoh. E.g. sendiri adalah kependekan dari exempli gratia yang merupakan berasal dari bahasa Latin.

High level smartphones, (e.g. Samsung and Iphone) are the leader of the world market

A. Jika tidak berada dalam kurung kita bisa menggunakan koma sebelum e.g.

Today people, e.g. children, play with smartphone.

Orang-orang sering salah dalam penggunaan i.e. yang sering disamakan dengan e.g. Perbedaannya adalah i.e itu sama dengan that is yang umumnya digunakan untuk mendefinisikan seuatu, sedangkan e.g. digunakan untuk memberikan contoh/ sepadan dengan e.g.

Penjelasan:

Ketika kita diharuskan untuk menulis jurnal internasional, surat lamaran pekerjaan dalam Bahasa Inggris, ataupun Surat Resmi dalam Bahasa Inggris, kita harus mempunyai kemampuan menulis yang bagus. Gaya menulis dalam bahasa Inggris memiliki ciri khas sendiri. Salah satunya adalah ketika kita ingin memberikan sebuah contoh dalam tulisan tersebut. Memberikan contoh di dalam bahasa Inggris dapat menggunakan for example, for instance, e.g., such as, like, dan sebagainya.

Chandler: "There are many countries I would like to visit".

Monica: "Such as?" / "Like?" / "For example?" / "For instance?"

37. Soal pilihan ganda tentang for example dan such as


Explanation:

For example can be used both within a sentence and at the start of a sentence.

Example:

- I like to eat sweet food, for example, jelly, ice cream, and chocolate bar. (within a sentence)

- I learnt some new things in the camp yesterday. For example, I made a fire with sticks and tie various knots. (at the start of a sentence)

Please note that in written, For example always has a comma after it. Moreover, If it is used within a sentence, it will be followed by noun or noun phrase and if it is used at the start of a sentence, it will be followed by a full sentence.

 

Such as can be used within a sentence and in the middle of a sentence.

Example:

- I can speak a few foreign languages, such as English, Japanese, and Chinese. (within a sentence)

- Sportswear brands such as nike and adidas manufacture their products in many different countries around the world. (in the middle of a sentence)

Please note that in written, such as does not have a comma after it. Moreover, such as in the middle of a sentence does not have comma before and after it.

40. Contoh dialog such as


Explanation:

Such as is used to give an example or more of something that we mention before. It can be placed in the middle of a sentence or at the end of a sentence.

For example,

- I like to eat Japanese food, such as sushi, ramen, udon, and mochi (at the end of a sentence)

- Fruits such as orange and strawberry are rich in vitamin c (in the middle of a sentence)

Please note that, if such as is placed in the middle of a sentence, we will not put a comma before and after it, but if it is placed at the end of a sentence, we will put a comma before it. This is necessary for writing. Such as can be replaced by "Like"
